Welcome to [Weekly Thing](https://weekly.thingelstad.com/).

Someone you know has brought you into the friends-and-family circle around the newsletter by gifting you a Supporting Membership. Here's what that means.

Weekly Thing is a free newsletter I've written every weekend since May 2017 — links and notes from things I've read, watched, or built, with a short reason for sharing each one. The person who gifted you a subscription has made a contribution to this year's chosen nonprofit on your behalf. The newsletter itself is the same one anyone can subscribe to, and it's free for everyone.

Being part of the friends-and-family circle means that if I ever add anything supporter-only down the road — early previews, an exclusive community, that sort of thing — you'll be in it.

Your first issue will land in your inbox this weekend. The [archive](https://weekly.thingelstad.com/archive/) goes back to May 2017 if you'd like to poke around in the meantime.

Glad to have you along.
