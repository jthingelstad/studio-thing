Thank you for becoming a Supporting Member of [Weekly Thing](https://weekly.thingelstad.com/). It means a lot.

A note on what this is and isn't. Your contribution goes 100% to the nonprofit I'm supporting this year — you aren't paying for the newsletter. Weekly Thing is free for everyone and always will be. You're using the membership as a way to support that nonprofit, and I'm grateful you chose to do it through here.

Becoming a Supporting Member also brings you into the friends-and-family circle around the newsletter. The issues you'll receive are the same ones everyone else gets. If down the road I add anything supporter-only — early previews, an exclusive community, that sort of thing — you'll be in it.

You can [read more about the program](https://weekly.thingelstad.com/members/), including which nonprofit is being supported this year. You can also change your subscription or payment method [here]({{ manage_subscription_url }}) at any time.
