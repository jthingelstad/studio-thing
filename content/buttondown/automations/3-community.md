One more thing worth knowing about: **[r/WeeklyThing](https://www.reddit.com/r/weeklything/)** on Reddit.

It's a small community — readers chatting about issues, sharing related links, occasionally going off on threads of their own. Low-traffic, no algorithm pushing things at you. Each issue gets its own tag in the subreddit, so you can drop into the discussion just for that week if you'd like.

If an issue ever leaves you with thoughts you want to share, that's a good place to send them. (Hitting reply to the email works too — both reach me.)

In a few days, I'll send the last note in this welcome series. That one's about the Supporting Member program, which is how readers can give back to a nonprofit through Weekly Thing.
