A heads-up: every issue of Weekly Thing also goes out as a podcast.

Same content as the email, narrated, delivered alongside the weekend issue. If listening fits your week better than reading — or you'd like both — here's where to find it:

- [Apple Podcasts](https://podcasts.apple.com/us/podcast/weekly-thing/id1895865769)
- [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/show/43A9fytZDKaZhrkp3qbukh)
- [Overcast](https://overcast.fm/itunes1895865769/weekly-thing)

The podcast feed is at [weekly.thingelstad.com/podcast.xml](https://weekly.thingelstad.com/podcast.xml) if your app of choice isn't in that list. There's no separate sign-up — subscribing in your podcast app of choice is all it takes.

In a few days, I'll send the next short note in this welcome series. That one's about Thingy, an AI agent that knows the entire Weekly Thing archive and can dig things up for you on demand.
