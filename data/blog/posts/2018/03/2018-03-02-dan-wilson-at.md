[Dan Wilson](https://danwilsonmusic.com)’s 3-Ring Binder "all request" show at [Icehouse](https://www.icehousempls.com) was fabulous. Small venue, and all songs performed solo on either piano or guitar. He encouraged the crowd to sing along on some of the favorites. The song selection process was chaos, but he made that fun too. A very unique show, that I’m glad I got to be part of. 🎶
