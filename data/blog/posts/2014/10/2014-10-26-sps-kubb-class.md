[Garrick](https://garrickvanburen.com) and I joined forces and auctioned
off a Kubb Class at the annual [SPS](http://www.spscommerce.com) Charity
Auction this year. The sales engineering team won the event and we got
12 of them together this past week for a tournament level Kubb class. It
was an awesome time with a ton of great questions on the game and a
jump-start into Kubb strategy and technique.
