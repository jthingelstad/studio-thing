Gorgeous Coffee in St. Louis.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2024/47912943-2d40-4cfd-a57e-ab50de9e9010.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="Two lattes with latte art in brown ceramic cups on saucers, a biscotti on a wooden board, a spoon, and two glasses of water on a wood table.">

**Update (Mar 2023):** While doing [blog gardening](https://www.thingelstad.com/2023/09/04/blog-gardening.html) I saw this post and thought I should have called out what coffee shop had this "gorgeous coffee". I was sure it wasn't from [Sump Coffee](https://www.sumpcoffee.com). I wasn't sure if I could figure it out but I looked at the phot and found the location data. It was taken at [2103 Cherokee Street in Saint Louis](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Dsa9oCuHMPzZDzoHA). Sump is actually just a few blocks away from that location. There is currently a coffee shop called [The Mud House](http://www.themudhousestl.com) there, but that name doesn't ring any bells and who knows if that was there 10 years ago. We may never know what shop made this delicious combo.
