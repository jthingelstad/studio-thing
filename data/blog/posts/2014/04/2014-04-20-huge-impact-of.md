I host all of my personal projects on two servers at Linode. Last week [Linode announced new "cloud servers" with SSD's, double RAM and a new chip architecture](https://blog.linode.com/2014/04/17/linode-cloud-ssds-double-ram-much-more/). I migrated both of my hosts over to the new servers that evening and the performance impact was immediately noticeable. WikiApiary is the most taxing project I run, and it was clearly much faster. This graph though of the WikiApiary API response time is the most telling. So much better! ![response-time](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/8c4d169188.png)
