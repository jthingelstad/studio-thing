I've been planning to start working on using a [configuration
management](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Configuration_management)
solution for my hobby servers. [Chef](http://www.getchef.com) was too
heavy for my purposes, and confused me a lot. I got really enamored with
[SaltStack](http://www.saltstack.com), but never really caught onto it.
I decided to give [Ansible](http://www.ansible.com/home) a go and
actually have a couple of hosts in [Vagrant](https://www.vagrantup.com)
and am managing some limited packages with Ansible. My [work is in
GitHub](https://github.com/thingles/ansible).

In theory, it should be straightforward to take my build notes and turn
them into Ansible playbooks. We'll see.
