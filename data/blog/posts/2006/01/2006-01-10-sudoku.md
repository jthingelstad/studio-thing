Have you played [Sudoku](http://www.sudoku.com/) (Wikipedia:
[Sudoku](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sudoku)) yet? My mother turned me
onto it. She figured I would like it in part because I like math. Turns
out there really isn't any math to Sudoku, but there are a lot of
numbers and that can be fun. Sudoku reminds me a lot of Minesweeper, but
it's a lot more fun, and you get several "A-ha" moments while playing.

I would play it more, but it just burns time in a big way (hint: great
for flights!) and I've already got a huge backlog of stuff I need/want
to do.
