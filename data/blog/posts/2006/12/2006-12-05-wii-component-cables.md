My component cables arrived for the Wii today. I finally was able to move off the incredibly lame composite connection and [480i](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/480i) to a nice crisp component connection and [480p](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/480p). I know that this is a much better setup, and I eagerly plugged it in and decided to get my camera out and do some before/after comparisons. While I can tell the difference, it's not as striking as I expected. These shots are all taken from the Wii menu screen. I took photos of the "disc" channel and of the Mii channel. I also took a photo of the Wii settings button on the lower left corner of the screen. See what you think.
