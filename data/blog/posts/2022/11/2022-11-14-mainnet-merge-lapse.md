The **[Mainnet Merge Paint Party](https://app.poap.art/mainnet-merge)** was [one of the fun events](https://www.thingelstad.com/2022/09/14/participated-in-my.html) to celebrate the Ethereum Merge in September, and I put some pixels down and put "Thing" on that canvas to be memorialized forever. At the time there was a comment of being able to mint an NFT from the canvas, but it wasn't ready yet. I recently stumbled onto the new and improved POAP Art website and saw that the mint feature was now live!

I [minted](https://etherscan.io/tx/0x696358758972ddc4be2dc602656c90d1b623f59fd4b39e86d492ebe054eb1aae) the **[12th lapse](https://app.poap.art/lapse/12)** and now have my own NFT that is a animated zoom of just the area of the POAP Canvas that my name is in. You can see that I had to defend my pixels through the night.

<a href="https://app.poap.art/lapse/12"><img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2022/7f455b94f9.gif" width="448" height="448" alt=""></a>

If you look closely you'll also see an "HF" in red. That is Hector's pixels that he placed and then I defended, and eventually moved, to keep them in the final. 

I also have my [Mainnet Merge POAP.art Canvas Pixel Placer POAP](https://app.poap.xyz/token/5692651) token as well. 🤩

What a great way to remember such a historic event, and mark "I was here!"
