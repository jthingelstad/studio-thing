[Yesterday](https://www.thingelstad.com/2022/01/01/first-attempt-at.html) I made more 12oz candles, as well as some tealight candles. After realizing that the small tealights needed to be on a towel to slow down cooling they came out great. Today I did a burn test on four of them. The wood wick still gives a great crackling sound, even at the small size. Burn time is impressive at **5 hours** or more. Good scent throw too for such a small candle.

I'll keep a number of these around because you always have little bits of extra wax when making candles and having tealights to fill up would be a great way to use that.
