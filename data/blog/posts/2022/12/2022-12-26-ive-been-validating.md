I've been validating the [Gnosis Chain](https://www.gnosis.io) for about three months now. I'm running **32 validators**. Gnosis made the [transition to using proof-of-stake](https://app.poap.xyz/token/6036366). The validators generate about 0.4 mGNO a day, or about $1.09, a 14% annual yield. On average my 32 validators propose 4 to 5 blocks a day, with a maximum of 10. There are currently 109,386 validators.
