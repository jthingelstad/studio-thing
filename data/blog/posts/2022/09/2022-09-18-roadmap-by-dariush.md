I stumbled upon [Roadmap](https://deca.art/Dariush/roadmapislive) by Dariush ([0xDariush.eth](https://rainbow.me/0xdariush.eth), [@0xDariush](https://twitter.com/0xdariush)) and the project intrigued me. He's hitchhiking from Armenia to Georgia and Turkey and from there to Europe. He is going to share the story on Ethereum. Interesting!

He's supporting the journey a number of ways, including [proposal 862](https://prop.house/proposal/862) from [Nouns DAO](https://nouns.wtf). I also grabbed one of [the OG 100 tokens](https://rainbow.me/thingelstad.eth?nft=ethereum_0xf0acf6d8c28bcd028dea57f3edbf0ba75b35f163_1) to support his trip. 

I find this sort of experimentation interesting and am curious to see how the experience works.
