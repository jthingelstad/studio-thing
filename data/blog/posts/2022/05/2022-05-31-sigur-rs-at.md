We saw [Sigur Rós](https://sigurros.com) at the [State Theatre](https://hennepintheatretrust.org/theatres/state-theatre/) this evening. I first heard of Sigur Rós from their 2008 album [Með suð í eyrum við spilum endalaust](https://sigurros.com/music/medsud/), which I [got into](https://www.thingelstad.com/2012/09/09/back-from-iceland.html) after visiting Iceland in 2012 and then [saw them in 2013](https://www.thingelstad.com/2013/04/03/195650.html) when they played in Minneapolis. 

I enjoy their music but with every album it seems to get more "ethereal" or even ambient, and slow. Their newest work is a far leap from that 2008 album. It is good, but I wouldn't mind if they picked up the pace on some of the newer albums.
