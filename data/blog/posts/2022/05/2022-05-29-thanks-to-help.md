The [LEGO World Map](https://www.lego.com/en-us/product/world-map-31203) is complete!

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2022/4db97f22a8.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="Completed LEGO World Map mosaic with white continents on a colorful multicolored ocean background, mounted in a white frame on a wood wall" />

There are two very notable things about the LEGO World Map.

1. I think this is the ideal LEGO for someone that is really into jigsaw puzzles. The build of this LEGO is not like any other that I've done.
2. I found this LEGO an overwhelming build. I would typically want to do a LEGO on my own, so I experience it all. This one I needed help, and it really got done because a few others got involved and it was very easy to do each tile in parallel.

At 11,695 pieces this is a big project. I [started this project](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/12/26/lego-world-map.html) right after Christmas!

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2022/03a5cd5c97.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="Close-up of a completed LEGO mosaic showing continents and oceans built from thousands of colorful 1x1 round tiles on a table" />

Once we got about 80% done with the tiles we could see the end and and there was a strong desire to get it all together. The last half of the build was more fun than the first half for sure. 

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2022/a2fd9e273c.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="Close-up of a completed LEGO mosaic showing continents in white studs on a colorful teal, green, orange, and pink ocean background." />

Thanks to help from Tammy, the kids, and two of their cousins!
