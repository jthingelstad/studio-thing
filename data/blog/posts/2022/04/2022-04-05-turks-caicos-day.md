- Captain Jack full-day tour with [Grace Bay Adventures](https://www.gracebayadventures.com).
  - Snorkeling at Coral Gardens. Many turtles including Giant Turtles.
  - Toured the canals
  - Half moon bay 
  - Iguana Island
  - Toured coastline
  - Lunch at [Fort George Cay](https://www.visittci.com/other-islands/fort-george-cay).
  - Snorkel at Barrier Reef. Saw two sharks!
  - Snorkel for Sand Dollars in Grace Bay.
- Delicious dinner at [Indigo](https://www.wymara.com/indigo).
