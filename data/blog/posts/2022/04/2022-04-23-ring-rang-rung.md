I recently discovered the work of **[Cats Will Eat You](https://catswilleatyou.com)**, a Minnesota artist who has created and published a new, original piece of art every day for the last **15 years**. To do anything for 15 years is incredible, but to push your creativity in this way is truly remarkable and impressive. I knew right away I wanted to own a piece in this wide ranging collection and Cats Will Eat You has been minting some of his art as an NFT. I'm now the proud [owner](https://etherscan.io/tx/0x623f26ba627fb66d0126264d20d0048ff74af8cb6fa3cf4134116fc8ff431527) of "[ring rang rung](https://knownorigin.io/tokens/8880000)". 

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2022/460359f06d.jpg" width="600" height="600" alt="" />

I liked the art as well as the communication metaphors embedded in it. You can also use [Artivive](https://artivive.com) and see the creation process. The artist also let me know that "ring rang rung" was featured in the [2022 SXSW NFT Gallery](https://schedule.sxsw.com/2022/films/2054796).
