Thursday, August 4th  
Morning was very cold and windy, and improved throughout day. 

- Morning hot tub time. 
- Depart Blue View Cabin. 
- [Explore](https://www.strava.com/activities/7580934743) [Gullfoss](https://www.thingelstad.com/2022/08/04/gullfoss.html). Beautiful! 
- [Explore](https://www.strava.com/activities/7583366341) Geysir in Haukadalur Valley.
- Visit Þingvellir National Park ([part 1](https://www.strava.com/activities/7583366589) and [part 2](https://www.strava.com/activities/7583366751)): [Öxarárfoss](https://www.thingelstad.com/2022/08/04/we-happened-upon.html), [Langistígur](https://www.thingelstad.com/2022/08/04/the-langistgur-or.html), and [Lögberg](https://www.thingelstad.com/2022/08/04/me-at-the.html).
- Dinner at [Restaurant Mika](https://mika.is). The Hot Chocolate (really, Drinking Chocolate) was amazing!
- Return to Blue View Cabin for relaxing evening. 
- Second session in hot tub. Great!
