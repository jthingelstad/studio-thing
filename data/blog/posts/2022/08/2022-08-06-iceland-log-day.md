Saturday, August 6th  
Nice Icelandic day, a little misty in afternoon, no wind. 

Our final full day of this epic [Iceland 2022 trip](https://www.thingelstad.com/collections/iceland-2022/)! To frame the day, imagine that each of us had a few things on our list that we wanted to get done before we finished our trip. The net of that is a lot of walking around, various tasks, and a wide variety of things!

- Breakfast at [Sandholt](https://sandholt.is). 
- Walk down Laugavegur.
- Walk along harbor to a small lighthouse
- [Escaped from Reykjavik Escape](https://www.thingelstad.com/2022/08/06/we-escaped-from.html)!
- More shopping on Laugavegur, with long stop at [Hörg](https://horg.is) but no purchases.
- Yummy snack at Loo Koo Mas.
- [Watch the Reykjavik Pride Parade](https://www.thingelstad.com/2022/08/06/reykjavik-pride-is.html).
- More shopping, resulting in shoes!
- Drop off shopping at Airbnb.
- Never having enough baked goods, stop to try [Brauð & Co](https://www.braudogco.is/en).
- Visit the [Kattakaffihusid](http://www.kattakaffihusid.is/home.html) (Cat Cafe) for cappuccino with Cats!
- More shopping at 66° North to get Mazie a shirt.
- Much deliberation at art gallery.
- [Go see the Sun Voyager](https://www.thingelstad.com/2022/08/06/family-selfie-from.html).
- Shop at Museum Shop in [Harpa](https://harpa.is).
- Tried to see if we could use the [Hopp](https://hopp.bike) scooters to get around, but decided wouldn't work.
- Visit [Vínberið](https://vinberid.is) candy store.
- Visit [Rammagerðin](https://rammagerdin.com) to get Icelandic Wool Blanket.
- Visit [Kronan](https://kronan.is) to get some local snacks to bring home.
- Go to [Fly Over Iceland](https://www.flyovericeland.com)! Stunning visuals, and a lot of places we had been to on the trip.
- We got super lucky and found another street spot to park car in after driving to Fly Over Iceland! 😅
- Final dinner in Iceland at [Íslenski Barinn](https://islenskibarinn.is/en/). Very good food and a very fun waitress.
