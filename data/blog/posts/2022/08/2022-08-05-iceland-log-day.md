Friday, August 5th  
Nice, warm in sun. Sprinkles in afternoon. 

- Depart Blue View Cabin and travel to Reykjavik.
- Breakfast at [Sandholt](https://sandholt.is/).
- Shopping and strolling on Laugavegur: [Skúmaskot](https://www.skumaskoticeland.com), 66° North, Blue Lagoon, Nordic Store, [Nielsen Sérverzlun](https://nlsn.is)
- [Icelandic Punk Rock](https://www.lonelyplanet.com/iceland/reykjavik/laugavegur-skolavordustigur/attractions/punk-museum/a/poi-sig/1571088/1341241) museum. Very fun!
- Cappuccino at Te & Kaffe
- Snack at [Sandholt](https://sandholt.is/)
- Visit [Hallgrímskirkja Church](http://hallgrimskirkja.is).
- Walk around looking for Fish & Chips stand that Tammy had found online but we never were able to find it.
- check into Airbnb at Baldursgata 22.
- Dinner at [Bastard Brew & Food](https://bastard.is/?lang=en)
- Dessert at [Sandholt](https://sandholt.is/). Seems everyone really liked Sandholt. 🤩
- More shopping and exploring on Laugavegur.
- Go to the [Icelandic Sagas: The Greatest Hits](https://icelandicsagas.com) at Harpa. Very fun! 🎭
