Monday, August 1st  
Very nice and sunny, with high winds in parts.

- Visited Skogafoss. Majestic waterfall.
- [Did one-half](https://www.strava.com/activities/7566703913) (2.5 miles) of [Waterfall Way](https://www.earthtrekkers.com/skogafoss-waterfall-way-hike/) hike from Skogafoss to Cascading Falls. 5 miles round trip. Next time we'll do it all.
- Visit [Skogar Museum](https://www.skogasafn.is) to see the turf houses and visit the cafe. 
- [Walk to](https://www.strava.com/activities/7566703838) Kvernufoss from Skogar Museum. 
- Return to The Garage to relax. 
- Pizza at Black Crust Pizzeria in Vik. 
- Go to the [Icelandic Lava Show](https://icelandiclavashow.com)!
