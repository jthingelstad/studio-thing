Our city shared this update on the assessed market value of property.

> **2022 Assessment Report**  
> The 2022 Assessment Report is in. Edina’s total Estimated Market Value rose from $13.76 billion in 2021 to $15.45 billion in 2022. Overall, that’s a <mark>**12.4 percent growth**</mark> in value from January 2021 to January 2022. Total percentage growth in 2022 <mark>exceeds total percentage growth for 2019, 2020 and 2021 combined</mark>. 

That much growth in one year doesn't make sense. Is the market catching up? Or getting ahead? Inflation?
