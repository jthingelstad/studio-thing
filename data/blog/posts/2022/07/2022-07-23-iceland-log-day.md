Saturday, July 23rd  
Warm with Thunderstorms and Hail. ⛈

- Start at home. 
- DL260 cancelled overnight and we are now on an "on time" **DL9900** flight to KEF at 12:45p. Flight should land at 11:55p in Iceland.🤞
- Lyft to airport. 
- [Thunderstorms threatening our departure](https://www.thingelstad.com/2022/07/23/after-last-nights.html). 
- +5 hours to Iceland GMT time. 
- 11:55p Land in Keflavik 
- 1:35a Get rental from Enterprise, trekking from airport since shuttle stopped at 8p. 
- 2:15a Checkin at [Silica Hotel](https://www.bluelagoon.com/accommodation/silica-hotel).
- 2:30a Go to sleep as the sun is rising.
