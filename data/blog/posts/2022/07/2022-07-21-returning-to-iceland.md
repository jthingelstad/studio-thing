In September 2012 I went to Iceland for a weeklong photography trip with three friends. Iceland amazed me and left me in awe. I have wanted to return, this time with the whole family, since the flight landed in Minneapolis. 

**Tomorrow is the day!** We depart from Minneapolis on Friday night, and will land in Iceland on Saturday morning.

I am eager for the adventure to begin!
