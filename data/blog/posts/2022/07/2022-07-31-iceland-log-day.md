Sunday, July 31st  
Pleasant weather.

- Checked out [Fosshotel Glacier Lagoon](https://www.islandshotel.is/hotels-in-iceland/fosshotel-glacier-lagoon)
- Five hour glacier hike on Vatnajökull Glacier with [Troll Expeditions](https://troll.is).
- Gas station for refuel and lunch snacks.
- Quick visit to [Fjaðrárgljúfur Canyon](https://www.theoutbound.com/iceland/hiking/exploring-fjadrargljufur-canyon). Did not go all the way up. We were short on time and Jamie's legs were spent.
- Dinner at [Smiðjan Brugghús](https://smidjanbrugghus.is) in Vík
- Get groceries at KR in Vik.
- Drive to [The Garage](https://thegarage.is) to check-in. This place is amazing!
