Thursday, July 28th  
Windy but Mostly Sunny

- Breakfast at [Fosshotel Mývatn](https://www.islandshotel.is/hotels-in-iceland/fosshotel-myvatn).
- Drive to Borgarfjörður Eystri.
- Stop at Rjúkandafoss to stretch our legs and walk a bit and found out it was the [12th tallest](https://icelandfalls.com/rjukandi-rjukandafoss/) waterfall in Iceland.
- [Epic hike](https://www.strava.com/activities/7544503187) to [Stórurð](https://visitegilsstadir.is/en/things-to-see/storurd/) from Njarðvík Trail Head (65° 33.05 -13° 58.24).
- Arrive in Borgarfjörður Eystri.
- Fabulous dinner at [Frystiklefinn](https://blabjorg.is/frystiklefinn-restaurant/) in [Blábjörg Guesthouse](https://blabjorg.is/resort/).
- Visit the Puffins at [Hafnarhólmi](https://www.east.is/en/place/hafnarholmi).
- Drive to [Skarðás Country Cabins](https://skardas.is) in Egilsstadir for the night.
