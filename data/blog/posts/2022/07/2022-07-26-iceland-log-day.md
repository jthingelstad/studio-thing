Tuesday, July 26th  
Cool and Pleasant

- Start in [Akureyri](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akureyri).
- Breakfast at [Berlin Cafe](https://berlinakureyri.is), downstairs from Airbnb.
- Visit and explore [Goðafoss](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Goðafoss). It is amazing.
- Drive to [Húsavík](https://www.visithusavik.com).
- Visit the [Húsavík Whale Museum](https://www.hvalasafn.is/en/).
- Go Whale Watching with [North Sailing](https://www.northsailing.is/tour/whale-watching-and-puffins/).
- Nice dinner at Naustið.
- Drive to Mývatn.
- Arrive at [Fosshotel Mývatn](https://www.islandshotel.is/hotels-in-iceland/fosshotel-myvatn).
