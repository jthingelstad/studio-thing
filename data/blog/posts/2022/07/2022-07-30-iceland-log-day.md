Saturday, July 30th  
Freaking cold and windy until 2p then really nice after that. 

- Checked out of [Fosshotel Vatnajökull](https://www.islandshotel.is/hotels-in-iceland/fosshotel-vatnajokull/). 
- Drive to [Jökulsárlón glacial lagoon](https://icelagoon.is).
- 1:10p [Zodiac Tour](https://icelagoon.is/zodiac-tours/) was cancelled due to wind. Rebooked on 3:20p.
- Explored the [Diamond Beach](https://guidetoiceland.is/travel-iceland/drive/diamond-beach) and played with ice pushed back onto the beach.
- Delicious lunch at [Nailed It Fish & Chips](https://nailed-it-fish-and-chips.business.site). Really, really good!
- Zodiac Tour which was amazing!
- Check in to [Foss Hotel Glacier Lagoon](https://www.islandshotel.is/hotels-in-iceland/fosshotel-glacier-lagoon).
- Very nice dinner at Foss Hotel Restaurant.
- Hike up to Svartifoss Waterfall.
- Outdoor hot tub at hotel.
