Iceland Log Day 5

Wednesday, July 27th  
Cool and Rainy throughout the day

- Our 20th Anniversary!
- Start in [Fosshotel Mývatn](https://www.islandshotel.is/hotels-in-iceland/fosshotel-myvatn). 
- Drive to [Dettifoss](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dettifoss) to see the incredible falls.
- Explore [Selfoss](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Selfoss_(waterfall)).
- Check out [Námafjall Geothermal Area](https://guidetoiceland.is/travel-iceland/drive/namafjall-geothermal-area), also known as Hverir.
- Hike to the ring of the [Hverfjall](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hverfjall). Incredibly windy at top. 
- Explored the [Dimmuborgir](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dimmuborgir).
- Figured out how to buy gas at N1.
- Anniversary dinner at [Vogafjos](https://www.vogafjosfarmresort.is) Farm-to-Table restaurant.
