Sunday, July 24th  
Cool and Partly Sunny in the morning, and Cloudy and cold in Evening

- Start at [Silica Hotel](https://www.bluelagoon.com/accommodation/silica-hotel) in Blue Lagoon. 
- Relaxation in mineral pools of the Blue Lagoon. 
- Walk around Blue Lagoon and see the big lagoon.
- Lunch and coffee at [Rjukandi Kaffi](https://rjukandi.com/facilities/).
- [Hiked](https://www.strava.com/activities/7522618826) to Narrow Canyon of Rauðfeldsgjá. 
- Visit the Protector of the Snæfellsnes peninsula — Bárður Snæfellsás.
- Hiked to harbor in [Arnarstapi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Arnarstapi). 
- Delicious pizza for dinner in Arnarbær.
- Enter [Snæfellsjökull National Park](https://www.visiticeland.com/article/snaefellsjokull-national-park) and visit the Basalt Formations, Black sand beach, and stare longingly at crater as we ran out of time.
- Visit [Kirkjufell](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kirkjufell) Mountain and waterfall where Jamie took pictures for a long time and Tammy was tired and had a bad headache.
- Arrive at Kirkjufell Guesthouse.
