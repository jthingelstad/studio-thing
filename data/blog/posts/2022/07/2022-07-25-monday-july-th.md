Monday, July 25th  
Cool and misty with some sun

- Start at Kirkjufell Guesthouse.
- Kaffi Grundarfjordur for coffee and croissants.
- Visit the [Glaumbær Turf House](http://www.glaumbaer.is/is/en).
- Drive to [Akureyri](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Akureyri). 
- Nice dinnner at [Strikið Akureyri](https://strikid.is).
- Mineral baths at [Forest Lagoon](https://www.forestlagoon.is/en).
