Friday, July 29th  
Cool and Rain starting in afternoon. 

- Start  at [Skarðás Country Cabins](https://skardas.is) in Egilsstadir.
- Breakfast pastries at [Fellabakarí](https://fellabakari.is).
- Visit [Seyðisfjörður](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seyðisfjörður) to see shops and town. 
- Return to Ring Road and drive to Höfn along the ocean. 
- Stop at [Djúpivogur](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Djúpivogur) to get a Coke and the kids jumping time on Jumping Pillow. 
- Early dinner at [Íshúsið Pizzeria](https://ishusidpizzeria.is) in Höfn. 
- Check in at [Fosshotel Vatnajökull](https://www.islandshotel.is/hotels-in-iceland/fosshotel-vatnajokull/).
