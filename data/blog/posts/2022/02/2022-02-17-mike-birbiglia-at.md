Tammy and I went to [Mike Birbiglia](https://www.birbigs.com)'s newest show at the Pantages and it was so great. We've seen him before and like previous times the entire show was strung together through one story diving off into various tangents. There were moments of incredible laughter intermixed with occasional touching and heartfelt points.
