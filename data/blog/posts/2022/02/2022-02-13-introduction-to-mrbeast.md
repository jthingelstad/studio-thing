Tyler is a pretty big fan of a handful of "[YouTubers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/YouTuber)", and usually at the top of his list is [MrBeast](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/MrBeast). I've been more pessimistic about MrBeast and frankly I’m not sure what to think of the whole YouTuber thing. Tyler decided since it was his birthday we would all get a proper introduction to MrBeast.

We started with Curiosity [Beyond the Spotlight: MrBeast](https://curiositystream.com/beast/index.html). I signed up for the free 1-week trial so we could watch the video. Overall, both Tammy and I left with a more positive view of MrBeast than when we started. Jimmy, MrBeast's actual name, seems like a reasonable person trying to have a positive impact in the world.
