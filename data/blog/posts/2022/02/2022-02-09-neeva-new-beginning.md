[Neeva](https://neeva.com/) recently [rolled out their premium search offering](https://neeva.com/blog/introducing-neeva-free-basic-and-neeva-premium). I've been using it as a tester for months and it was a "no brainer" to me to sign up for a year. In addition to the features I would have expected, they also offered an NFT to early Premium subscribers. What a great idea! This gives me an NFT as a keepsake to highlight my early support for private search. 

Today I received [my New Beginning with Neeva NFT](https://opensea.io/assets/matic/0x73032f6af78a3d92ab383bc8e6aadb5125c1b836/0), and it is super fun. OpenSea says there are 101 owners so far. OpenSea chose to mint the NFT on [Polygon](https://polygon.technology).

Thanks Neeva!
