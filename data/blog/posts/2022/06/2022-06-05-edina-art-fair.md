The [Edina Art Fair](https://www.50thandfrance.com/art-fair/about/) has returned from the pandemic this year! Mazie and I made a quick visit and the art was great! 

Some artists that caught our attention:

- [H. Ward Miles](https://www.hwardmiles.com)
- [Andrew Carson](https://windsculpture.com) – we have had one of Carson’s wind sculptures for 20+ years and love his work. 
- [Brian Schmidt](https://www.lowerwoodlandstudio.com) – Mazie liked his work so much she bought a couple of prints. 
- [Melissa Helene](https://www.melissahelene.com)
- [Bradley Fritz](http://www.fritz.city)
- [Linda Morvant](https://www.lindamorvant.art)
- [Beeper Bébé](https://www.etsy.com/shop/beeperbebe/)
