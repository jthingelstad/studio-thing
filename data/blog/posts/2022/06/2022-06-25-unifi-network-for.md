Ordered network for the new home. Decided to stay with UniFi given the great experiences I have had with our current setup. 

- Dream Machine with integrated router, firewall, and CloudKey. 
- I wanted WiFi 6 and the only option in stock was the Access Point WiFi 6 Long-Range. I got one for each floor. 
- Main switching bay is two Switch Lite 16 PoE and then 2 Switch Flex Mini for remote ports.
