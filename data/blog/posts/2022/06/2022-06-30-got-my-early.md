I hold eight [Noun Cats](https://nouncats.com). I found the project before I really got intrigued by Nouns projects. I guess Noun Cats were my gateway to the greater Noun ecosystem. 

The Noun Cats had a really cool innovation with the creation of [Early Birbs](https://birbs.nouncats.com).

> The Early Birbs are a first of its kind, community-created collection: a collaboration between Noun Cats' Co-Founder and pixel artist Dean Harvey and the Noun Cats community!

The only way to get an Early Birb is to have a Noun Cat. Today I got my eight Birbs to go with my Invisibles. I [minted Early Birbs #418-425](https://etherscan.io/tx/0x6d35ced09406f6079f06ea027b0530c4f87cac4e3bfb7236c2bf3246df6c58cf). I think they are great, and I love all the Nounish aspects of it.
