[Peter Yarrow](https://www.peteryarrow.net) blew us away. Playing the family tent, pure folk music. A high point of the [Winnipeg Folk Festival](https://www.winnipegfolkfestival.ca). Legend. 🎼 ☮️ 💟

[Peter Yarrow](https://www.peteryarrow.net): "I changed the ending of [Puff, the Magic Dragon](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Puff,_the_Magic_Dragon). You want to know why? ... Cause I can!" Puff doesn't die anymore.

[Richard Inman](https://richardinmanmusic.bandcamp.com) on Little Stage in the Woods. [Winnipeg Folk Festival](https://www.winnipegfolkfestival.ca). Great!
