We went to [Martina](https://www.martinarestaurant.com) for the first time last night and I was very impressed! The space is great and we got to sit right by the kitchen and watch the woodfire grill being stoked. We started with the Crab Cakes which were amazing. Then we had the Pork Chop Pizzaiola and Fusilli with Lamb Shoulder for the main and both were fabulous. The Panqueque dessert was delicious. The meal didn't cost an arm and a leg and they even have a kids menu and mocktails, which we will likely try out next time when we bring the whole family.
