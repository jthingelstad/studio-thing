I got the new micro.blog iOS app and I really dig how [Manton Reece](https://manton.org) has implemented subject lines and longer posts in a microblogging platform. The title bar appearing after the body gets to a certain length is a very elegant solution. I still like the idea of having a main blog separate from the fragments on my microblog, but it's great seeing this evolve in interesting ways.
