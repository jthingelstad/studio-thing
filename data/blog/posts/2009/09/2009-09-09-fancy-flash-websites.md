just one big flash application. Here is one simple one. The other day we
were thinking of going to Tejas for lunch and wanted to check their
hours. Simple enough. So I brought up their website on my iPhone. This
is what I got.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/0e740b3a26.png" style="max-width: 320px; " />

Needless to say, totally and completely useless. In fact, it took me a
