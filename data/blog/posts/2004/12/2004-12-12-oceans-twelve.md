Tammy and I along with her two sisters and their significant others went
to see [Oceans Twelve](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0349903/) today. I
thought [Oceans Eleven](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0240772/) was great
so I was eagerly looking forward to [Oceans
Twelve](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0349903/). It was a fine movie, but
didn't live up to the previous one. For some reason the soundtrack and
cinematography impressed me more than the actual story. Will have to
check out the soundtrack when it is available.
