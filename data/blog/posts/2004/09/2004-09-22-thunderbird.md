I [mentioned before](https://www.thingelstad.com/2004/08/14/thunderbird.html) that
I've ditched Microsoft Outlook in favor of
[Thunderbird](http://www.mozilla.org/products/thunderbird/). I've been
using [Thunderbird](http://www.mozilla.org/products/thunderbird/) for a
while now and I'm really liking it. Also, they just released 0.8 of it
which has notably added RSS support! Finally I have a worthwhile RSS
reader for home use that is integrated. If you haven't checked out
[Thunderbird](http://www.mozilla.org/products/thunderbird/), it's worth
looking into.
