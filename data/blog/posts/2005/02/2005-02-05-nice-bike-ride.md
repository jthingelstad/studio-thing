I took advantage of the unseasonably warm weather today to get out on my
bike. I realize that you can indeed ride year-round even here in
Minnesota, but it takes a fortitude that I do not seem to have. However,
today's 50+ °F temperatures made it a great day to get out on the
bike.

I did a quick 17 mile ride down to the lakes and back. I wasn't the only
one with the idea, there were plenty of cyclists on the trails. I should
have wore my leg warmers and booties. My bottom half was a bit frigid.

What a great day!
