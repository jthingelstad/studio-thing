I'm a big fan of high resolution album art. It improves the overall
music experience for me. I like having a high-quality album cover while
listening to Apple TV. It's a must for cover flow in iTunes. I recently
went through and tried to close to 100% coverage of album art, which
meant scanning some covers that I just couldn't find anywhere on the
Internet. I figured I should share that work, so that others can more
easily put the right art on their music. Here are 11 covers all at 1,600
by 1,600 pixels that I scanned for my collection.
