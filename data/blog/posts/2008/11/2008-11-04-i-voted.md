I got out and placed ballot #382 this morning around 8:30 am. Contrary to what I've heard in many precincts there was almost no line and I was able to walk in and out in about 10 minutes. I talked to my mother who said it took her about an hour and she had to stand in lines over a hundred people long. There was a [longer line in 2006](https://www.thingelstad.com/2006/11/07/010000.html), but I also voted right at poll opening that day with ballot #37.
