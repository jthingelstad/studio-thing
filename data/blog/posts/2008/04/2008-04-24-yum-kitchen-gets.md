The Internet continues to expand! I was recently at [Yum! Kitchen and Bakery](http://www.yumkitchen.com/), a [regular favorite restaurant](https://www.thingelstad.com/2006/01/22/yum-kitchen-and.html) of ours, and was happy to see [my iPhone](https://www.thingelstad.com/2007/07/01/i-got-my.html) detect a new open wireless network named "Yum".

You can now happily nosh on some great food, coffee and treats while surfing the Internet in style.
