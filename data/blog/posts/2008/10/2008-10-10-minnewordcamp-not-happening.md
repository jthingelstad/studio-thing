Back in February I posted a feeler to see if people would be interested
in [getting a Minneapolis WordCamp
together](https://www.thingelstad.com/2008/02/16/interested-in-a.html).
The response was pretty great, without any date, promotion or agenda
nearly 75 people signed up to indicate interest.

While there has been a lot of interest, I'm not going to be able to lead
the effort to get a WordCamp going here in Minneapolis. Coordinating an
event like this is far outside my interest area and not something I do
all that well. Plus we weren't getting great uptake on sponsorship and
the current economic meltdown is not going to make that better. We also
lost our keynote speaker and were again going to have to look for a new
date.

I'm hopeful that someone else is interested in seeing this happen and
may take the lead and run with it.
