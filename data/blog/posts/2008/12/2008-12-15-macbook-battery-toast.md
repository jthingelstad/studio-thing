I was just updating our oldest Mac, my original MacBook (black) that is now pretty much Tammy's laptop. I was upgrading it to 10.5.6 and realized two things.

1.  It was way behind in updates. I'm usually so good with this, but 6
    updates to apply? Yikes!

2.  The battery is so wasted that it couldn't even make it through the
    update process. Ouch! I fired up
    [CoconutBattery](http://www.coconut-flavour.com/coconutbattery/) to
    see how bad it was and promptly ordered a new battery.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/8dfecc2981.png" alt="CoconutBattery 2.6.3 app screenshot showing battery at 0 mAh charge, 513 mAh capacity vs original 5020 mAh, 10 percent, 184 load cycles, 31 months old" style="max-width: 426px;" />

184 load cycles and 31 months isn't horrible though.

### New Battery

Took a quick trip to the Apple Store and got a new battery. The old black MacBook is now like new! Actually, better than new looking at the numbers.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/9d897299fd.png" alt="CoconutBattery 2.6.3 app showing new MacBook battery at 98% charge, 5494 mAh capacity exceeding original 5020 mAh, 1 load cycle, 32 months old" style="max-width: 426px;" />
