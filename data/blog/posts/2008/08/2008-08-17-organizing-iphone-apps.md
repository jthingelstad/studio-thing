I've loaded my iPhone up with a decent number of applications now and
found it difficult to find things quickly. I've started organizing the
Springboard panels: **main, info, media, games, other**.

This is working well but it bugs me when you update an app and it loses
it's location and has to be put back manually. I hope Apple is working
on something to make this more manageable.
