A couple of days ago I was grilling some chicken breasts on the [Big Green Egg](https://biggreenegg.com) and opened the top too fast without "burping" the egg and letting some oxygen in more slowly. A huge burst of flame shot out and took care of a good patch of hair on my arm! Smelled gross.
