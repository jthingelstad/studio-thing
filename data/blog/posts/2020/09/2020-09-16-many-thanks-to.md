Many thanks to Slalom Consulting for their [Slalom Presents: Realizing Racial Equality](https://www.slalom.com/presents-minneapolis) program. Hearing the more complete story from [Ruby Bridges](http://www.rubybridges.com) in her school desegregation experience, [Dr. Condoleezza Rice](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Condoleezza_Rice) and her views on race and education, and a musical closing from [Leon Bridges](https://www.leonbridges.com) was a good backgrounder on the work we are pursuing to address racism in our society.
