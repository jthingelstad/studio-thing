With the kids away with their cousin for a couple of days, I cleared my calendar and Tammy and I took a day to ourselves. We got started early with a big bike ride. We drove out to Wayzata and had a delicious breakfast outside at [The Grocer's Table](https://thegrocerstablemn.com). Then we went to the start of the start of the [Dakota Rail Regional Trail](https://www.threeriversparks.org/location/dakota-rail-regional-trail/) and took off. The trail was great with wonderful views in all directions. When we got to Lake Waconia we turned around. We put **33 miles** in on the out and back.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/1615c2db77.jpg" width="375" height="350" alt="" />

In addition to breakfast we stopped on our ride back for a fun round of mini golf at [Big Stone Mini Golf](http://bigstoneminigolf.com). This is the [best mini golf place](http://bigstoneminigolf.com/?ID=Map) that I've been. You are surrounded by stone sculpture too. After we finished golfing we wandered through the [sculpture garden](http://bigstoneminigolf.com/?ID=OG). After the round of mini golf we stopped for lunch at [Dakota Junction](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Restaurant_Review-g43343-d4842885-Reviews-Dakota_Junction-Mound_Minnesota.html) in Mound and then finished the last few miles back to Wayzata. What a great way to spend a morning!
