Before leaving the Lanesboro area we stopped at [Eagle Bluff Environmental Learning Center](https://eaglebluffmn.org) and did a casual hike on their trails. We hiked down to the Root River on a forested loop.

We had the place all to ourselves, plus a lot of bugs, two snakes, and some frogs and toads.
