After our [morning bike ride](https://www.thingelstad.com/2020/09/02/ride-the-dakota.html) Tammy and I continued our day of fun and adventure with a late afternoon kayak down the Crow River. We met [River Dragon Kayak Rental](https://sites.google.com/view/river-dragon-kayak-rentals/home) outside of Saint Michael and then drove upstream to Hanover and put in the river there.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/118b1aecc7.jpg" width="374" height="542" alt="" />

Here we are at the put in spot. If I look nervous it’s because I’m not very confident in kayaks still. 

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/c2d2c83e93.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="" />

After I settled in and navigated a few shallow water rock bumping occasions I got comfortable and we had a stellar paddle down the river. It took us about 90 minutes to go the 5 miles. After we finished we had a nice dinner outside at [Main Street Farmer](http://www.mainstreetfarmer.com) in St. Michael.
