We had a great event recognizing  [International Women’s Day](https://www.un.org/en/observances/womens-day) at [SPS Commerce](https://www.spscommerce.com). Great keynote and exercise around vulnerability and bias led by [Brave New Workshop](https://www.bravenewworkshop.com/creativeoutreach/keynotes/)'s Margi Simmons and a wonderful panel from women leaders in #TeamSPS! Thank you [Kara Boespflug](https://www.linkedin.com/in/karaboespflug), [Jenny Nolan](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jenny-nolan-a39ba813), [Anna Picchetti](https://www.linkedin.com/in/annapicchetti), [Nikki Riemersma](https://www.linkedin.com/in/nikkiriemersma), and [Bre Taft](https://www.linkedin.com/in/bretaft).
