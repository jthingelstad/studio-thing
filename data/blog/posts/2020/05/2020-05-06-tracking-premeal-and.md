I’ve been tracking my food using [Ate](https://youate.com). One of my favorite features in Ate is that you can setup questions to answer about the meals, and tracking pre-meal and post-meal hunger level is useful information to me. However, I didn’t like the options so I made this set of options combined with fun emoji that work really well for me. I have the same options for a pre-meal and post-meal question.

0 - Lost hunger 😶  
1 - Irritable 😠  
2 - Hungry 👍  
3 - Satisfied 👍  
4 - Full 👍  
5 - Sluggish 😒  
6 - Stuffed 😟  

The goal is to stay in the 👍 values.
