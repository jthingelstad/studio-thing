Tammy unlocked a perfect pandemic evening for us last night. First, get a bottle of a beverage of choice. We had a bottle of Moscato d'Asti. Second, place a to go order at [Station Pizzeria](https://stationpizzeria.com). Get your pizza at the scheduled time, and park in Burwell Park about a block away. Enjoy said beverage with your pizza along the shore of Minnehaha Creek, the sound of rushing water in the background. Afterward, walk back where you were and get dessert at the [Dairy Queen](https://www.dairyqueen.com/us-en/locator/Detail/?localechange=1&store-id=1625&) and have a casual stroll back to the park with your ice cream. 

5 stars! Would do again!
