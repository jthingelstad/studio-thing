Watching the Dragon capsule slowly approaching the [International Space Station](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/International_Space_Station). The space station was launched in 1998. The technology on it is 21 years old, but the [C2V2 system was updated in 2016](https://www.spaceflightinsider.com/missions/iss/international-space-station-gets-communications-upgrade-new-crew-preps-arrival/), designed for arrivals like the one happening today. The level of detailed and long-term planning required to make all this happen is just amazing.
