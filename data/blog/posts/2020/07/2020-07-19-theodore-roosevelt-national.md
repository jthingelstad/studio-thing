We spent the night in Medora last night and before heading out on our way to Bozeman we decided to take a very brief trip into [Theodore Roosevelt National Park](https://www.nps.gov/thro/index.htm). We only had a couple of hours so we skimmed the high points hitting. We made the drive on the Scenic Loop with stops at Wind Canyon, Boicourt Overlook, and Buck Hill. We also made a stop at the Prairie Dog town. It was gorgeous scenery and we had a great time checking out the short trails.
