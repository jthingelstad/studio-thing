These results are incredible, and seem almost unstoppable.

> “America’s biggest tech firms reported dizzying figures, as each surfed on the pandemic’s waves. **Amazon’s** third-quarter sales grew 37% year-on-year, to more than $96bn; it hired 50% more staff to cope with the demands of locked-down households. Alphabet was the only giant whose share price strengthened, as **Google’s** ad sales recovered, especially on YouTube. **Apple’s** iPhone sales were even weaker than expected, while overall revenue and profit held steady. **Facebook** blew past glowering governments’ scrutiny to report quarterly revenue growth of 22%, with a prediction of better to come over the holidays.” — [The Economist](https://www.economist.com) Espresso, October 30th, 2020
