Rain made our stop at [Jay Cooke State Park](https://www.dnr.state.mn.us/state_parks/park.html?id=spk00187#homepage)’s [Swinging Bridge](https://www.dnr.state.mn.us/state_parks/jay_cooke/swinging_bridge.html) far too short. The light was really cool, and the scenery was awesome. We are going to come back tomorrow!
