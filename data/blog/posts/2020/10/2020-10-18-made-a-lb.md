Made a 3 1/2 lb tenderloin special dinner tonight. Cubed the tip and fried with olive oil and garlic. Put the rest in Sous Vides for 3 hours and a finish sear in [Big Green Egg](https://biggreenegg.com). Scalloped Potatoes and homemade Peanut Butter Pie for desert. 🤤🔥
