Today #TeamSPS joined together around the world for Growth Summit 2021! We had a great event, with the show being broadcast live from the Minneapolis Convention Center. We had a ton of precautions to keep things as safe as possible with the pandemic. 

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/d4bda9dfe4.jpg" alt="Large LED stage screen displaying Greetings from GROWTH Summit 2021 postcard-style graphic with mountain backdrop, blue stage lighting">

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/56a0f25138.jpg" alt="Large conference hall with sparse socially distanced audience watching a Customer Centric Experience presentation on a wide stage screen">

We had a thought provoking keynote from [Greg Bell](https://www.gregbellspeaks.com) on the power of a simple question, What is Going Well?
