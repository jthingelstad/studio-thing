Today I got to take Tyler to [Berry Patch](http://www.berrypatchschool.com) for pre-school to attend Dads and Donuts day! Tyler was **super** excited for Dad to come to school with him. We played with Play-Doh while everyone arrived. We then went in and talked to Fireman Tom from the Edina Fire Department. Tyler was sitting up front and volunteered to come up front and try on a fire fighter suit! He was so excited!

He got to stand in fireman boots:

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/069ea15d52.jpg" alt="Young boy in oversized firefighter turnout pants and SCBA tank stands beside a kneeling Edina Fire Department firefighter indoors.">

Put on a fireman jacket!

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/e57a380142.jpg" alt="Young blond boy wearing an oversized yellow firefighter jacket stands next to a smiling firefighter in uniform crouched beside him indoors">

And a real fireman helmet!

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/3d8ccb9f86.jpg" alt="Toddler wearing an oversized red firefighter helmet and yellow turnout gear stands beside a kneeling Edina Fire Department firefighter indoors.">

After our talk with Fireman Tom we went out front and got to see a ladder truck up close! Tyler posed with Ms. Anita for a picture in front of the truck.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/1973aa99f4.jpg" alt="Woman crouching beside a toddler boy in a blue jacket and a girl in a white coat in front of an Edina Fire Rescue truck">

He even got to drive it!

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/60ea6239c8.jpg" alt="Young blond boy in a blue plaid jacket sitting in the driver seat of a Pierce fire truck cab, hand on the steering wheel">

We finished with a delicious donut with another fire truck on the table!

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/50025ece08.jpg" alt="Young blond boy in a blue jacket eating a donut at a round table with toy fire trucks on yellow napkins and a glazed donut on a yellow plate nearby.">
