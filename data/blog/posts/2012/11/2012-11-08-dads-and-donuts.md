Today I got to take Tyler to [Berry
Patch](http://www.berrypatchschool.com) for pre-school to attend Dads
and Donuts day! Tyler was **super** excited for Dad to come to school
with him. We played with Play-Doh while everyone arrived. We then went
in and talked to Fireman Tom from the Edina Fire Department. Tyler was
sitting up front and volunteered to come up front and try on a fire
fighter suit! He was so excited!

He got to stand in fireman boots:

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/069ea15d52.jpg)

Put on a fireman jacket!

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/e57a380142.jpg)

And a real fireman helmet!

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/3d8ccb9f86.jpg)

After our talk with Fireman Tom we went out front and got to see a
ladder truck up close! Tyler posed with Ms. Anita for a picture in front
of the truck.

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/1973aa99f4.jpg)

He even got to drive it!

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/60ea6239c8.jpg)

We finished with a delicious donut with another fire truck on the table!

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/50025ece08.jpg)
