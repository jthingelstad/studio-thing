My buddy [Garrick](http://garrickvanburen.com/) came over yesterday and
we enjoyed some practice rounds of Kubb across the street at [Minnehaha
Creek West
Park](http://www.minneapolisparks.org/default.asp?PageID=4&parkid=496).
We played three games. The first one quickly went to him and the last
quickly went to me. The one in the middle was an hour long ebb and flow
of knights shifting from side to side.
