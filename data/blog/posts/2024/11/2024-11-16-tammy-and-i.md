Tammy and I went to the _sold out_ **[7 Nights in the Entry](https://www.soundunseen.com/events/mpls-festival-film-7-nights-in-the-entry-filmmaker-in-attendance)** at the [Parkway Theater](https://theparkwaytheater.com), part of the [Sound Unseen](https://www.soundunseen.com) 25th Annual Film festival. [Chris Strouth](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chris_Strouth) of [Twin/Tone](https://www.twintonedigital.com), [Greg Norton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Greg_Norton) of [Hüsker Dü](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hüsker_Dü), and [Rick Fuller](https://www.rickfuller-inc.com/) introduced it. This was only the **seond showing ever**!
