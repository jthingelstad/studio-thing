[Alby](https://getalby.com) is deprecating their shared [Bitcoin Lightning](https://lightning.network) wallet service. I don't want to host my own node or use their cloud hub so I transferred the [satoshis](https://www.coindesk.com/learn/what-is-a-satoshi-understanding-the-smallest-unit-of-bitcoin/) I had there via lightning to my [Strike](https://strike.me) wallet. Still amazes me how fast and simple Lightning is. ⚡️
