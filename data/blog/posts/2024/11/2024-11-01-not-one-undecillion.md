Not one undecillion, but two!

> **Russia** ordered **Google** to pay a fine of two undecillion roubles (roughly $20,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000). The sum is many times bigger than the world's GDP. The fine is punishment for restrictions on [Russian state media](https://www.economist.com/1843/2023/04/26/inside-the-school-where-russias-state-tv-journalists-are-trained) on YouTube, a website that Google owns. A Kremlin spokesperson reportedly said that he "cannot even pronounce this number". — The Economist, World in Brief, Nov 1 2024

Yes an [undecillion](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Names_of_large_numbers#Standard_dictionary_numbers) is a thing. What is the point of a ruling like this?
