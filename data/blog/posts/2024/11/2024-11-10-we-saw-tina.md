We saw [Tina Schlieske](https://tinaschlieske.com)'s Birthday Show at [The Dakota](https://www.dakotacooks.com) and it was incredible! She was joined by her sister Laura and they hit a ton of great songs and pulled in a bunch of covers too. It got so fired up by the end that people were standing up and dancing, at a jazz club, and she sang from a table! 🔥
