The annual [Aspirations in Computing](https://mntech.org/about/mhtf/aspirations/) event highlights the accomplishments of incredible young women that are doing amazing things in technology. For the eighth year running [SPS Commerce](https://www.spscommerce.com) has sponsored this program and we've hosted dozens of Aspirations interns now, including some that have gone on to be college interns and even then team members! This was also the first year for Aspirations in Computing since finding a new home with the [MnTech Foundation](https://mntech.org/about/mhtf/) and holding the event at the [Metropolitan Ballroom](https://metropolitanmpls.com). 

Many members of TeamSPS volunteer and are engaged with Aspirations in Computing, but special recognition for [Amy Patton](https://www.linkedin.com/in/amyjpatton/) who has put so much into this great program! 👏
