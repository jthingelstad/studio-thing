For Mother's Day I ordered some [Freese's Candy](https://www.freesescandy.com) for Tammy. We had these when we were in Milwaukee and she has declared them her favorite Turtles. They are delicious. So I ordered one pound of them for her as well as a pound of salted caramel in milk chocolate. 

I timed the order so it would come next week, but they shipped it fast and it delivered even faster. They dropped it off on Saturday at 2:34pm. 

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2024/23102d47-c775-4d63-8059-0a3bccc04ff3.jpg" alt="Delivery person in a cap and dark jacket leaving a white bag on a covered front porch with wood ceiling and hanging lanterns.">

In just two hours, an intrepid squirrel approached. The squirrel smelled it, pushed the box around, tipped it over, and got to work at whatever delicious smells were inside.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2024/f18cdb89-b211-4ae2-a3cc-ab4b56227d4d.jpg" alt="Front porch security camera footage showing a squirrel near a package by a decorative column, with lawn and suburban street visible beyond.">

This rascal even took a break sitting on my chair! 🐿️

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2024/8f612ae9-85d1-410c-9ab4-9d3d5cfd1d69.jpg" alt="Front porch security camera still showing a squirrel resting on a wooden chair with Get off my chair written in yellow text overlay">

After the mayhem was in full swing one of the neighborhood rabbits thought they would check it out too.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2024/aa0aac48-a3cf-47b6-8255-64fa491ab2d6.jpg" alt="Front porch security camera footage at 8:24am showing two wooden chairs and yellow text overlay reading Rabbits too beside a spiral column">

Over the course of that afternoon and the next morning several squirrels stopped by to check out the situation. 

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2024/4cf3f3af-9c27-4bc6-95e6-239236013689.jpg" alt="Front porch security camera view showing a small cardboard box on the ground near rocking chairs, with text Chocolate Feast written over the scene">

When we got home we found both boxes of chocolate completely emptied and bits of the box scattered around. I hope those squirrels were miserable from all that sugar. 

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2024/c9a0491a-1f3d-4cac-8663-81dad9064185.jpg" alt="Destroyed USPS mailing box sitting on a stone porch, top torn open and shredded, with scattered cardboard pieces on the ground around it.">
