We have celebrated [Vyshyvanka Day](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vyshyvanka_Day) in our Kyiv office for years now, but this year we also decided to take part in Vyshyvanka Day in Minneapolis as well. [Alexander Ivakhnenko](https://www.linkedin.com/in/aivakhnenko/) and [Valentin Kornukh](https://www.linkedin.com/in/valentinkornukh/) led the event with delicious Ukrainian snacks and treats including: Pierogi (including Potato, Meat, and Cherry), [Modovik](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Medovik) cake, [Kyiv cake](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kyiv_cake), Napoleon Cake, and Kvas to drink. We also had a Ukrainian trivia challenge to learn more about different aspects of Ukraine. It was a great time! 🇺🇦
