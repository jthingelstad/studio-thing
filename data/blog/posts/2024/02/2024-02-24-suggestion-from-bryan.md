Suggestion from [Bryan Hansel](https://www.bryanhansel.com) to find **your** photography style — go through your photos for the year and pick your favorite single one from each month. Not your best, or most liked. Your favorite! Look at those twelve photos and reflect on what the common threads are between them all.
