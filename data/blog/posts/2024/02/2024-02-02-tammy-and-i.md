Tammy and I had a great night seeing Words & Music by **[Dan Wilson](https://danwilsonmusic.com)** at the [Woman's Club of Minneapolis](https://www.womansclub.org). He was joined my **[John Munson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Munson)** for a few **[Semisonic](https://semisonic.com)** songs. The rest of the night he shared stories of various writing sessions and sang the songs that came from them. **Laamar** opened the night. It was great. 🎶
