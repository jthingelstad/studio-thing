While [blog gardening](https://www.thingelstad.com/2023/09/04/blog-gardening.html) today I found random `<0xa0>` characters in over 100 blog posts. A quick search revealed it as the decimal for a non-breaking space. I used micro.blog replace function to replace with a space, except two that were in titles and manually replaced.
