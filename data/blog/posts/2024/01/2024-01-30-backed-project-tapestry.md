[Iconfactory launched](https://blog.iconfactory.com/2024/01/kickstarting-project-tapestry/) a [Kickstarter campaign for Project Tapestry today](https://www.kickstarter.com/projects/iconfactory/project-tapestry). [Iconfactory](https://iconfactory.com) is a great company that has built some of the most delightful experiences ([Linea](https://linea-app.com/), [Frenzic](https://frenzic.com/), [Twitterific](https://twitterrific.com/)). Tapestry intends to be an open "timeline" of content from a variety of services. I’m intrigued because of the focus on open systems and the world-class skills of Iconfactory. Backed!
