The [Singing Sakatah Hills State Trail](https://www.dnr.state.mn.us/state_trails/sakatah/index.html) is closed right by Highway 60 and Dalton Ave near Cannon Lake. They have a section fenced off where the trail edge has collapsed. The record rains must have been too much for this area. Couple of trees down too.
