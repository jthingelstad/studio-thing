It was a nice hot day to enjoy a float down [Minnehaha Creek](https://minnehahacreek.org/) today. Tiffany and Andrew were visiting so we got the tubes inflated, drove to [Utley Park](https://www.edinamn.gov/Facilities/Facility/Details/Utley-Park-31) in Edina to put in and floated right back to our house taking out in [Minnehaha Creek Park](https://www.minneapolisparks.org/parks-destinations/parks-lakes/minnehaha_creek_park/). This was the furthest we've gone on the tubes going 3.7 miles and took just over 2 hours. 💦
