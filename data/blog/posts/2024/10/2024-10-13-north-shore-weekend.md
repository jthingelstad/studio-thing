- Drive to Duluth.
- Pizza and beer at [Ursa Minor Brewery](https://www.ursaminorbrewing.com).

- Drive to Grand Marais.
- Stop at [Fika Coffee](https://fikacoffee.com) for delicious coffee.
- [Pickup donuts](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/10/12/worlds-best-donuts.html) at [World's Best Donuts](https://worldsbestdonutsmn.com).

- Ice cream at [Superior Creamery](superiorcreamery.com).
