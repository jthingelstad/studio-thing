On our way from Ballyshannon to Belfast we took a big detour so we could go see [Giants Causeway](https://giantscausewayofficialguide.com). We had seen this kind of columnar geology [before in Iceland](https://www.thingelstad.com/2012/09/07/columnar-geology.html), but it wasn't as accessible and right along the sea like this. We didn't have a lot of time to explore, but we made the most of what we had. It was a very cool place to visit.
