_Weather: Great weather. Maybe the best day we've had._

**[Father's Day!](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/16/im-a-very.html)**

- Breakfast at the hotel. 
- Figured out Dublin bus logistics.
- Took C2 bus from Wellington Quay (stop 312) to South Circular Road. 
- [Guided tour of Kilmainhham Gaol Museum](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/16/kilmainham-gaol-museum.html). 
- Walked to [Guinness Storehouse experience](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/16/guinness-storehouse.html) at St. James Gate.
- Lunch at 1837 Bar & Braserie.
- Took cab to Windmill Lane Studios for [very cool tour](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/16/windmill-lane-stuido.html).
- Walked to Trinity College to [see the Book of Kells](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/16/we-took-the.html).
- Walked back toward hotel and explored Grafton Street and got some truffles at [Butler's Chocolates](https://www.butlerschocolates.com/en).
- Got to hotel and cleaned up for dinner.
- Special Father's Day dinner at [F. X. Buckley on Crow Street](https://www.thebuckleycollection.ie/crow-street). Delicious steaks.
- Return to Cloud Nine for Tyler to get a crêpe and Tammy and Mazie an ice cream.
- Stop quickly at the hotel to change clothes.
- Walk to Darkey Kelly's for some traditional music.
- Four piece band playing with a lot of issues with their microphones.
- Return to the hotel. Everyone tired.
