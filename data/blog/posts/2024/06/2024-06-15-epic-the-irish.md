This evening we made our way to [EPIC: The Irish Emigration Museum](https://epicchq.com/) in Dublin to learn about the history of emigration in Ireland. EPIC is a newer museum just opened in 2016. It tells the story of how Irish people have emigrated throughout the world and the impact made by them. It also covers much of the history of Ireland itself. I thought it did an amazing job using multimedia experiences. The best I'd ever seen for that.
