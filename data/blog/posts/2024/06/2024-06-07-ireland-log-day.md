_Weather: Mixed bag. Periods of cool and cloudy, sunny and warm. It rained at times. Quickly changing and variable._

We did everything on foot or bike today — walking between all of our destination.

- Up early to leave cottage at 8 AM. 
- Walk 2 miles from cottage to the Doolin pier. 
- Stop at [Rocket House](https://www.fiddleandbow.ie/the-rocket-house.html) for breakfast snacks and cappuccino.
- [Bumpy ride](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/07/we-took-the.html) on [Doolin Ferry](https://doolinferry.com) to [Inis Mór](https://www.aranislands.ie/inis-mor-inishmore-island-aran-islands).
- [Explore Inis Mór](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/07/exploring-inis-mr.html).
- Additional shopping in the stores along the Doolin Pier.
- Final shopping stop at [Irish Crafts](https://www.irishcraftsonline.com). Tammy ends up getting four sweaters today.
- Dinner at [The Ivy Cottage](https://www.theivycottage.ie) for what most think is the best fish & chips we've had on the trip.
- Walk back to cottage.
