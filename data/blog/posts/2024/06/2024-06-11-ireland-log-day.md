_Weather: Reasonably pleasant. A little less windy. Cool temperatures._

- Relaxed morning recharging. Tammy and Mazie go for run. Jamie tends to photos and blog. Tyler sleeps in. 
- Drive to Donegal. 
- Brunch at [The Old Stone](https://www.theoldstone.ie). 
- Shopping and exploring around [The Donegal Diamond](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Diamond%2C_Donegal) including Elvery Sports and Magee of Donegal.
- Drove to [Rossnowlagh Beach](https://donegalbeaches.com/beaches/rossnowlagh/).
- After two failed attempts to have Tyler and Mazie do a surf class, serendipity struck and we were able [to get them learning to surf](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/11/tyler-and-mazie.html)!
- Pick up breakfast items for tomorrow at the Bakery Shack. 
- Drive to Cria Lodge so kids could get warm and dry. 
- Drive back to Donegal for dinner at [La Bella Donna](https://labelladonnarestaurant.com). 
- Drive back to Cria Lodge and walk a kilometer down the road (precisely the middle of nowhere) for a [pint at Whoriskey's Pub](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/11/todays-guinness-enjoyed.html). 
- Walk back to Cria Lodge to get packed and ready for early morning departure tomorrow. Alarm set to 6:30 AM.
