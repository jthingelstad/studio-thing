_Weather: Rain. Wind. Crappy. Better as we got further South._

- Tammy and Mazie go for a run in Belfast. 
- Continental breakfast in [Titanic Hotel](https://www.titanichotelbelfast.com/#hp1). 
- Check-out of hotel. 
- Visit [Titanic Museum](https://www.titanicbelfast.com). 
- Afternoon tea at Titanic Hotel. 
- Visit the [S.S. Nomadic](https://www.titanicbelfast.com/explore/ss-nomadic/), a tender for the Titanic. 
- Departed Belfast for Kilkenny. 
- Crossed into Ireland between Killeen and  Dromad.
- Experienced rush hour traffic around Dublin. 
- Fastest driving of the day — nearly all divided highways and 120 km/h speed limit. 
- Dinner at [Brewery Corner](https://brewerycorner.com) in Kilkenny. 
- Get Tyler crêpe at Scrumdiddlys.
- Walk around Kilkenny briefly, everything closed for the night.
- Drive to [Tubbrid Castle](https://www.tubbridcastle.com) and [check-in for our stay](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/13/tubbrid-castle.html).
- Explore all the various areas of the castle.
