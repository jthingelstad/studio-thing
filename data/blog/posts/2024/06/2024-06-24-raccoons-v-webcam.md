With [Cannon Lake flooded](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/23/cannon-lake-flooded.html) I had repositioned one of the [UniFi G4 Instant](https://store.ui.com/us/en/collections/unifi-camera-security-compact-wifi-connected/products/camera-g4-instant) cameras so it was pointing right at the boat.

I've also been battling with raccoons at our place. This summer I had 6 of them trapped and taken away. But there are still more. 

And on the very first night of having this camera in position the raccoons decided to play with it.
