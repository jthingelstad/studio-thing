_Weather: Sunny. High of 67 °F. Pretty cool after sunset._

- Jamie gets some sleep on flight, everyone else not so much. 
- DL114 arrives in Dublin slightly early at 10:39 AM local time. 🛬
- Clear passports and get luggage.
- Pick up [SEAT Leon](https://www.seat.ie/new-cars/leon/overview) from [NewWay](https://www.newway.ie) rental. Make sure we have all insurance options. Figure out how to fit all of our luggage. Just barely fits.
- Drive to Killarney. Soundtrack was [U2](https://www.u2.com), [Foo Fighters](https://foofighters.com), [The High Kings](https://www.thehighkings.com). Stop at [Barrack Obama Plaza](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barack_Obama_Plaza) to get Jamie coffee and a Coke. Kids slept the whole time.
- First experience on narrow Irish roads — wow there is not much room for two way traffic. 
- Stop at [Killarney Brewing & Distilling](https://killarneybrewingdistilling.com) for a beer and a snack.
- Check into AirBnb in Killarney and get settled.
- Walk around Killarney and explore shops.
- Dinner at [Hilliard's](https://hilliardskillarney.com).
- Walk back to rental to rest and get some sleep.
