_Weather: Best weather yet. Good amount of sun. Reasonably warm. Not too windy._

Another day spent entirely on foot. Activity for Tammy is 40,995 steps and 95 flights climbed and Jamie 32,498 steps and 81 flights climbed.

- Tammy and Mazie go on independent runs. 
- Tyler and Jamie walk to [Irish Craft](https://www.irishcraftsonline.com) for breakfast. Tyler got pancakes. I got a scone and ham & cheese croissant. 
- Tyler, Jamie, Tammy meet at [Doolin Inn](https://www.doolininn.ie) about 10 minutes early for cliff walk. Mazie arrives 2 minutes late. 
- [Pat Sweeney](http://www.doolincliffwalk.com/About.aspx) collects the group for the amazing Doolin Cliff Hike.
- After completing hike visit the [shop at Cliffs of Moher](https://www.cliffsofmoher.ie/about-the-cliffs-of-moher/cliffs-of-moher/the-visitor-centre/) and Tammy gets a purse. 
- Take shuttle back to Doolin Inn. 
- Shopping at [Village Crafts](https://doolin.ie/village-crafts/). Tammy and Mazie get hats to vibe with Jamie's. 
- Ice Cream stop at [Doolin Ice Cream](https://doolin.ie/doolin-ice-cream/)! 
- Chocolate stop at [Village Crafts](https://doolin.ie/village-crafts/). 
- We try our hand at golf at the [Doolin Pitch & Putt](https://doolin.ie/doolin-pitch-and-putt/).
- Everyone is very hungry so walk and have dinner at [Stonewall Wood-Fired Pizzeria](https://www.hoteldoolin.ie/stonewall.html) in Hotel Doolin.
- Walk back to cottage to hang out for a bit.
- Walk back into Doolin for music and Guinness at [Gus O'Connors Pub](https://gusoconnorsdoolin.com).
- As we lose light, walk back to cottage to wrap the day.
