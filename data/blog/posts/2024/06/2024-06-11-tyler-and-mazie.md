Tyler and Mazie wanted to try surfing in Ireland and there are a number of places that do surf lessons but we weren’t having luck with any of them. Then we found [Rossnowlagh Surf School](https://rossnowlaghsurfschool.com) on the beach and they were happy to have them join. 55 °F with 15 mph winds.
