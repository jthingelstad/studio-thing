We joined thousands of our best friends on the [Guinness Storehouse](https://www.guinness.com/en-gb/experiences/guinness-storehouse) visit today. As a fan of Guinness this was a Father's Day highlight for me. It was not a brewery tour, but instead more of a history of Guinness itself. I liked seeing some of the archive of advertisements over the years. And we got to get a "Stoutie", a selfie printed into the head of the Guinness. We finished with lunch at [1837 Bar & Brasserie](https://www.guinness-storehouse.com/en/eat-and-drink#1837bar) and my favorite beef stew of the trip. Awesome.
