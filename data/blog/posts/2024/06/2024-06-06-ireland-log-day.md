_Weather: Good weather today — partially cloudy and less wind._

- Tammy was up at 8 AM, completely ready to go and prepared by 9 AM. Everyone else awoke shortly after. 
- Tammy and Tyler go to [Marine Bakery & Cafe](https://www.themarine.ie/cafe-and-bakery/) for light breakfast. 
- We all go for final walk along Ballybunion Beach at low tide. 
- Depart Ballybunion for [Adare Manor](https://www.adaremanor.com) in Adare. 
- [The best Afternoon Tea](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/06/afternoon-tea-at.html) ever at Adare Manor. 
- Post tea shopping at Adare Store where unexpectedly [Tammy buys a jacket ](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/06/tammy-modeling-her.html)and Tyler swim trunks. Jamie gets 1893 Tea to bring home. 
- Drive to Doolin. 
- Stop at Doolin Lodge, [check-in](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/06/checking-in-at.html) to [Cottage at Doonagore Castle](https://www.airbnb.com/rooms/956608867348704411). 
- Walk to [McGann's Pub](https://mcgannsdoolin.com) for [dinner, Guinness, and traditional Irish music](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/06/we-walked-from.html) set. 
- [Walk back to cottage](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/06/sunset-over-a.html).
