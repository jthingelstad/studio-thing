_Weather: Windy and rainy today and very cool. Highs around 55 °F._

- Breakfast at [The Shire](https://www.theshirekillarney.com). Waffles and French Toast. 
- Finished packing and loaded car. Checked out of Airbnb. 
- Drove to [Ross Castle](https://heritageireland.ie/places-to-visit/ross-castle/).
- Started raining as we boarded the [Gap of Dunloe Traditional Boat Tour](https://gapofdunloetraditionalboattours.com). 
- [Took boat 14 miles](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/04/we-had-an.html) to Lord Brandon's Cottage. 
- Jamie gets Scone and Earl Grey Tea before we start hike. 
- Depart on [hike of the Gap of Dunloe](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/04/hike-the-gap.html). 
- Ascend 3.5 miles up the road. 
- 1:37 PM we reach the top point in the Gap of Dunloe and it is ridiculously windy. 
- Descend 3.5 miles to [Kate Kearney's Cottage](https://katekearneyscottage.com). 
- [Jamie gets Guinness](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/04/todays-pint-of.html), everyone else an ice cream. 
- Dux from boat tours picks us and others up to return to our car at [Ross Castle](https://heritageireland.ie/places-to-visit/ross-castle/). 
- Dinner at [The Tan Yard](https://thetanyardkillarney.ie). 
- Drive to Ballybunion. 
- Listen to [Jamie's interview on Omni Show](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/03/omni-show-how.html) on the way. 
- Check into [Breakers Beach](https://www.airbnb.ie/rooms/45171198) Airbnb with amazing views. 
- Unload luggage. 
- Mazie and Tyler explore the very cold beach. 
- Tyler returns and Tammy went out to collect Mazie. 
- Watch sunset from living room.
