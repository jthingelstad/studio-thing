_Weather: Windy, partly cloudy. Cool but pleasant. Very cold for water activities._

A rare unscheduled Thingelstad vacation day with open options. 

- Breakfast at [Marine Bakery & Cafe](https://www.themarine.ie/cafe-and-bakery/). 
- Wandered around Ballybunion.
- [Walked and explored along the beach](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/05/ballybunion-beach-day.html) for extended time. 
- Lunch snacks. 
- Attempt to book [Surf School](https://ballybunionsurf.com) but too late.
- Back to beach and Tyler and Mazie got on suits and braved the very cold water a bit. 
- Dinner at Namir's. 
- [Walk the Cliff Walk](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/05/after-dinner-at.html). 
- Head to [McMunn's per my Barber's suggestion](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/05/my-barber-sent.html)
- Return to Airbnb
- Mazie and Tyler explore the beach even more
