_Weather: Very good, periods of warmth, periods of cold. Was sunny for good portions. Scattered rain shower._

**Happy Birthday Mazie! 🎉**

- Depart Tubbrid Castle at 8:30 AM for Kilkenny, about 30 minute drive.
- Arrive in Kilkenny early so Mazie can go on a birthday run. Tammy goes on a walk. Tyler and Jamie go to breakfast spot early.
- [Cafe La Coco](https://cafelacoco.ie) for a delicious breakfast.
- Brief shopping — Tyler gets soccer cleats at Intersport.
- [Medieval Mile Museum](https://www.medievalmilemuseum.ie) [walk](https://www.medievalmilemuseum.ie/medieval-mile-trail/) to learn so much about Kilkenny.
- Visit [Kilkenny Castle](https://kilkennycastle.ie) and take self-guided tour.
- Some more wandering around exploring various shops.
- [Smithwick's Experience](https://www.smithwicksexperience.com) to learn about Smithwick beer and have a glass of Smithwick Red Ale and Kilkenny Cream Ale.
- Go to [Cakeface Pastries](https://cakeface.ie) to get treats for Mazie's birthday!
- Wander around Kilkenny visiting various stores.
- Visited the [Rothe House](https://rothehouse.com) and spent most of our time exploring the magnificent walled garden. 
- More shopping and exploring for Tammy, Mazie, and Tyler — Jamie stops at The Field pub to get a Guinness and listen to live music. 
- Everyone meets at The Field and we walk down to the Nore Valley Path.
- Walk Nore Valley Path to bridge and back.
- Had nice dinner at [Ristorante Rinuccini](https://www.rinuccini.com) including dessert that came with a candle and rendition of Happy Birthday for Mazie.
- Get car out of car park.
- Drive back to Tubbrid Castle for the night, about 10:15 PM.
