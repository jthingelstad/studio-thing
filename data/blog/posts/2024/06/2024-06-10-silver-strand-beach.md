After taking in the Sliabh Liag we set the navigation to Silver Strand Beach about 30 minutes North. We drove through the most remote part of Ireland that we had experienced so far with [tons of sheep](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/10/today-was-filled.html) everywhere, including some on the road. When we arrived we were greeted to one of the most picturesque beach scenes we've ever taken in. This beautiful harbor with a perfectly crescent beach that seemed almost undisturbed by anything. Including the four of us there were never more than 10 people on the beach.

Toward the end of our time we had a bunch of dolphins show up in the bay jumping out of the water and playing. It was incredible.
