_Weather: Overall good. Some sun. Kind of windy. Chilly but not too bad._

- Picked up to go baked goods and breakfast items at the [Bakery Shack](https://goodfoodireland.ie/dining/the-bakery-shack/) in Rossnowlagh. 
- Drove to [Teelin Pier](https://www.thewildatlanticway.com/sight/teelin-pier/). 
- Took [Sliabh League Boat Trips](https://sliabhleagueboattrips.com) and [got a tour](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/10/sliabh-league-boat.html) of [Sliabh Liag Cliffs](https://www.sliabhliag.com) from the water with Paddy. 
- [Lunch](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/10/todays-guinness-at.html) at [The Rusty Mackerel](https://therustymackerel.com).
- Coffee stop at [Brew In Thru](https://www.tripadvisor.ie/Restaurant_Review-g2389895-d21160299-Reviews-Brew_In_Thru-Teelin_County_Donegal.html) in Teelin. 
- Drove to upper car park for [Sliabh Liag Cliffs](https://www.sliabhliag.com) and hiked up to viewing location. Mazie hiked up beyond to a further point. 
- Drove to [Silver Strand Beach](https://donegalbeaches.com/beaches/silver-strand/).
- Delightful time on this [incredibly secluded beach](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/10/silver-strand-beach.html). 
- Started drive back to Airbnb. 
- Stopped in Donnegal for food. Ended up having crêpes at Little Mamma's as most dining options were closed. 
- [Fed the Alpacas](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/10/mazie-has-been.html) at the Airbnb. 
- Evening hot tub at Airbnb. Tammy gets an evening walk in.
