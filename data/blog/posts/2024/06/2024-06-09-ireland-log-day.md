_Weather: Cool with gentle but steady rain much of the day._

Today was a longer transit day starting in Doolin and making our way to Ballyshannon for the next stop.

- Left Doolin at 8 AM. 
- Drove to Galway. 
- Breakfast at [Dela](https://dela.ie). 
- Shopping and exploring in Galway: Port West (Tyler Jacket), [Butler's Chocolates](https://www.butlerschocolates.com/en) (Truffles), Aran Sweater Shop, [Weavers of Ireland](https://weaversofireland.com), Intersport, Life Style Sports (Tyler Jersey), [EuroGiant](https://eurogiant.ie) (Tyler Fan), [Coffeewerk + Press](https://coffeewerkandpress.com) (Jamie Mug), [Wooden Heart](https://www.woodenheart.ie) (Mazie Maileg Mice), [Le Petite Delice](https://www.lepetitdelicegalway.com) (Jamie Scone), [Harper](https://harper.ie) (Tammy clothes), [The Sheep](https://www.thesheep.ie), and [Murphy's Ice Cream](https://murphysicecream.ie).
- Checked out the [Galway Market](http://www.galwaymarket.com).
- Drive to [Ashford Castle](https://ashfordcastle.com). 
- [Go on a Hawk Walk](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/09/hawk-walk-at.html)!
- Drive to Bundoran. 
- Dinner at Blue Leaf. 
- Continue to [Cria Lodge](https://www.airbnb.ie/rooms/739161778099261165) to check-in. 
- Try out hot tub for our first night.
