We went Duckpin Bowling tonight at [Koz's Mini Bowl](https://www.yelp.com/biz/kozs-mini-bowl-milwaukee). Koz's has the oldest original Duck Pin bowling lanes with manual pin setters that sit behind the pins while you bowl.

**Tammy won with 198**, Mazie second at 194, Tyler third at 177, and I came in last at 140.
