Tammy and I saw [Brett Goldstein](https://brett-goldstein.com)'s standup show "[The Second Best Night of Your Life](https://www.pabsttheatergroup.com/events/detail/brett-goldstein-2024)" at the [Riverside Theater](https://www.pabsttheatergroup.com/venues/detail/the-riverside-theater) while in Milwaukee. Goldstein is best known for playing [Roy Kent](https://ted-lasso.fandom.com/wiki/Roy_Kent) on [Ted Lasso](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ted_Lasso). Many good laughs and a great venue.
