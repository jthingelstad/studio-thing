- 7:24a Fill Mazda CX-9 with gas
- 12:03p Lunch at [Archie's Flat Top](https://www.archiesflattop.com) in Milwaukee: Triple Cheeseburgers for Tyler and Dad. 4 oz patties. Triple patties were a mistake. Brilliant French fries.

- [Milwaukee Public Museum](https://www.mpm.edu). Particularly enjoyed Old Streets of Milwaukee.

- Dinner at [SafeHouse](https://www.safe-house.com).
