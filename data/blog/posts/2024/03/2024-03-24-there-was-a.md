There was a stack over two feet high of large sheets of paper in the [Milwaukee Art Museum](https://mam.org/). They all had printed in very small print in the middle "VETERANS DAY SALE". We brought one back figuring Mazie may want to do a drawing on it. In the meantime it became Tammy's own movable art piece.
