The most open of the [Summer Challenges](https://www.thingelstad.com/collections/summer-challenge-2024/) I gave [Tyler](https://tyler.thingelstad.com) was to [create a Playdate Experience](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/07/10/summer-challenge-create.html). No requirements other than something that could be played on the [Playdate](https://play.date) and created in [Pulp](https://play.date/pulp/). Tyler really got into this and created a full interactive game experience — **[The 5 Piece Sword](https://tyler.thingelstad.com/2024/08/30/i-made-a.html)**. 🗡️

He wrote additional blog posts on the [simple enemies](https://tyler.thingelstad.com/2024/08/31/this-post-is.html) in the game, how the [heart system](https://tyler.thingelstad.com/2024/09/06/heart-system-in.html) for health works, and the [spikes and spike balls](https://tyler.thingelstad.com/2024/09/07/spikes-spikballs-ect.html) in the game.

The game is fun to play and has a lot of challenging levels. He's still crafting the final boss stage and at that point he may publish the file so others could load it. Nicely done! 👏
