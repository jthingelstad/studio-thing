We made three trips to the Minnesota State Fair this year and the third visit really happened just because of [Peachey's Donuts](https://peacheysbakingco.com). We wanted to go back and try them fresh. The line was crazy long and it indeed took an hour, but these warm and tasty donuts are incredible.
