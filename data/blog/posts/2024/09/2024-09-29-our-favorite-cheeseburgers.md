Since we just [tried a new burger place](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/09/29/we-made-our.html) I thought it would be fun to capture our favorite places that the Thingelstad family goes to for cheeseburgers.

- [Lion's Tap](https://lionstap.com): this is not a smash burger but it is a delicious classic cheeseburger. The standard bearer.
- [Tap Society](https://www.tapsociety.com): this is closest to our house and the burger here has evolved over the years. It is always good and a bonus that their fries and shakes are top notch too.
- [Lake & Irving](https://www.lakeandirving.com): the cheeseburger here is heavenly. 
- [The Dakota](https://www.dakotacooks.com): there are much fancier items on the menu, but the double cheeseburger at the Dakota is great and one of the cheaper items on the menu.
- Flat Top: Honestly the best, and only **true smash burger** we get is the one I make on the flat top at the cabin. I haven't found many (any?) restaurants that truly let the patty adhere to the grill surface to be scraped off. It adds a lot of time and effort.
