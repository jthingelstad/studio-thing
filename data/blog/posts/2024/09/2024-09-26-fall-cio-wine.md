Another incredible evening for the **CIO Wine Tasting** event hosted by [Dee Thibodeau](https://www.linkedin.com/in/deethibodeau/) of [Charter Solutions](https://www.chartersolutions.com) and [Terri Naughtin](https://www.linkedin.com/in/terri-naughtin-b6141a2/) of [Naughtin Group](https://www.naughtingroup.com). [Tony Peleska](https://www.linkedin.com/in/tony-peleska-2839102/) of [Kraus Anderson](https://www.krausanderson.com) provides a stunning venue on their balcony in downtown Minneapolis. What a wonderful group of technology leaders to connect with. And the highlight of the evening is [Chuck Kanski](https://www.solovinowines.com/about) of [Solo Vino](https://www.solovinowines.com) providing an incredible array of unique wines with the background on each of the grapes. 🍷
