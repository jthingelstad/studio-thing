I took the pontoon back to [La Canne's](https://www.lacannesmarine.net) for winterization and storage. I got it out on May 10th this year for 127 days on the water. With the [flooding this year](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/06/23/cannon-lake-flooded.html) we have done very little boating. I tried to avoid the rain but ended up getting pretty soaked on the ride in.
