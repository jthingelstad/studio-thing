We wrapped up a very fun weekend in Crosby exploring the Cuyuna area. Here is what the weekend covered.

### Thursday

- Arrived at [Crosby Lofts](https://www.crosbyloftsmn.com) just before 9pm.
- Walked around Crosby and explored [Lake and Company](https://www.thelakeandcompany.com) store that was open late. 

### Friday

- Jamie gets coffee at [Mixed Company](http://www.enjoymixedcompany.com).
- Jamie working remote for the morning.
- Tammy goes on a run.
- Mazie goes on a run.
- Tammy and kids get donuts at [Mac Daddy's Donut Garage](https://www.cuyuna.com/macdaddysdonutgarage/). Bring back for Jamie. 
- Kids and Tammy go to beach at Serpent Lake to swim and read.
- Lunch at [Drunken Noodle](https://www.drunkennoodlecrosby.com). 
- Get rental bikes at [Red Raven](https://www.redraven.bike). 
- Mountain bike the Gallooping Goose "easy" trail. 
- Return bikes. 
- Post-ride smoothies at [Red Raven](https://www.redraven.bike).
- Dinner at [Hudson Smokehouse](https://www.hudson218.com). 
- Walked to [High Wheel Confectionary](https://www.highwheelconfectionery.com) and indulged in shopping and sugar. 
- Walked around Crosby while Mazie was incapacitated due to sugar overload. 
- Mazie and Tyler walk to playground to check it out.

### Saturday

- Tammy goes on a run.
- Jamie to [Red Raven](https://www.redraven.bike).
- Tammy joins at [Red Raven](https://www.redraven.bike).
- Mazie goes on a run.
- Tyler on raids for [Pokémon GO Fest 2024 (Global)](https://gofest.pokemongolive.com/global).
- All meet at [Crosby Farmers Market](https://www.cuyuna.com/farmers-market/): scones, jams, maple syrup, cinnamon rolls, kombucha, homemade Nutella, chocolate dipped mangoes. 
- Shopping in Crosby: [Nord Hus](https://www.cuyuna.com/nord-hus/), [Smith + Trade](https://smithandtrade.com), [Crosby Pharmacy](https://www.guidepointpharmacyrx.com/locations/crosby), [Cuyuna Outfitters](https://cuyunaoutfitters.com).
- Jamie and Tyler walk to lake while Mazie tries on lots of clothes at [Cuyuna Outfitters](https://cuyunaoutfitters.com).
- Lunch at [Burritos California](https://www.cuyunabrewing.com/food.html) and beer at [Cuyuna Brewing](https://www.cuyunabrewing.com).
- Mazie and Tammy find treasures at [Common Goods](https://www.bridgesofhopemn.org/common-goods) thrift store.
- Went to Pennington boat launch to go on water with kayaks from [Cuyuna Outfitters](https://cuyunaoutfitters.com). Mazie and Tammy on clear kayaks. Tyler on paddle board. Jamie on fishing kayak. 
- Paddle from 3 to 7p, cover about 3 miles. Nice time. Lots of jumping in water. 
- Dinner at [Rafferty's Pizza](https://www.raffertyspizza.com).
- Return trip to [High Wheel Confectionary](https://www.highwheelconfectionery.com) for ice cream!
- Tammy, Mazie, and Tyler walk back to [Crosby Lofts](https://www.crosbyloftsmn.com). 
- Mazie and Tyler hang at the playground
- Jamie drives the car back.

### Sunday

- Jame heads to [Red Raven](https://www.redraven.bike) for morning blogging and coffee.
- Tammy and Mazie on a walk and meet Jamie at [Red Raven](https://www.redraven.bike).
- Brunch at [Croft Pub & Grub](https://croftkitchencrosby.com). 
- Head home.
