<p class="callout">This post is part of the <a href="/collections/summer-challenge-2024/ ">Summer Challenge Collection</a>, a collection of tasks or projects for our kids to do over the summer.</p>

This is a very open ended challenge to use the Playdate [Pulp](https://play.date/dev/#cardPulp) web-based game editor to create some kind of experience for the [Playdate](https://play.date). This could be a game, something educational, or just silly. There is no complexity requirement but it should have some goal that it is designed for.
