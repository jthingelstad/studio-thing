My cousin [Quinn](https://quinnchrest.com?ref=thingelstad.com) had mentioned this place that sold coffee via the Terminal using `ssh` but I hadn't remembered to check it out. He recently [built his own terminal-based task tracker](https://quinnchrest.com/2024/07/19/terminal-task-tracking.html) and mentioned terminal.shop again so I gave it a try. 

Clearly the goal here is to appeal to the techies and they are doing it great. They also did a good job with `curses` support as I tested resizing the window and everything was nicely responsive.

You start in the shopping experience. Who needs pictures? I know what coffee beans look like…

The FAQ page…

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2024/terminal-shop-faq.png" width="600" height="438" alt="">

And I even placed an order. The email I got said it was order #000, and made sure to indicate it was zero-indexed. 🤣

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2024/terminal-shop-order.png" width="600" height="438" alt="">

We'll see how the coffee tastes! If you want to order some coffee of your own, just type `ssh terminal.shop` in your terminal.
