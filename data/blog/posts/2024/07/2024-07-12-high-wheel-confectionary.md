We were all super impressed and had a fun time visiting [High Wheel Confectionary](https://www.highwheelconfectionery.com) in Ironton. The ice cream was delicious with very generous scoops. The soda cellar was wild with some very crazy flavors. I got a bottle of [Moxie](http://drinkmoxie.com/) which I discovered for the first time when we were visiting Maine many years ago. I also got a [Goo Goo Cluster](https://googoo.com) which [we made when we were in Nashville](https://www.thingelstad.com/2022/03/21/we-stopped-into.html)! There is even a toy store selection upstairs with a lounge.

It was clear that the person that created this store had put a lot of thought and passion into it. Great stop if you are in the area!
