When I decided to get a hot tub one of my buying criteria was making sure I can monitor and control the spa from my phone. Yesterday I got the [Bullfrog X8](https://www.bullfrogspas.com/spas/x-series-hot-tubs/) connected. The app itself isn't fancy but does the things you need it to do including changing modes and locking the controls.
