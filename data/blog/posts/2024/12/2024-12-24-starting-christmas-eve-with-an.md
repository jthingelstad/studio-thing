Starting Christmas Eve with an amazing cup of [Dreamy Melon](https://paircupworks.com/products/101-wash-processed-coffee-beans-from-colombia) from the ever amazing [Pair Cupworks](https://paircupworks.com/).

> **Dreamy Melon!** -- We've sold some pretty great coffees this year, but this one is probably the top. Clean, crisp sucrose sweetness, and fragrant melon flavor -- this marvelous flavor is a result of meticulous processing including constant monitoring, temperature control, and finely tuned fermentation.

A truly delightful cup. Very subtle flavor tones. Smooth with very little bite. Would agree on the clean, crisp taste. One of the best coffees I've ever had. ☕️
