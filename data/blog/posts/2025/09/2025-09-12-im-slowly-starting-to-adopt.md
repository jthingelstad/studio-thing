I'm slowly starting to adopt the new **Planned Date** in [OmniFocus 4.7](https://www.omnigroup.com/blog/omnifocus-4.7-now-available). At a minimum it will reduce the use of a "Today" tag. I already use **Due Dates** sparingly but can now use them even less. The combination of Planned and **Defer Dates** are good enough for most things.
