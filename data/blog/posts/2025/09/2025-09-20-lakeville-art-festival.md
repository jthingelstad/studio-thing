We found ourselves with some open time this weekend and decided to check out the [Lakeville Art Festival](https://lakevilleartscenterfriends.org/art-festival/) this morning. We've been to many art fairs but this was our first time to this particular one and we thought it was great. They had a great selection of art from artists in Minnesota and connected states. It was setup very nicely on grass instead of walking on blacktop. 

It was great that they sectioned a stage with music and food into an entirely adjacent area with a good amount of picnic tables. It wasn't hard to find a spot to sit a delightful lunch of [Pizzeria 201](https://www.pizzeria201.com) from Montgomery, MN (we [drove there once to have their pizza](https://www.thingelstad.com/2020/07/05/made-a-short.html)) followed by some [HomeTown Creamery](https://www.hometowncreamerymn.com) ice cream. We also brought home some [Groveland Confections](https://grovelandconfections.com) chocolate.

Overall it had a nice and relaxed vibe.

Some artists that caught our eyes:

- [Tin Cat Studio](https://www.tincatstudio.com)
- [Amanda Pearson](https://amandapearsonart.com)
- [Pleasant Street Pottery](https://pleasantstreetpottery.com)
- [Dan Wiemer](https://danwiemer.com)
- [Mya Austin](https://www.myamakesstuff.com)
- [Platypus Builds](https://www.platypusbuilds.com): We very nearly came home with a super cool lamp made entirely from wood.
- [Reiko Uchytil](https://www.helloimreiko.com)
- [Barret Lee](https://www.barretpaints.com)
- [Wenwen Liao](https://www.wenwenliao.art)
- [Shane Anderson](https://www.shaneandersonart.com): Very "guy art" but I dug the concept and he had a bison painting that was interesting.
- [Tyler Maddaus](https://www.tmadsart.com): His bison painting caught my eye.
- [Burly Babe Woodworking](http://burlybabewoodworking.com)
- [Noah Sanders](https://www.artofnoahsanders.com): We very nearly came home with one of his fox paintings. It may still happen. 
- [Ed Lefto](https://eddiesartwork.com): We purchased one of his birdhouses. (the URL is there, but no website)
