We stopped at the Arboretum’s [AppleHouse](https://arb.umn.edu/AppleHouse) today and picked up a bag of [First Kiss](https://mnhardy.umn.edu/first-kiss-rave) Apples today. I love Honeycrisp but this early season variety is really good! Very juicy, crunch, and tart. Not as sweet but available a month earlier! 🍎
