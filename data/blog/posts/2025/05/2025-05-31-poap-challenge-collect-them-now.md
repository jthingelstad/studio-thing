Way back in the day I loved Geocaching. It was so much fun, particularly before it got commercialized. It was a way of exploring the area around you and finding cool, hidden things. 🗺️

When I first found POAPs the capability to claim a POAP for a location was something I wanted right away, and now you can with location drops! I created [my very first location POAP](https://www.thingelstad.com/2025/05/25/i-created-my-very-first.html) and even [recorded a video claiming it](https://www.thingelstad.com/2025/05/25/screen-recording-claiming-my-very.html). Awesome. I wanted to make something with this. 🤔

Then I connected the dots between the [612 Series](https://opensea.io/collection/612-series) NFTs which I have a [complete collection of](https://www.thingelstad.com/2023/04/05/complete-collection-of.html). You may remember I also [interviewed Erik Halaas](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/01/15/interview-with-series.html) who created it. This was perfect -- peanut butter and chocolate moment! ✨

**Thus the [612 POAP Challenge](https://www.thingelstad.com/projects/612-poap-challenge/) was created!** 😲

If you live in Minneapolis join in and go collect some of these locations. The event runs until Labor Day. I think this will be a fun summer activity! Get the whole family involved on some walks, bike rides, kayaks, and explore the city. I [collected my first one](https://www.thingelstad.com/2025/05/31/finished-mile-walk-in-minutes.html) this morning! 🧭

I love that I was able to create an entire activity on the POAP platform. 🤩

_PS: Please spread the word to others so we can get a bunch of folks playing along!_
