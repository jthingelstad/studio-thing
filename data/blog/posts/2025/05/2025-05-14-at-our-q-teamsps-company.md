At our Q2 #TeamSPS company meeting I was honored to share the stage with Elizabeth Neuville and [Christy Sovereign](https://www.linkedin.com/in/christy-sovereign/) and announce our support of the [2026 Special Olympics USA Games](https://2026specialolympicsusagames.org) — [SPS Commerce is the official Bocce sponsor](https://2026specialolympicsusagames.org/sports/bocce)! Why Bocce? It needs the most volunteers and TeamSPS values volunteering!
