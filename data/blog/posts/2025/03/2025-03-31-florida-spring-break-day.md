- Up early at 7:00 am. 
- Head to [The Donut Experiment](https://www.thedonutexperiment.com) to start the day. 
- Tammy, Mazie, and Tyler go on [C Ponies](https://cponies.com) horse riding activity. 
- Jamie goes to [Orange Blossom Coffee](https://www.orangeblossomcoffeeshop.com) to tend to some [SPS](https://www.spscommerce.com) stuff he needed to do. 
- Jamie picks up everyone and head back to Airbnb. 
- [Walk around the shoreline of the island](https://www.thingelstad.com/2025/03/31/finished-mile-walk-in-minutes.html) to lunch. 
- Long and relaxing lunch at [The Waterfront](https://thewaterfrontrestaurant.net). 
- Tammy and Mazie shop. Jamie and Tyler return to Airbnb. Shopping: [Pineapple Marketplace](https://www.pineapplejunktion.com), [Shiny Fish Emporium](https://shiny-fish-emporium.myshopify.com), [Ami Outfitters](https://amioutfittersfl.com), [Two Sides of Nature](https://twosidesofnature.com), and [The Lazy Turtle](https://lazyturtlebeachrentals.com).
- Overcast and cool day, so head to the beach. 
- Lots of throwing football, sand feet. 
- Got treats from an [Ice Cream Boat](https://www.thingelstad.com/2025/03/31/project-there-are-ice-cream.html)!
- Cleaned up and went out on bikes to dinner. 
- Went to [The Porch](https://www.theporchami.com) for late dinner.
- Ride bikes back to the Airbnb in the dark.
