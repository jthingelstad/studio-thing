While doing my [blog gardening](https://www.thingelstad.com/2023/09/04/blog-gardening.html) today I noticed that it was **16 years** ago today that I was a [guest on MPR's Midmorning show talking about Twitter](https://www.thingelstad.com/2009/03/16/guest-on-minnesota.html)! I decided to give it a listen, and while I was at it I ran the audio through [Whisper Transcription](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/whisper-transcription/id1668083311?mt=12) and added a transcript to the post. [Listen or read](https://www.thingelstad.com/2009/03/16/guest-on-minnesota.html)! 😊

It all seems very quaint in retrospect. I had been on Twitter at that time for 27 months and as [John Moe](https://www.johnmoe.website/) shares in the intro it had 8 million users at the time. Moe quotes [Joe Sucheray](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Joe_Soucheray) saying Twitter was "the end of the world." He was maybe seeing better than [Julio Ojeda-Zapata](http://www.ojezap.com), my fellow panelist and I. I had almost forgot that [John Hodgman](https://www.johnhodgman.com/) joined us on the phone and that one of the callers "Dave" who teaches "Greek and Latin" is a friend of mine!

Hashtags weren't a thing. Moe even says "number sign". The word "tweet" was only just forming. I refer to the "Twitterverse" at one point. 

It did give me a big warm smile at the end though when I got a plug in for using RSS! As Tammy reflected, some things never change! ￼￼😁
