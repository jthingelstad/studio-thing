I asked my [Weekly Thing GPT](https://www.thingelstad.com/2025/02/25/weekly-thing-gpt.html) a simple question "Which issue did the Fortune first appear in?". Seems easy enough. It came back with [Weekly Thing 212](https://weekly.thingelstad.com/archive/weekly-thing-212-words-mermaid-beats/) in 2022. I knew that was not right. The right answer? [Weekly Thing 53](https://weekly.thingelstad.com/archive/weekly-thing-53-may-12-2018/) in 2018. Another example where LLMs are not great at specific and exact requests. 🤨
