We first discovered [Peachey's Donuts](https://peacheysbakingco.com) at the [Minnesota State Fair where we enjoyed](https://www.thingelstad.com/2024/09/02/we-made-three.html) the delicious treats — not once, or twice, but three separate occasions. Our family likes donuts! 😁

But Peachey's "home base" is actually Sarasota, Florida and that is where Spring Break was bringing us this year. Tammy made sure our travel plans aligned so that we could make a stop and have our breakfast on the road. A bonus was that we got to try the pretzel as well — it was incredible!

If you see a Peachey's truck anywhere near you. Go there!
