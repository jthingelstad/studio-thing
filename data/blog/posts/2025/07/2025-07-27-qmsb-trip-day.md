- Our **23rd Wedding Anniversary**! 💍
- Checkout of [Hotel Vermont](https://hotelvt.com).
- Walk in rain to breakfast at [August First](https://augustfirstvt.com) ☔️
- Our plan to rent bikes and ride the [Island Line Trail](https://www.localmotion.org/island_line_trail_map) including the [Bike Ferry](https://www.localmotion.org/bike_ferry) was stopped due to rain. 🌧️ We went to the place and tried riding but the rain was too much and not letting down. Rescheduled for Aug 4. Must ride Island Line! 😬
- Four hour drive to Quebec from Burlington. 
- 11:35 AM: Cross into Canada at [Highgate Springs—St. Armand/Philipsburg Border Crossing](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Highgate_Springs–St._Armand/Philipsburg_Border_Crossing). 
- Park car. 🅿️
- Explore Old City
- [Cow's Ice Cream](https://cows.ca) 
- [Chocolate Flavoris](https://www.chocolatsfavoris.com/en) dipped cones. 
- 4:00 PM: Check into [Airbnb in Quebec](https://www.airbnb.com/rooms/46939647). Lovely place!
- Relax, get situated in Airbnb. Mazie, Tyler, and Tammy explore on foot a bit.
- Tammy and I have a wonderful anniversary dinner at [Le Clan](https://restaurantleclan.com/en/). Four course tasting menu with many surprises added.
- Kids have dinner at [Le Chic Shack](https://lechicshack.ca/en/) on their own and even [make a POAP](https://poap.gallery/drops/193214) to commemorate it (which makes this geeky Dad super proud). 
- Mazie explores Quebec a bit while Tyler does some Geometry.
- Everyone reconvenes at Airbnb to wrap the night.
