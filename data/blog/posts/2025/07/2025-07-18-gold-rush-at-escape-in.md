We solved the Gold Rush room at [Escape in Time](https://www.escapeintimemadison.com) in Madison today. The room is rated **hard** and we definitely hit a couple of spots where we needed a clue and felt pretty stumped. But even with that, we solved the room with 13m 15s remaining on the clock. We thought the location was great and loved that the entrance is a Candy Shop puzzle room that you need to get through to check in for your game! [Room 73](https://www.thingelstad.com/lists/escape-rooms/)!
