_Also Olson Family Vacation 2025 Day 2._

- Max and I walk to [Structure Coffee](https://structurecoffeeroasters.com) for a delicious Cortado and light roast to start the day.
- Tammy, Tyler, and Levi join us and walk to [Olive+Gourmando](https://oliveetgourmando.com/en) for a pastry.
- We all head to [Montreal on Wheels](https://caroulemontreal.com/en) to rent bikes and take a private guided bike tour of Montreal.
- After bike tour walk to [Papermane Tea Room](https://paparmanemtl.ca/en/) for afternoon tea and lunch.
- Then Max and I take four of the boys and visit two card shops: Capitaine Québec and [Silver Goblin](https://silvergoblin.cards).
- Jamie has call with [Quartersawn](https://quartersawndesignbuild.com) with some updates on construction happening at home. Some surprises to deal with. 💸
- Return and park Max's car in Place Victoria ramp.
- Freshen up at Sonder.
- Jamie and brother-in-laws head to [Vieux-Port Steakhouse](https://vieuxportsteakhouse.com/en/) for Men's dinner. Great table outside with two wonderful bottles of Borolo to add.
- Go to Old Port for evening fun. A number of folks get Henna tattoos. Everyone has Mary's Popcorn. Mazie, Tyler, Nora, Levi, and Angie do zipline!
- Watch Taylor Swift themed fireworks in Montreal.
- Walk back to Sondor and call it a night.
