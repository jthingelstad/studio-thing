_Also Olson Family Vacation 2025 Day 1._

- Up early to pack up, get car, and unload out of Airbnb. Delightful time in Quebec. 
- Drive to Laval, suburb of Montreal. 
- Go to [Escaparium](https://www.escaparium.ca) in Laval.
- Did the **The Lost Island of the Voodoo Queen** room — amazing!
- Went to [Birdhouse Wings](https://birdhousewings.com/en/restaurant/laval) for lunch.
- Returned to Escaparium and completed **Wardrobe for Sale**. Was incredible as well. Had us talking for a while after.
- Drove into Montreal.
- Parked at Victoria Place.
- 5:12 PM: Official beginning of [Olson Family Vacation 2025](https://poap.gallery/drops/193101)!
- Walk to Notre Dame Basilica for Aura Lightshow.
- Walk to Bevo Pizza for great pizza, beers, and pasta.
- Family pictures after pizza.
- Kaye and Fernandez's return to Sonder.
- Us, Lundeen's, and Daily's have an ice cream and walk down to the [Grande roue de Montréal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grande_roue_de_Montréal). 
- Check out the waterfront.
- Casual walk back to Sonder.
- We return to Victoria Place parking to get our luggage but find the elevator doesn't go down. After a while find staircases to get to the parking levels and find the car without too much difficulty. Retrieve luggage.
- Return to Sonder, respective rooms, turn in for the night.
