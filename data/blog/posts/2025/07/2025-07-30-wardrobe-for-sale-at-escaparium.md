After our morning defeating the Voodoo Queen we had a break for lunch and then returned to Escaparium. We were guided through a number of hallways until we came upon a wardrobe sitting in the hall. We were left and upon inspection the wardrobe transported us to another world where we made many new friends and were guided by Moss along the way. The room had many hidden secrets to find and at one point we even ventured down a river on a raft to a hidden cave where we ultimately saved Moss and all of her friends before coming back to this world through another wardrobe.

This was a two hour room and was an incredible story.
