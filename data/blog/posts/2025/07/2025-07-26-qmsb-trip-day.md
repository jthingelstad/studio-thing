- Up early for final trip prep. 
- Ride to the airport with Burhan at 7:05 am. 
- Check in for flight. Baggage weights: Tammy 45 lbs, Jamie 38 lbs, Mazie 34 lbs, and Tyler 30 lbs.
- Delta offering $1,200 a person to take a different flight. 😬 Boarding stopped due to oversold flight. 
- Flight delayed and delayed waiting for three people willing to give up their seat.
- Finally got three volunteers. Went up to $1,400 and took an hour. We considered it but passed. 
- Depart DL 4901. 🛫
- Arrive Burlington, VT. 
- Tyler gets a crêpe from [Skinny Pancake](https://skinnypancake.com). 
- Pickup Ford Escape from Enterprise. 
- Check in at [Hotel Vermont](https://hotelvt.com).
- Late lunch at [Rogue Rabbit](https://www.roguerabbitvt.com).
- Changed afternoon plans to do bike ride tomorrow morning and take in Block Party on Church Street celebrating [Outdoor Gear Exchanges 30th anniversary](https://www.gearx.com/blog/oge-30th-anniversary-block-party). OGE is very much like Midwest Mountaineering was in Minneapolis. 
- Explore Church Street Marketplace: [Phoenix Books](https://www.phoenixbooks.biz), [BCA Center](https://www.burlingtoncityarts.org/about-bca), FP Movement, [Garcias Tobacco Shop](https://garciastobaccoshop.com), Harbour Thread, Helly Hansen, Lake Champlain Chocolate, [Vermont Eclectic Company](https://vteclecticco.com), [Outdoor Gear Exchange](https://www.gearx.com), [Crow Books](https://www.crowbooks.com). 
- Dinner at [Veritá Pizzeria](https://www.pizzeriaverita.com). 
- Walk along Lake Champlain at sunset.
- Return to Hotel Vermont.
