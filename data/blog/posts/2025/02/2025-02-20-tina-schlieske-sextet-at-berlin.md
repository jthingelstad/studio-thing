We made our very first visit to [Berlin](https://www.berlinmpls.com) tonight to see the [Tina Schlieske Sextet](https://www.berlinmpls.com/calendar/tina-schlieske-sextet) play. On stage was [Tina Schlieske](https://tinaschlieske.com) (vocals), [Cody McKinney](https://codymckinney.bandcamp.com) (bass), [Pete Hennig](https://www.shiftingparadigmrecords.com/artist/pete-hennig/) (drums), [Bryan Nichols](https://www.bryannichols.org) (piano), [Zacc Harris](https://zaccharris.com) (guitar), and [Brandon Wozniak](http://www.brandonwozniakmusic.com) (saxophone).

Tina has been dipping into jazz and doing jazz focused shows and this show was all jazz in a great jazz venue. They did two one hour sets with a break in the middle. It was all great with the highlight for me being when they did some [Frank Sinatra](https://www.sinatra.com) songs. Tina really nails those songs with tons of energy. Her other favorites are [Nina Simone](https://www.ninasimone.com) and [Billie Holiday](https://billieholiday.com).

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2025/img-9534.jpeg" width="600" height="337" alt="Jazz band performs on a warmly lit stage with red curtains, featuring a vocalist at a microphone, saxophonist, bassist, pianist, and drummer">

I think they hit every song on the recently released album "[The Good Life](https://shiftingparadigmrecords.bandcamp.com/album/the-good-life)". If you like jazz you should give it a listen.
