I love the scent and crackle of our [Things 4 Good](https://https://www.thingelstad.com/candles/) candles. For my birthday my sister got me a [candle warmer](https://www.amazon.com/Candle-Warmer-Lamp-Timer-Adjustable/dp/B0DLWYQ5PM/). I didn't know that this was a thing and gave it a go. It worked really well! The wax performed very similar to burning and the scent was great. Another way to enjoy our candles.
