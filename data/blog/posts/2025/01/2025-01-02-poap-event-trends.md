It has become a tradition for me to create a [POAP](https://poap.xyz) for my birthday. I've been doing it since my [50th Birthday](https://poap.gallery/drops/20896) and now have [51st](https://poap.gallery/drops/95194), [52nd](https://poap.gallery/drops/166479), and now my [53rd](https://poap.gallery/drops/183305). Since POAP uses a simple sequential number for events I can easily see how many POAP events have been created for those years.

| Year (Approx) | Events |
|---------------|--------|
| 2022          | 74,298 |
| 2023          | 71,285 |
| 2024          | 16,826 |

That is a big drop in 2024, however it is still **46 events a day**. I love what POAP is doing and they are slowly getting their whole offering easier to use and more streamlined. My guess is that they are not actively pushing to create more events right now while they improve the product. I’m rooting for them to be massively successful. I think POAP could be one of the products that shows the path for blockchain adoption.
