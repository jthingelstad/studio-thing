I've used [Sanebox](https://www.sanebox.com/) for a decade to defer emails, but now I'm trying [Fastmail](https://www.fastmail.com/) and I'm realizing how nice it is to be able to individually specify a defer time per email instead of just having two or three options as folders. Downside is I can only defer in the Fastmail clients.
