This is a big deal.

> **America's** Food and Drug Administration approved a daily pill version of **Wegovy**, a weight-loss treatment manufactured by **Novo Nordisk** that is usually injected. Eli Lilly, a rival drugmaker, is expected to receive regulatory approval for a similar pill soon. Pills--which are less effective than jabs, but far more convenient--mark [a new frontier](https://www.economist.com/the-world-ahead/2025/11/10/a-second-helping-of-weight-loss-drugs-is-coming) in weight-loss drugs. — The Economist, World in Brief, Dec 23, 2025
