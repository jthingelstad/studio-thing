For those that believe that government should be run more like a company — having five different leaders in the same role in a year is a sign of complete chaos and dysfunction in any company.

> Mr Trump will reportedly replace the acting boss of the **Internal Revenue Service** after **Scott Bessent**, the treasury secretary, complained to the president. Gary Shapley, who was appointed just three days ago, was apparently installed [at the behest](https://www.economist.com/united-states/2025/03/27/elon-musk-is-powersliding-through-the-federal-government) of **Elon Musk**, even though the IRS commissioner reports to Mr Bessent. Mr Shapley's replacement will be the <mark>fifth to hold the job this year</mark>. — The Economist, The World in Brief, April 19 2025
