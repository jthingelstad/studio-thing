"Blogging is small-p political again, today. It's come back round. It's a statement to put your words in a place where they are not subject to someone else's algorithm telling you what success looks like; when you blog, your words are not a vote for the values of someone else's platform." — [Matt Webb](https://manuelmoreale.com/pb-matt-webb), Interview for [People and Blogs](https://peopleandblogs.com), as [quoted by Simon Willison](https://simonwillison.net/2025/Apr/5/matt-webb/).
