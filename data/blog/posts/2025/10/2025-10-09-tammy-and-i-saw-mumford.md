Tammy and I saw [Mumford & Sons](https://www.mumfordandsons.com) for the second time tonight and for the second time they were incredible live. We like their music but there is something about the energy they bring on stage that is so powerful. The setlist was great. I loved the aesthetic and how they had it all setup. 🎶
