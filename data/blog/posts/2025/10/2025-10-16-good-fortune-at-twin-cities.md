Tammy and I have went to the [Twin Cities Film Fest](https://twincitiesfilmfest.org) showing of [Good Fortune](https://www.goodfortune.movie) tonight at the [Edina 4](https://manntheatres.com/theatre/89/Edina-4) theatre. It was our first time at a Film Fest event and we got to be all official and vote on the movie. Also, the Edina 4 theatre is just a great place to see a movie in general, but an even better place to see a movie during the Film Fest with tons of dining options nearby to make it a great evening.

The movie itself brings tons of star power with [Keanu Reeves](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Keanu_Reeves), [Aziz Ansari](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aziz_Ansari), [Seth Rogan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seth_Rogen), [Keke Palmer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Keke_Palmer), and [Sandra Oh](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sandra_Oh), with Ansari writing and directing it.

The movie tells the story of Arj who is struggling to get by and living out of his car and Jeff who is wealthy from tech investing and has whatever he wants. Their lives get switched by Gabriel the Angel and they get to experience each others reality.

We both gave it ★★★★ and enjoyed the quirky and funny takes while telling a meaningful story.
