_Also Olson Family Vacation 2025 Day 3._

- I go to [Structure Coffee](https://structurecoffeeroasters.com) for some delicious coffee and time to catch up on blog. Tammy off on a morning run.
- Max and Nora join for some coffee at Structure.
- Mazie, Tammy, and I go to bakery.
- Tyler meets up with us and runs off to get crepes.
- Super fun time on [Jet Boating Montreal](https://jetboatingmontreal.com)!
- Return to room to put on dry clothes. 
- Difficult driving to bagel making class.
- Fun time learning to make Montreal style bagels at [The Bagel Class](https://www.thebagelclass.com).
- Levi joins our family and drive to lookout on Mount Royal.
- Mazie shares that Montreal got its name from "Mount Royal". Say it quickly together.
- Return to downtown and park car.
- Get ready for Cirque du Soleil performance.
- Salad and smoothies for dinner at Mandy's.
- Amazing time at [Cirque du Soleil: Luzia](https://www.cirquedusoleil.com/luzia) under the big top.
- Walk back to Sonder.
