We got to see [Ruthie Foster](http://www.ruthiefoster.com) play at the [Dakota](https://www.dakotacooks.com) tonight. She did a solo set — just her and her guitar named Pearl. We hadn't seen Foster play before but my Mom is a big fan of hers and I can see why. Her songs were amazing and she added so much with the stories behind them as well. I could have kept listening for another hour easily. What a great musician. 🎶
