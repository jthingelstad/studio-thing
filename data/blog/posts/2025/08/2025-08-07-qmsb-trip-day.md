- 12:53 PM: Start drive to Alexandria, MN. 23 minutes late. 
- [Up North Ice Cream](https://explorealex.com/listing/up-north-ice-cream-company/) for some delicious house made ice cream. 
- [Escape Room Alexandria](https://www.escapealexandria.com) to do the Mad Scientist room. 
- Talked to owner of escape room and shared as well as received a bunch of recommendations. 
- Drove to Fergus Falls. 
- Check-in to [Mill Boutique Hotel](https://fergusmillhotel.com) which has just opened. We are one of the first guests. 
- Dinner at [Union Avenue](https://www.unionavenueff.com). Really impressive meals. The pasta was exceptional.
- Walk along the Otter Tail River. 
- Return to hotel for the night.
