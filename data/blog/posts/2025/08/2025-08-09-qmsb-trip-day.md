✅ Quebec ✅ Montreal ✅ Stowe ✅ Bismarck

- Tammy and Mazie go on independent runs around Downtown Fargo. 
- Jamie goes to [Youngblood Coffee Roasters](https://youngbloodcoffee.com) for a coffee.
- Check out of [Jasper Hotel](https://jasperfargo.com). 
- Breakfast at [Prairie Kitchen](https://701eateries.com/prairie-kitchen). 
- Drive to Bismarck, ND. 
- Stop for lunch at [Fireflour](https://www.fireflourpizza.com). 
- Explore downtown Bismarck: [Anima Cucina](https://www.animacucina.com), [The Capital Gallery](https://thecapitalgallery.com), and [Brick Oven Bakery](https://brickovenbakerynd.com). 
- Escape Room at [Breakout ND](https://www.breakoutnd.com). 
- [Amanda & Reid](https://www.theknot.com/us/amanda-undhjem-and-reid-arnold-aug-2025) wedding reception at [Baymont in Mandan](https://baymontmandan.com) and see a lot of my relatives! ￼￼🥳
