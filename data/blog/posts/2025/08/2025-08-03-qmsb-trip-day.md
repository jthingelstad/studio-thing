_Also Olson Family Vacation 2025 Day 5._

- Max and I drive and get six cups of really terrible coffee.
- Put the Hungarian Grand Prix on my laptop and watch with some others while Ferrari goes from leading the race to not even making it on the podium.
- The Grand Prix viewers leave the Airbnb and drive to Stowe Farmer's Market for delightful food.
- Farmer's Market Highlights: Incredible Blueberries, amazing baked goods from Two Son's bakery, delicious brisket from [Black Diamond Barbecue](https://www.blackdiamondbarbeque.com), great bike blended smoothie, popcorn to go for the evening.
- Drive to [Stowe Bee Bakery](https://www.stowebeebakery.com) and get a delicious Iced Maple Latte.
- Head back to Airbnb for afternoon in the pool.
- Go to Stowe for some shopping and exploring.
- Return to Airbnb and collect everyone to head to Ben & Jerry's Experience.
- Go to [Doc Ponds](https://www.docponds.com) for dinner.
- Return to Airbnb for the rest of the evening.
