_Also Olson Family Vacation 2025 Day 6._

- Drive to Burlington for bike ride. 
- Morning snacks at [August First](https://augustfirstvt.com). 
- Rent bikes at [Local Motion](https://www.localmotion.org) and [ride Island Line Trail, take Bike Ferry, and go South Hero](https://www.thingelstad.com/2025/08/04/we-all-rented-bikes-at.html). 
- Get too much chocolate at [NU Chocolat](https://nuchocolat.com). 
- Drive back to Airbnb. 
- Afternoon relax in the pool. 
- Dad's take the kids to dinner. First place is closed so fallback to [Doc Ponds](https://www.docponds.com). Women are out for dinner [The Bistro at Ten Acres](https://thebistroattenacres.com). 
- Stop for Maple Creemees at [Red Barn Ice Cream](https://www.redbarnicecreamshop.com). 
- Return to Airbnb for the night.
