- Tammy and Mazie go for a morning walk around Fergus Falls. 
- Breakfast at Toast Cafe: Swedish Pancakes, Frikkadiller Toast (Swedish Meatballs), French Toast, Avocado Toast, and Fluffernutter to share.
- Drive to [Prairie Wetlands Learning Center](https://www.fws.gov/refuge/fergus-falls-wetland-management-district). 
- Walk trail at Prairie Wetlands. 
- Drive back to Fergus Falls downtown. 
- Explore stores: Fergus Apothecary, Biffley's & Dragon's Den, Wildflour Bakery, [Dahlia's](https://dahliasdowntownboutique.com), [Kaddatz Galleries](https://www.kaddatzgalleries.org), Dutchmen Organics, and The Bric. 
- Drive to Fargo. 
- 2:31 PM Enter North Dakota.
- Stop at [Culver's](https://www.culvers.com) for chicken tenders. 
- Time Machine room at [Puzzled Escape Rooms](https://puzzledescaperooms.com).
- Walk around downtown Fargo.  Jamie gets a delicious coffee drink at [Youngblood Coffee Roasters](https://youngbloodcoffee.com/). Tammy and Mazie visit [The Nines](https://shopthenines.com) and [Proper](https://www.proper-shops.com) while Tyler and Jamie go to [Paradox Comics-n-Cards](https://paradoxcnc.com/) and [buy a couple of Pokémon packs](https://www.thingelstad.com/2025/08/08/tyler-and-i-stopped-by.html).  
- Jamie and Tyler get Macrons at [Bakehouse 23](https://www.bakehouse23.com).
- Dinner at [Beer & Fish Company](https://beerandfishcompany.com). Mazie’s favorite dessert.
- We stop at [Ferguson Books](https://www.fergusonbooks.com) after dinner and pick up "[My Friends](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/217163697-my-friends)" by Fredrik Backman.  Mazie realizes how expensive hard cover books are when you don’t buy them on amazon.  
- Fill up water bottles at Jasper Hotel.
- Jamie and Tyler get Rolled Ice Ice cream at [Tea + Crepe](https://teaandcrepe.com).
- Stop at [Silver Lining Creamery](https://www.silverliningcreamery.com) on the way back to the hotel. Tammy gets salted caramel and Jamie gets German Chocolate Cake ice cream to share.
- Really enjoyable elevator ride and feet up in hotel room. :D
