I used GPT-5 to create [introduction text](https://www.manton.org/2025/08/11/category-improvements.html) for my blog categories. Now I just need [Tiny Theme](https://tiny.micro.blog) to pull them in and I’ll be set. 

This is the prompt I used:

<div class="chat">
<div class="message right">

I'm including a list of all categories on my blog along with the link to the archive page for that category. I would like you to visit each one of these links and use the sample of blog posts to create a short one to three sentence introduction for each category. Don't get overly specific and make sure to keep the introductions in a similar writing style to the content on my blog.

- Escape Room: [www.thingelstad.com/categorie...](https://www.thingelstad.com/categories/escape-room/)
- Coffee: [www.thingelstad.com/categorie...](https://www.thingelstad.com/categories/coffee/)
- Videos: [www.thingelstad.com/categorie...](https://www.thingelstad.com/categories/videos/)
- Crypto: [www.thingelstad.com/categorie...](https://www.thingelstad.com/categories/crypto/)
- _list truncated_

</div>
</div>

Passing the explicit list of categories and links worked much faster than the first prompt which I asked it to just look at the Archive page and figure out what to do.
