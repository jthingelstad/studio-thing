We all rented bikes at [Local Motion]([Island Line Trail](https://www.localmotion.org/) in Burlington and rode the [Island Line Trail](https://www.localmotion.org/island_line_trail_map) over the Colchester Causeway. We took the [Bike Ferry](https://www.localmotion.org/bike_ferry) across "the gap" and finally turned around at [Allenholm Farms](https://www.localmotion.org/allenholm_farm) on South Hero Island and made our way back.
