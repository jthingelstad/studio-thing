Mazie wanted to do her first triathlon on her birthday and Tammy signed up to do it with her! They were out of the house before 6am in the cold to get ready for the [Rocket Racing Women's Trifecta](https://www.rocketracingmn.com/womens-trifecta) in Shoreview. They did great — see [Tammy](https://www.athlinks.com/event/387440/results/Event/1113968/Course/2608057/Bib/133) and [Mazie](https://www.athlinks.com/event/387440/results/Event/1113968/Course/2608058/Bib/161) results. Tammy missed a turn and ran longer than needed.
