Tyler and I got the Pokémon cards we sent to [TAG Grading](https://taggrading.com) back today. [My Galarian Perrserker V](https://my.taggrading.com/card/f1255466) came back an 8.5. I dig the clear slabs that they use. The grading reports are insanely detailed and driven by algorithm and computer vision. It seems much more accurate than [PSA](https://www.psacard.com), but also much harder.
