At the [Stone Arch Bridge Festival](https://stonearchbridgefestival.com) this weekend these clear candles by [Lake Escape Candle Co](http://lakeescapecandleco.com) caught my eye. I hadn't seen anything like it. They are made with a gel wax and they did a brilliant job using that in the design. The next day I lit one and was wowed by the way the light lit it up. Lovely!
