Tyler got [Finding Winnie](http://www.amazon.com/Finding-Winnie-Story-Worlds-Famous/dp/0316324906/) for Christmas this year and it's a really awesome book. If your kid has any affinity to Winnie-the-Pooh they will love this book. Even if they don't, it's still a great book. Really well done and one of the better books for a bedtime story. Recommended!
