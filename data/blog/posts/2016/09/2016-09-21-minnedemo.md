### Civic Eagle

### Evereve

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/cdbbb04afe.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="Presenter on stage demonstrating a clothing stylist app projected on a large screen, with two other people watching nearby." />

Stylist application to allow people to create a profile and allow a stylist to custom pick wardrobes. Minnesota based retailer. Allow stylist to see purchases and wishlist items to further refine recommendations. Interesting idea. 

### Marbles / Live Beyond the Label

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/01ded58cae.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="Presenter at a podium with a laptop speaks to an audience in a darkened venue, with a large projected slide showing the Marbles 2.0 mental health app website behind them." />
