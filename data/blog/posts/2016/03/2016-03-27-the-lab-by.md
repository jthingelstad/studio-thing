I was looking for a coffee fix and Tammy did some searching and found [The Lab by Alchemy Coffee](http://www.alchemycoffeerva.com). We drove here and the place looked pretty solid. Great ambiance and they seemed to have their act together. I got a macchiato as well as a pour-over to go. The macchiato presented great, but was a bit thin. They need their espresso dialed in a bit better. The pour over was really good however the roast was a little lighter than I would typically go.
