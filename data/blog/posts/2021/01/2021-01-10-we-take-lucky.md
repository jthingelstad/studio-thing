We take Lucky on a walk nearly every day and usually go through [Pamela Park](https://www.edinamn.gov/Facilities/Facility/Details/Pamela-Park-24) for a 1.2 to 1.6 mile loop. With the pandemic the ice rink and ponds have been very busy with lots of skaters. It makes the walk more fun to see people out and having fun. ⛸🏒
