Starting in on my Christmas LEGO project: **[World Map](https://www.lego.com/en-us/product/world-map-31203)**! Item 31203, **11,695 pieces**. Unlike any other LEGO project I’ve ever done. 🤩

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/2e2d8c2091.jpg" width="600" height="600" alt="LEGO World Map set box propped on a wooden table surrounded by black base plates and dozens of sorted bags of colorful 1x1 round tiles" />

Painting with LEGO?

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/8c8238cde7.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="Ten white bowls filled with small colorful LEGO round flat tiles sorted by color including orange, tan, coral, green, white, teal, navy, bright green, yellow-orange, and cyan" />

World Map LEGO is a giant pixel art project. Each tile is 16 x 16, 256 pixels each. 

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/0af8d33299.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="Three white bowls of tan, dark navy, and teal 1x1 LEGO round tiles above an instruction booklet and a partially completed black baseplate with teal, green, and yellow tiles placed on it." />

Completed first tile of LEGO World Map. Thirty nine tiles to go. 10,240 pixels in total. 😮
