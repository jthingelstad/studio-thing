> **9 short rules for a better life:**
> 
> 1. Wake up early
> 2. Focus on effort, not results
> 3. Read every day
> 4. Strict with yourself, forgiving of others
> 5. Seek out challenges
> 6. Stay a student
> 7. Cut toxic people out of your life
> 8. Think about death
> 9. Focus on what you can control
