My friend and amazing photographer [Layne Kennedy](http://laynekennedy.com) put together this short video of life on a Central Minnesota Farm. I love how he captures it all so well. I’ve been lucky enough to take classes with Layne and his talent is amazing. 

> I had the fortunate opportunity to visit a neighbor's family farm several times this summer and document some of the activities there. Really, it was just done for fun. This was not meant as a documentary, but something for the family to share life on the farm with extended family. The little things like harvesting sweet corn to donate to the local church, protecting lands for wildlife habitat and wildflowers, gathering everyone for meals during a Vikings game, and the traditions of deer hunting and gathering of friends all part of farm life here.

It is a wonderful video.
