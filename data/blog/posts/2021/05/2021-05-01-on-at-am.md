On 4/30 at 11:29am I got an email from [OpenSea](https://opensea.io) officially announcing that they are adding support for [Polygon](https://polygon.technology) (MATIC). Polygon is a network built on top of [Ethereum](https://ethereum.org/en/). Interoperability to move NFTs in and out of Polygon into the main blockchain is a key feature for OpenSea.

> Support for other blockchains (Polygon (formerly Matic) trading coming soon!)

Look at the trading activity for Polygon (MATIC). it is pretty obvious when the news leaked to the market. 😏
