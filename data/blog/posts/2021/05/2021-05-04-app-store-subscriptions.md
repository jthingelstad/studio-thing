I did an audit of my App Store subscriptions recently. This seems like a good process to do on an annual basis.

My **annual subscriptions** totaled <mark>**$309.06**</mark> (including taxes).  This includes subscriptions to 16 services including: [1Blocker](https://1blocker.com)\*, [Albums](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/albums-album-focused-player/id1469948986)\*, [Darkroom](https://darkroom.co)\*, [Day One](https://dayoneapp.com), [Drafts](https://getdrafts.com)\*, [iTunes Match](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT204146), [mPaceline](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/mpaceline/id1202554303), [NoMoRoBo](https://nomorobo.com) (multi), [Overcast](https://overcast.fm/), [RadarScope](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/radarscope/id288419283), [Tweetbot](https://tapbots.com/tweetbot/)\*, [Unread](https://www.goldenhillsoftware.com/unread/)\*, [Weatherline](https://weatherlineapp.com), and [YouAte](https://youate.com). _\* denotes family subscriptions._

I also have five **monthly subscriptions** for <mark>**$79.55**</mark> (including taxes). This includes [JAXJOX](https://jaxjox.coma), [Disney+](https://www.disneyplus.com), [HBO Max](https://www.hbomax.com), [Paramount+](https://www.paramountplus.com), and [Apple One](https://www.apple.com/apple-one/) Family Premium. Those add up to $954.60 a year.

So how much are App Store subscriptions costing me a year? <mark>**$1,263.66**</mark>. They all add up! 💸 

I did cancel a handful of subscriptions. These are all subscriptions that I use so I’m getting value from them.
