_Weather: Still a lot of warmth. Morning was very windy. Sunny and warm in afternoon. Cooled down at night._

- Depart [Wild Pines Cabin](https://www.airbnb.com/rooms/46332707). 
- Drive to [Grand Marais](https://www.ci.grand-marais.mn.us)
- Donuts at the [Worlds Best Donuts](https://www.worldsbestdonutsmn.com). 
- Walk the pier to lighthouse.
- Hike around [Artist Point](https://superiorhiking.com/artists-point/).
- Played with rocks at the shore.
- Lunch at [Voyageur Brewery](http://www.voyageurbrewing.com).

- Bought painting from [Douglas Ross](https://douglasrossstudio.com)

- Back to [Wild Pines Cabin](https://www.airbnb.com/rooms/46332707). 
- Ordered pizza delivery from [Jimmy's Pizza](https://jimmyspizza.com/silverbay/store/) in Silver Bay. 
- Campfire and S'mores.
