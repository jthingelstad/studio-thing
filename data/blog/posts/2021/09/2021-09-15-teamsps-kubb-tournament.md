TeamSPS had a great time playing Kubb today, connecting with colleagues, and having a fun time. We weren't able to have this event in 2020 due to COVID-19, but thanks to vaccines we were able to throw some batons together! Several people commented how much they loved this event. Here are some selected photos from [Tim Van Cleave](https://www.linkedin.com/in/timothy-van-cleave/) who was so generous to volunteer as official photographer! He has an [entire album of additional shots](https://gallery.fiddlingphotographer.com/2021spskubbtournament-highlights/) as well.

You can also see the official [SPS Tech Blog post](https://tech.spscommerce.com/2021/09/16/kubb-tournament.html) on the Kubb event, and looks back to our [2019](https://www.thingelstad.com/2019/08/22/teamsps-kubb-tournament.html) and  [2018](https://www.thingelstad.com/2018/09/27/sps-kubb-tournament.html) events. This event got real momentum after we did [Kubb at Tech Jam in 2016](https://www.thingelstad.com/2016/06/04/sumologic-sumosighting-on.html). The first introduction was a [Kubb Class in 2014](https://www.thingelstad.com/2014/10/26/sps-kubb-class.html) that was part of our SPS Charity Auction. Kubb has some deep roots with Team SPS!
