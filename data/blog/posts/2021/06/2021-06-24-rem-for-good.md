[REM5 for Good](https://www.rem5forgood.com) ran a digital art event, [Simulacra Two](https://www.rem5forgood.com/simulacratwo.html), and in addition to other options they offered NFTs of a number of items.

I missed the real event but found out about it before it was all done and picked up [four NFTs](https://www.hicetnunc.xyz/tz/tz1ScjmJyVpN1ph4gGWgbbkWsTRykzAmiNtV/collection).

They used [Tezos](https://tezos.com) for this and minted and sold the NFTs on [H=N](https://www.hicetnunc.xyz). I wasn't setup for that at all so I bought some XTZ on Coinbase, setup a walled on [Kukai](https://wallet.kukai.app), and was then able to [grab these](https://tzkt.io/tz1ScjmJyVpN1ph4gGWgbbkWsTRykzAmiNtV/operations/).
