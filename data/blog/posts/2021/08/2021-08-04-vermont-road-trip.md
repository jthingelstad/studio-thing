Weather: Great weather. Cool morning. Clear for our entire drive. Couple drops of rain on the sail. Cool in evening.

- 7:00a: Clean up [Modern Accord Depot](https://www.modernaccorddepot.com) and get car loaded
- 8:07a: Depart for Woodstock, NY. 
- Light breakfast at [Bread Alone Bakery](https://www.breadalone.com/woodstock) in Woodstock. 
- Short [hike in Overlook Mountain Wild Forest](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/08/04/we-had-some.html).
- [Visited  Karma Triyana Dharmachakra Buddhist Monastery](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/08/04/we-visited-the.html). 
- Walked around downtown Woodstock and checked out shops. 
- Delicious lunch at [Shindig](http://www.woodstockshindig.com). 
- Left for Kingston. 
- 2:00p: [Sailing in Hudson River](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/08/04/we-took-an.html). 
- 5:15p: Depart for Greenbush, NY. 
- 6:21p: Dinner at [Nic's Trattoria](https://www.nicstrattoria.com). 
- 7:30p: Depart for Shaftesbury, VT. 
- 8:14p: Enter Vermont. 
- 8:30p: Arrived at [Vermont Schoolhouse Farm Cottage](https://www.airbnb.com/rooms/19227471).
