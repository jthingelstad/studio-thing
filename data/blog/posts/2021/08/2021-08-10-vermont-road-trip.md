Weather: Good weather, pretty hot. Started raining after we got settled in Rochester. Hot and humid in the evening.

- 7:45a: Depart Treehouse. 
- 8:00a: [Dunkin' Donuts](https://www.dunkindonuts.com/en/locations?location=05819)! 🍩
- Drive to Rochester, NY.
- 10:31a: Enter New York. Goodbye Vermont. 👋
- 11:45a: Lunch at [DomAdi's Deli](http://www.domadisdeli.com) in Amsterdam, NY. 
- 3:40: Arrive in Rochester, NY. Get settled in [Downtown Rochester Retreat](https://www.airbnb.com/rooms/28578397). 
- Walk to [Vern's](https://www.iloveverns.com) for an amazing Italian dinner and drinks outside.
