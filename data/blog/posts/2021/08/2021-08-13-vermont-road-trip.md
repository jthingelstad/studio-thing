Weather: Perfect day!

- Enjoyed a relaxing morning on the [Floathaus](http://floathausofsaugatuck.com).
- Amazing breakfast at [Pennyroyal Cafe](https://www.pennyroyalprovisions.com).
- Check out the art at [Roan & Black](https://roanandblack.com).
- [Iced coffee](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/08/13/afternoon-craft-coffee.html) at [Uncommon Roasters](https://uncommoncoffeeroasters.com). 
- Walk around downtown Saugatuck: [Teeny Tiny Toy Store](https://www.saugatuck.com/business-listings/teeny-tiny-toy-store/), [Glik's](https://www.gliks.com), [Landshark](https://www.shoptheshark.com/en/), [it is what it is](http://www.shopwhatitis.com/), [Grins of Saugatuck](https://www.saugatuck.com/business-listings/grins-of-saugatuck/), [Jeff Blandford Gallery](http://www.jeffblandford.com), [Sea Bags of Maine](https://seabags.com), [Wine Sellers of Saugutuck](https://winesellersofsaugatuck.com), [Amazwi Contemporary Art](https://amazwionline.com), [Kindel and Company](https://www.kindelco.com), [Urban Found](https://www.urbanfound.net), [Good Goods](https://goodgoods.com). 
- [Kilwins Ice Cream](https://www.kilwins.com). 
- [Saugatuck Dune Rides Over the Years](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/08/13/saugatuck-dune-rides.html)! 🤩
- Hang out and have fun at [Oval Beach](https://www.saugatuckcity.com/oval-beach.html). The [waves were great](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/08/13/one-minute-at.html).
- Return to [Pennyroyal Cafe](https://www.pennyroyalprovisions.com) for a delicious dinner and incredible desserts.
