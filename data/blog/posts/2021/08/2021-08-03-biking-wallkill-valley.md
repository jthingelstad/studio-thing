This morning we rented bikes from [New Paltz Biking](https://npbiking.com) and took a 16.2 miles round-trip ride on the [Wallkill Valley Rail Trail](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Attraction_Review-g48245-d4702448-Reviews-The_Wallkill_Valley_Rail_Trail-New_Paltz_Catskill_Region_New_York.html). It was a wonderful ride and the Trellis Bridge 160 feet above the Hudson River provided immense views. We turned around at the Ice Caves that felt like you were standing in front of a giant air conditioner.
