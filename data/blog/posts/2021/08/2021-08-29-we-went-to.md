We went to the [Minnesota State Fair](https://www.mnstatefair.org) today. My brother and his family were in town and we decided to go _early_ so that we could avoid as much of any crowd as possible. We started at 8am and left around noon. It was fun to do some of our normal traditions, and there weren't that many people at that time.
