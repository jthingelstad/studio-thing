Weather: Perfect.

- Breakfast at [Kerhonkson Diner](https://www.yelp.com/biz/kerhonkson-diner-kerhonkson) in [Kerhonkson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kerhonkson,_New_York).
- Drive to Bethel, about an hour.
- Woodstock Museum and Historical Site at [Bethel Woods Center for the Arts](https://www.bethelwoodscenter.org).
- Drive back to Accord, and found [a Road Sign Math winner](https://www.roadsignmath.xyz/wurtsboro) on the way.
- Dinner at [The Egg's Nest](https://www.theeggsnest.com) in High Falls.
