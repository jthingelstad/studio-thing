One of the things we wanted to do in Rochester was to return to [Good Luck](https://restaurantgoodluck.com) to share a **Good Luck Burger**. "1 lb. house-ground, grass-fed local beef on brioche with cuba cheddar, vegetable slaw & fries." You split this four ways. We had to return here after our [visit in 2018](https://www.thingelstad.com/2018/08/10/013052.html), 3 years and 1 day ago.
