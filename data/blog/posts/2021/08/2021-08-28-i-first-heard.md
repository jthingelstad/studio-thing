I first heard of Rivian via [The Long Way Up](https://www.longwayup.com). Their trucks look pretty amazing. The valuation seems crazy high, but this might be a good investment still. 

> **Rivian**, a California-based [electric-vehicle startup](https://www.economist.com/the-world-ahead/2020/11/17/the-battle-within-the-electric-vehicle-industry-will-intensify), filed confidentially for a public listing, seeking a valuation between $70bn and $80bn. Among its planned offerings are a sport-utility vehicle and a pick-up truck. Like Tesla, it wants to bypass dealerships and sell directly to customers. Deliveries were meant to start in June but have been delayed due to the pandemic. — _[Economist Espresso](https://www.economist.com/espresso), Saturday, August 28._
