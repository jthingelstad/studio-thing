Weather: Perfect.

- Breakfast pastry stop at [Saunderskill Farms](https://saunderskill.com).
- Rented bikes in New Paltz and [ride the Wallkill Valley Rail Trail](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/08/03/biking-wallkill-valley.html).
- [Lunch at Lola's](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/08/03/tyler-got-the.html) in New Paltz.
- [Tour of Historic Huguenot Street](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/08/03/historic-huguenot-street.html).
- [Ice cream at Sleeping Giant](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/08/03/ice-cream-at.html) in Kerhonkson.
- Return to [Modern Accord Depot](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/08/03/we-have-had.html) to enjoy our final night here.
