We visited the [Van Gogh Experience](https://vangoghmsp.com) and we all enjoyed it. It is an immersive experience built on Van Gogh's artwork. I found it very captivating and engaging. My only complaint was the lack of seating. It was a long time to sit on a cement floor. I might consider going again and watching it a couple of times.
