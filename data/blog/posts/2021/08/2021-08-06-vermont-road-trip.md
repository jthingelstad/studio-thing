Weather: Perfect.

- Drive to Manchester, VT. 🚙
- Coffee at [Charlie's Coffee House](https://www.manchestervermont.com/directory/charlies-coffee-house/). Better coffee elsewhere. ☕️
- Breakfast at [Little Rooster](https://www.tripadvisor.com/Restaurant_Review-g57312-d517987-Reviews-The_Little_Rooster_Cafe-Manchester_Vermont.html). 🍽
- Alpine Slide at [Bromley Mountain](https://www.bromley.com). We all did it, first time for everyone but Tammy. 🤪
- Hiked a bit of the [Appalachian Trail in Vermont](https://appalachiantrail.org/explore/explore-by-state/vermont/), a mile up Bromley Mountain and then back. 🥾
- Iced Maple Latte at [Bonnet & Main](https://www.bonnetandmain.com). 🥤
- Shop at [Northshire Bookstore](http://www.northshire.com). Great bookstore! 📚
- Ice cream at [Cold Cow Creamery](https://coldcowcreamery.com). Maple shake was delicous. 🍦
- Visit [Hildene](https://hildene.org), family home of the Lincoln's. 🎟
- Shopping at [Orvis Flagship Store](https://stores.orvis.com/us/vermont/manchester). 🛍
- We played unlimited pinball at [Pastime Pinball](https://pastimepinball.com). Very well maintained pinball machines. [Metallica Pro](https://sternpinball.com/game/metallica/) machine was my favorite.
- Pizza outside at [Sam’s Wood Fired Pizza](https://www.samswoodfiredpizzavt.com). 🍕
- Head back to Airbnb! 🚙
