We are dedicating this day to our friend Ellen. Ellen is from this area of Vermont and most of the activities today are great suggestions from her!

Weather: Nice morning. Hot and humid in afternoon. Rain showers during outdoor theater, got plenty wet. Rain into the evening. 

- 7:30a: Depart the [Schoolhouse](https://studiohill.farm/pages/the-schoolhouse). 
- 9:35a: Breakfast at [Skinny Pancakes](https://skinnypancake.com)
- **Shopping in Woodstock:** [Woody's Mercantile](https://woodysmercantile.com), [Unicorn](http://unicornvt.com), [Collective: The Art of Craft](https://www.collective-theartofcraft.com), [Vermont Eclectic Company](https://vteclecticco.com), [Woodstock Gallery](http://www.woodstockgalleryvt.com), [Clover](https://clovergiftshop.com), [Gillingham's General Store](https://www.gillinghams.com), 
- Quick visit to [Marsh - Billings - Rockefeller National Historical Park](https://www.nps.gov/mabi/index.htm).
- Coffee stop at [Abracadabra Coffee](https://www.abracadabracoffeeco.com). Iced coffee, cans of cold press, and beans for home. 
- 1:07p: [Simon Pearce Gallery](https://www.simonpearce.com)
- 1:49p: [King Arthur Flour Company](https://www.kingarthurbaking.com/cafe-bakery-store)
- 2:30p: Depart for Glover, VT. 
- 4:00p: [Bread & Puppet Theatre](https://breadandpuppet.org)
- 6:00p: Drive to [Treehouse](https://www.airbnb.com/rooms/35162322), defer check-in due to rain. 
- 6:45p Dinner at [Salt Bistro](https://www.saltvt.com/)
- 8:35p: Back to [Treehouse](https://www.airbnb.com/rooms/35162322) and unload in the rain, up two flights of stairs, in the dark. 
- 9:40p: Treehouse doesn't have WiFi. Went to enable Mobile Hotspot on my iPhone and to my surprise my new AT&T plan doesn't support that. Spent 30-mins with customer support fixing that.
