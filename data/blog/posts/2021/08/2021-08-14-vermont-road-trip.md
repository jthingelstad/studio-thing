Weather: Perfect. 

- Breakfast at [GROW Café + Bistro](http://www.grow-food.com). 🍽
- Return shopping at [Urban Found](https://www.urbanfound.net) for Mazie. 🛍
- [Pottery projects](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/08/14/express-yourself-art.html) at [Express Yourself Art Barn](https://expressyourselfartbarn.com). 🎨
- Return visit to Roan & Black for [Kimmy Cantrell mask](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/08/14/when-we-visited.html)!

- Afternoon in the waves and sand, [lots of sand](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/08/14/the-kids-buried.html), at [Oval Beach](https://www.saugatuckcity.com/oval-beach.html). 🏖
