Weather: Still a bit rainy in the morning, but cleared up. Fairly warm in the afternoon. Evening turned out nice.

- 9:14a: Left 14 mins late for Montpelier, VT. 
- Brunch at [Three Penny Taproom](https://www.threepennytaproom.com). 
- Check out [Vermont State House](https://statehouse.vermont.gov). 
- Escape Room at [Greatest Caper](https://thegreatestcaper.com).
- Afternoon coffee and chocolates at [Rabble-Rouser](https://www.rabblerouser.net). 
- Drive back to St. Johnsbury. 
- [Fairbanks Museum & Planetarium](https://www.fairbanksmuseum.org) for exhibits and two showings in the platerium. 
- [Dog Mountain](https://www.dogmt.com/) very cool, the [Chapel](https://www.dogmt.com/Dog-Chapel.html) was moving. 😢
- Dog Mountain [Levitt Amp Conert](https://www.dogmt.com/Summer-Music-Series.html) to see [Mike Mains and The Branches](https://mikemainsmusic.com). 🎶
- Dinner at [Kingdom Taproom & Table](http://www.kingdomtaproom.com).
