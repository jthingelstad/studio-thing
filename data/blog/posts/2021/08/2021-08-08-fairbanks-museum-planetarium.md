We visited the [Fairbanks Museum & Planetarium](https://www.fairbanksmuseum.org) today  in St. Johnsbury, VT. The museum has been operating since 1889, and is a bit of a museum piece itself. It shows what this type of museum would have been like a hundred years ago, with only small modifications to the displays. We also took in two sessions at the planetarium.
