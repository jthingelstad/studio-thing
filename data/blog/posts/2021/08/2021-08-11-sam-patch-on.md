We took a 90-minute river Erie Canal cruise today on the [Sam Patch](https://www.sampatch.org). We got to go to Lock 32 and experience that and then went back the other way to see where the canal is actaully 70' higher than the houses in the area.

Some fun stats:

- There is a 568 foot elevation change as the canal goes from Lake Erie to New York Harbor.
- The original canal was 4' deep, 40' wide and 363 miles long. The canal has been lengthened and widened three times.
- Doors to the locks weigh 40 tons!
