_Weather: Little cool but sunny._

- Breakfast pastries and delicious coffee at [Blue Collar Coffee](https://www.bluecollarcoffee.net/indy). 
- Explore area: [City Dogs Grocer](https://citydogsgrocery.com), [Moonshot Games](https://moonshotgamestore.com), [Homespun: Modern Homemade](https://www.homespunindy.com), [Penn & Beech](https://www.pennandbeech.com), and [Decorate](https://decorateindy.com). 
- Return to [Pins Mechanical](https://www.pinsbar.com) for another round of Duckpin Bowling.
- Lunch at [Punch Burger](http://www.punchburger.com).
- Shopping at [8 Fifteen](https://www.8fifteen.com).
- Return to [Cake Bake Shop](https://thecakebakeshop.com) for some cake to-go: [Gwendolyn's Famous Earl's Court Chocolate Cake](https://thecakebakeshop.com/collections/fall-cakes/products/gwendolyns-famous-earls-court-chocolate-cake-also-available-on-williams-sonoma), [Pumpkin Chocolate Cake](https://thecakebakeshop.com/collections/fall-cakes/products/pumpkin-chocolate-cake), [Millionaire Cake](https://thecakebakeshop.com/collections/fall-cakes/products/millionaire-cake), and [Carrot Cake](https://thecakebakeshop.com/collections/fall-cakes/products/carrot-cake). The Carrot Cake and Millionaire Cake were the family favorites. 
- We all made [custom designed shoes at Kicasso](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/10/24/creativity-on-shoes.html)!
- Dinner at [Public Greens](https://www.publicgreensurbankitchen.com).
- Final round of billiards at the [Bottleworks Hotel](https://www.bottleworkshotel.com).
