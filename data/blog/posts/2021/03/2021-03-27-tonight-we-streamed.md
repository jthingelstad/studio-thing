Tonight we streamed [The Cactus Blossoms](http://thecactusblossoms.com) as they played live to a limited 50-table audience at [First Avenue](https://first-avenue.com). It was great to see them play live, and the production from First Avenue was really good with multiple cameras sweeping through the room. I hope that they continue to offer streaming options for shows. I like the idea of having an option to see bands live in the main room, but also being able to see them streaming opens up a lot of additional options to see live music that otherwise aren't available.
