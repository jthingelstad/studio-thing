Having the [Ever Given, a massive container ship, stuck in the Suez Canal](https://www.cbc.ca/news/business/suez-canal-ever-given-1.5961615) and halting all normal activity through the canal is a familiar problem to any technologist that has dealt with queue problems. Look at the [real-time vessel map](https://www.vesselfinder.com/?imo=9811000) and all the ships stuck waiting. 🚢

They normally pass 50 ships a day through the canal. With so many backed up, it will be a few days before things get back to normal. 😬
