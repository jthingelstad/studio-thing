Arizona State Route 88 goes right along [Canyon Lake](https://www.visitarizona.com/places/parks-monuments/canyon-lake/) on the way to Tortilla Flat. It even has two fun single-lane bridges that you have to negotiate with other cars. The rock and water is striking with the right sunlight.
