Wanting to experience even more desert plant life we visited the [Desert Botanical Garden](https://dbg.org) today and it was incredible. Everywhere you looked there were great displays. It was a great day to walk around and take in the great scenery and learn more about various cactus and plants.

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/b1ccb97f70.jpg)  
*A dense field of various cactus.*

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/06bd381144.jpg)  
*I loved the red needles on these cactus.*

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/49d8beca0b.jpg)  
*Saguaro cactus with Cholla cactus in the foreground.*

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/cb05e3df77.jpg)  
*At first we thought these giants were Saguaro but they are actually [Cardon cactus](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pachycereus_pringlei) that grow even bigger. They are less resilient to cold so they do not grow native in Arizona.*

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/2efcba5f0b.jpg)  
*Very rare [Crested Saguaro](https://www.nps.gov/sagu/learn/nature/why_crested.htm) cactus.*

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/d43f211612.jpg)  
*These [Chihuly](https://www.chihuly.com) glass sculptures were incredible.*
