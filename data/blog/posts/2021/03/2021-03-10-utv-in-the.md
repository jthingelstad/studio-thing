We have always thought it would be fun to rent a UTV and go out for some trail riding. We gave [American Powersport Rentals](https://americanpowersportsllc.com) a call and Duke got us all set up. We rented for a half day and were complete newbies but had a great time. We drove to the [Rolls OHV Area](https://www.fs.usda.gov/recarea/tonto/recreation/ohv/recarea/?recid=83054&actid=93) in the Tonto National Forest. We didn’t have the electrical hookup for the trailer so I was happy it was only 15 miles of straight driving. 

Here is our route for the day, you can also see the official [trail map](https://www.fs.usda.gov/Internet/FSE_DOCUMENTS/fseprd725196.pdf).  

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/b70e146f3d.jpg)
