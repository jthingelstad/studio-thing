We took a drive today and for lunch stopped at [Silver King Smokehouse](https://www.silverkingbbq.com) in Superior. It was a spectacular lunch most centered around brisket. Their brisket was phenomenal and they had a great outdoor area with picnic tables. If you find yourself in the area, get yourself some delicious barbecue.
