After our hike at the [Lost Dutchman](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/03/05/we-visited-lost.html) and the delightful [drive on Arizona State Route 88](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/03/05/we-took-the.html) we stopped at [Tortilla Flat](https://www.tortillaflataz.com), proudly stated **population 6**. It is very much a tourist trap, but it was fun to see and goof around with the kids for a bit. We got there just moments before they closed up shop, just in time to get some water and head back to Mesa.
