We hiked up the [Wind Cave trail](https://www.alltrails.com/trail/us/arizona/wind-cave-trail) this morning and had a great time. Tammy declared it perhaps her favorite hike ever. It was **1.7 miles** with **800 feet** of elevation on the way up. It took us just short of an hour. 

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/afc181ec2b.jpg" width="600" height="600" alt="Satellite map view of a hiking trail route with a color-coded GPS track going from a green pin to a red pin across desert terrain." />

The views were fantastic and the terrain was challenging and varied. Lucky went up with us and did a great job. The trail was already busy at 8:30a and when we were going down we passed large groups. So get out there early!
