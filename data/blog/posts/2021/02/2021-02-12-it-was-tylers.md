It was Tyler's birthday today and we decided to get a really cool Minecraft themed cake to celebrate. [Lesley's Creative Cakes](https://www.lesleyscreativecakes.com) did an amazing job on this creation!

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/9640d75c36.jpg" alt="Minecraft-themed birthday cake for Tyler with pixel grass blocks, Steve, a Creeper, TNT, and a house on top, displayed outdoors on a table.">

It was a little confusing to figure out how to cut into it, but the main cube was delicious Red Velvet cake.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/74909cd11b.jpg" alt="Minecraft grass block cake cut open revealing red velvet interior, decorated with green and brown fondant pixel squares">

There was plenty of cake. Cake decorators probably know this but I was surprised that the more decorative blocks were actually Rice Krispie bars. 

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/3eef87fda9.jpg" alt="Minecraft Creeper birthday cake cut open revealing red velvet interior, with decorative fondant character figures on a black board beside a knife.">

Most importantly Tyler loved it!
