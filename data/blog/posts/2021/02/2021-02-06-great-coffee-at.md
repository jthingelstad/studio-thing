I had a great start to the day with a visit to [Pair Cupworks](https://paircupworks.com) this morning. I had a top notch cappuccino followed by a cup of Costa Rica pour over. The brown sugar and plum flavors in the pour over were great.

I left with some [Guatemalan coffee](https://paircupworks.com/collections/micro-roast-coffee-beans/products/8oz-wash-processed-coffee-beans-from-guatemala) by Adrián Hernández from La Galera Farm in the village of La Pinada, Guatemala as well as [Ethiopian coffee](https://paircupworks.com/collections/micro-roast-coffee-beans/products/copy-of-8oz-wash-processed-coffee-beans-from-guatemala) from the Mijane Family and the Halo Hartume Station.

I spent an hour chatting with the owner Kimhak En who runs the business with his wife Eugenia Tai. He was very nice and had a ton to share on great coffee shops around the country, different techniques and equipment, and their journey [starting their own shop](https://dailycoffeenews.com/2019/12/04/now-roasting-in-mesa-arizona-pair-cupworks/).
