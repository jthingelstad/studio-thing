This morning we drove to [Cave Creek Regional Park](https://www.maricopacountyparks.net/cave-creek-regional-park-cc/) to go for a hike. It was about 40 minutes from where we are staying but well worth the drive. We did a loop heading out on Go John Trail, then to Quartz Trail, and then back on Slate Trail. We hiked **3.5 miles**, gaining **425 feet**, and took a total of **1h 22m**.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/3e56dc3894.jpg" alt="GPS track of a 3.5-mile loop hike at Cave Creek Regional Park shown on satellite map, color-coded yellow and green by pace.">

It was a really nice hike with great views. There were gentle climbs up but overall it was a nice trail that wasn't overly difficult. 

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/babb8edef0.jpg" alt="Brown Go John Trail sign on a desert dirt path leading toward a saguaro-covered hill under a clear blue sky.">

There were Saguaro Cactus all over the place. 🌵
