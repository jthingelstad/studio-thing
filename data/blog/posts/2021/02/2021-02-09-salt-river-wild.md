Today we visited [Coon Bluff Recreation Area](https://www.fs.usda.gov/recarea/tonto/null/recarea?recid=35399&actid=70) to see the Salt River wild horses. The non-profit [Salt River Wild Horse Management](https://saltriverwildhorsemanagementgroup.org) group helps provide for these wild horses, and they are currently putting out hay for them each day. You can visit while they do this and walk among these wild horses but you need to be slow and calm, and keep your distance. The horses themselves were skirmishing a bit. It gets your attention when twenty horses start stirring and running around you. I brought the good camera with a long lens to catch some photos of these beautiful horses.
