Today we visited the [Noah Purifoy Outdoor Museum](http://www.noahpurifoy.com/joshua-tree-outdoor-museum) in Joshua Tree. We weren't familiar with Purifoy, but his [background](http://www.noahpurifoy.com/about-noah) is very impressive and the outdoor museum he created here is unique, interesting, and a little confusing. We spent a while walking around and taking pictures. We didn’t read up about Purifoy until we left which we actually liked since it allowed us to see the art "as is".
