We visited the [Sabino Canyon Recreation Area](https://www.fs.usda.gov/recarea/coronado/recarea/?recid=75425) today on our day trip to Tucson. We took the shuttle to the top of the trail and then made our way down stopping at several locations. It was a very pretty walk and fun to see the spring creek with running water down the canyon. We had to cross it barefoot and the water was **very cold**!
