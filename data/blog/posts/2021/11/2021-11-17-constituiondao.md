[ConstitutionDAO](https://www.constitutiondao.com) caught my attention right away. This DAO is forming to raise a fund to bid on one of two privately owned authentic copies of the US Constitution. Sotheby's is holding the auction tomorrow.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/6517937da6.jpg" width="600" height="423" alt="" style="max-width: 600px;" />

The DAO started this morning with about $16M in ETH raised, and currently has $33.7M in ETH just twelve hours later via their [Juicebox listing](https://juicebox.money/#/p/constitutiondao). The project was launched six days ago!

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/dd1128dc94.jpg" width="600" height="312" alt="" style="max-width: 600px;" />

I couldn't resist being part of this so I [sent in a small amount of ETH](https://etherscan.io/tx/0x67037e9be16c11ed8fe27d82493892e91c5bc23d40266bb99f5348ec354eefe3) and am waiting for the auction to claim my PEOPLE governance tokens.

What a wild idea, and all enabled largely because of the crypto stack. We'll see what happens next! 🤞
