I've been building out my [approach to crypto](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/02/20/approaching-cryptocurrencies.html). I shared [my allocation in March](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/03/31/sharing-my-current.html) and updated it this weekend.

Notes:

- I've been increasing [Ethereum](https://ethereum.org/) to get closer to [Bitcoin](https://bitcoin.org/).
- Bitcoin and Ethereum together are 80%. 
- Increased the amount of [Cardano](https://cardano.org) as an additional Ethereum-like blockchain.
- Doubled [Filecoin](https://filecoin.io), which builds a permanence layer on IPFS.
- Added [Curve DAO](https://resources.curve.fi/) because of the focus on cost-efficient swapping between stablecoins.
- Added [Enjin](https://enjin.io/about/enjin-coin) because of the focus on NFTs.

I'm sharing this as my own notes for any value they may have to others.
