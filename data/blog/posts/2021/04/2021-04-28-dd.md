Tammy and I went to [Sooki & Mimi](https://www.sookiandmimi.com) for the first time and had their Prix Fixe Menu, titled "A Celebration Of Spring Flavors And [Nixtamal](https://www.wordnik.com/words/nixtamal)." The dining room was great with a nice warm feeling. The wait staff were top notch, and extremely informed on the menu and the preparations. 

### Aguachile Verde

This cold soup was a nice way to start the meal. I'm not a huge fan of cold soups, but this had a really good flavor combination and the green was visually striking.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/e2db604343.jpg" width="600" height="600" alt="Aguachile verde cold soup in a dark ceramic bowl topped with sliced radishes, asparagus, herbs, and yellow flowers" />

### Mackerel Gravlax Tostada

The tostada had a rich medley of flavors that kept me intrigued through the whole thing. No one thing stood out. It instead delivered this combination that was very satisfying.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/5cefc694c1.jpg" width="600" height="600" alt="Mackerel gravlax tostada on a purple corn tortilla topped with fresh dill, capers, shaved fish, and herbs on a speckled ceramic plate atop a wood table" />

### Walleye Tacos

Delicious tacos. Neither Tammy or I are big fans of Tartar Sauce, but it didn’t detract from the delightful fish. Yum.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/734dea7f58.jpg" width="600" height="600" alt="Two walleye tacos on corn tortillas topped with avocado, jalapeno slices, red cabbage slaw, cilantro, and creamy sauce, served on a blue ceramic plate with a lime wedge" />

### Kimchi Mandu

These were vegetarian and I’m not sure how the Kimchi was prepared but it was tasty and complex.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/6ea474fbfb.jpg" width="600" height="600" alt="Three kimchi mandu dumplings in clear broth garnished with cilantro, chili threads, and chopped scallions in a rustic ceramic bowl on a wood table." />

### Hibiscus Rhubarb Bar

This was the perfect flavor combination to end the meal with!

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2021/7965ca70ff.jpg" width="600" height="600" alt="Hibiscus rhubarb dessert bar topped with a quenelle of cream and thin herb garnish on a white speckled plate atop a wood table" />
