When people write about blogging platforms or services they often focus on features and functions. I say _hogwash_ to that. There is only one thing that matters about a blogging solution — **does it create an environment where you want to write?** For me, micro.blog does that better than any other service I've used. I'm happy to mark today as my **fourth year** using [micro.blog](https://micro.blog). Four years ago I was [poking around](https://www.thingelstad.com/2017/04/28/poking-around-microblog.html) having [waited for my invite code](https://www.thingelstad.com/2017/04/27/t857780228105654273.html). **This has been my favorite four years of blogging since I started in 2004.**

Over those 17 years I've used: .Text [→](https://www.thingelstad.com/2006/11/25/migrating-text-to.html) WordPress [→](https://www.thingelstad.com/2016/04/20/moved-my-blog.html) SquareSpace [→](https://www.thingelstad.com/2014/03/16/i-flopped-httpstcozmirtevd.html) Pelican [→](https://www.thingelstad.com/2017/10/29/managing-years-of.html) Jekyll [→](https://www.thingelstad.com/2020/05/02/importing-jekyll-site.html) micro.blog. (those → are all links to the relevant posts 🤓)

Micro.blog let's me pull out my phone and with extraordinarily low effort put a picture or two along with some text on the web. Nothing is faster and easier. I love that Micro.blog is built on a static blog engine, [Hugo](https://gohugo.io), since I never have to worry about performance. It is _fast_. It is great that I can hit a link in my favorites and have a text area on a web page to type and post. It is awesome that it can handle short things, a couple sentences, and really long things. The pages function is simple, but really powerful. I have 124 pages of content that goes with my blog. My [lists](https://www.thingelstad.com/lists/) are my favorite. Automatic categories based on text patterns in the posts is a very powerful feature. It doesn't have tags, I’m fine with that, I curate [collections](https://www.thingelstad.com/collections/) instead. There is no library of 5,000 plugins, good. This just lets me focus more on writing. 

**2,039 posts.** That is what micro.blog has enabled since April 2017. [I 💛 micro.blog](https://www.thingelstad.com/2021/01/24/i-microblog.html). Thank you **[Manton](https://www.manton.org)** and **[Jean](http://micro.welltempered.net)** for making such a great service!

Now 2,040 posts…
