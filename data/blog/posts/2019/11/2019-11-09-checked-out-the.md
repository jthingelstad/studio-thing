Checked out the [First Avenue](https://first-avenue.com) special exhibit at the [Minnesota History Center](http://www.mnhs.org/historycenter/) today. Loved the special highlights of [Hüsker Dü](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hüsker_Dü), [The Replacements](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Replacements_(band)), and [Soul Asylum](https://www.soulasylum.com). Of course [Prince](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Prince_(musician)) too. Hüsker Dü being described as “candy-coated razor wire” made me chuckle.
