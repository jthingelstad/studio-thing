[DevOpsDays MSP](https://devopsdays.org/minneapolis/) keynote from Liz Fong-Jones [@lizthegrey](https://twitter.com/lizthegrey) on reducing complexity in systems.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2019/e16fa92aab.jpg" width="600" height="337" alt="Speaker on stage at DevOpsDays conference presenting slide titled Detangling complex systems with compassion and production excellence" />

“Heroism isn’t sustainable.” [Liz Fong-Jones](https://twitter.com/lizthegrey). This is more than an individual thing, it’s a culture thing. Always beware of hidden incentives that encourage heroism in your culture. 

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2019/808fd4fe0e.jpg" width="600" height="337" alt="Speaker at a DevOpsDays conference podium beside a slide reading Heroism isn't sustainable with a cartoon superhero illustration and live captions visible." />

“Lack of observability is a systemic risk.” [Liz Fong-Jones](https://twitter.com/lizthegrey). This is a strong way to position observability, and frame it in a broader context. Very good keynote to start [DevOpsDays MSP](https://devopsdays.org/minneapolis/)!

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2019/7f663c68ed.jpg" width="600" height="337" alt="Speaker on stage at DevOpsDays conference presenting a slide reading Lack of observability is systemic risk to a seated audience." />

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2019/ffcb8410b7.jpg" width="600" height="337" alt="Speaker on stage at DevOpsDays conference presenting a slide titled Principles of Collaborative Automation to a seated audience." />

I learned a new word from [Jessica Kerr](https://twitter.com/jessitron) “[symmathesy](https://norabateson.wordpress.com/2015/11/03/symmathesy-a-word-in-progress/)”. Mutual learning in living systems. I like giving this powerful process a definition. This is key to continuous improvement. 🤔

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2019/e745070ff0.jpg" width="600" height="337" alt="Speaker on stage at DevOpsDays conference presenting a slide titled Symmathesy showing a diagram of interconnected learning parts, audience seated in foreground" />

This is a great message from [Jessica Kerr](https://twitter.com/jessitron), with [reference to John Allspaw](https://medium.com/code-for-america/john-allspaw-getting-the-messy-details-is-critical-59e641aa0a77), success begets complexity. That complexity delivers value, and more success. Repeat. 👍

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2019/b553ef9412.jpg" width="600" height="337" alt="Speaker on stage at DevOpsDays conference presenting a slide showing the words success and complexity with arrows between them, audience seated in foreground." />
