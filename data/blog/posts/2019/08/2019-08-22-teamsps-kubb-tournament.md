We had a great time with our 2019 #TeamSPS Kubb Tournament today! We had 20 teams having a lot of fun, getting to know their teammates better, and enjoying a gorgeous day outside!

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2019/10707fdf07.jpg" width="600" height="337" alt="Group of people playing Kubb on a grass lawn between office buildings on a sunny day, with wooden blocks scattered across the field" />

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2019/38cb9e9b7d.jpg" width="600" height="337" alt="Groups of people playing Kubb on a sunny urban lawn, tossing wooden batons at upright wooden blocks near downtown buildings." />

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2019/dff5903fce.jpg" width="600" height="337" alt="Group of coworkers celebrating outdoors on a grass lawn during a Kubb tournament, with wooden game pieces scattered around them." />

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2019/d6ed078178.jpg" width="600" height="337" alt="Groups of people playing Kubb on a green urban lawn with wooden pins set up, deck chairs labeled Turf Club nearby, and city buildings in the background." />

After the round robin our #TeamSPS Kubb groups split into brackets with a winner in each bracket! We kept track old school, just like most tournaments do!

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2019/94c740707d.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="Handwritten single-elimination tournament brackets on white paper spread across an outdoor folding table, labeled by division including Distinguished, Senior, Principal, Associate, and Staff." />

After the 2019 #TeamSPS Kubb tournament a bunch of folks hung out and played, you guessed it, more Kubb! Next year we might introduce Mölkky at the end!
