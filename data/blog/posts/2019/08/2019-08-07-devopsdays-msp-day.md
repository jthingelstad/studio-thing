[DevOpsDays MSP](https://devopsdays.org/minneapolis/) has done a fabulous job creating an inclusive event. Hearing from so many diverse voices is refreshing. With all of that goodness, it is jarring when sponsors address the group with "hey guys". Make sure your message reaches the entire audience.

Day 2 opening keynote for DevOpsDays MSP from [John Engelman](https://twitter.com/johnrengelman) on Cascading Failure. [DevOpsDays MSP](https://devopsdays.org/minneapolis/) 

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2019/dd503f8495.jpg" width="600" height="337" alt="Man standing on a conference stage presenting a Target-branded slide titled A Day A Week A Month in a Cascading Failure at a DevOpsDays event." />

“We were super confident in that answer, and we were super wrong.” [John Engelman](https://twitter.com/johnrengelman). I’ve been there many times in incidents. Whenever you are “super confident”, use that as a trigger for what you might be missing.
