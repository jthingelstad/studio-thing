We weren't able to get to the Edina Art Fair until shortly before it was closing today. We hustled around and got to see a large amount of the art. The fair seemed a bit fuller than previous years, with higher quality art than I had remembered. Artists that caught my eye:

- [Brendan Kramp](https://www.brendankramp.com/): I liked his style of painting and the various coffee shops he had on display were familiar to me. I think it would be cool to commission something from him. 
- [Straight Up Chess](https://www.straightupchess.com/)
- [Matthew Rucker](https://www.matthewrucker.com/)
- [Jamie Heiden](http://jheidenphoto.net/)
- [Greta Sandquist](https://gretasandquist.com/)
- [Dogfish Media](https://www.dogfishmedia.com/)
- Kate Whisler: Laser cut scenes.
