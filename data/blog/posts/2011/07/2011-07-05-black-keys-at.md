On Sunday night Tammy and I went to see [The Black
Keys](http://www.theblackkeys.com/) at [Roy Wilkins
Auditorium](http://www.theroy.org/). I only [recently discovered the
Black Keys](https://www.thingelstad.com/2011/02/22/the-black-keys.html).
I heard they were coming to town and they seemed like a great band to
see live so we made a point to go. The show was really good. The Black
Keys play a really good live show. I had fun taking some photos with my
iPhone while we were there.

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/1147db3dee.jpg)

Dan Auerbach and Patrick Carney came on the stage. It was a little odd
just seeing two guys, but they made an amazing amount of sound for just
two people.

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/b886a0c136.jpg)

I really liked the simplicity of their stage setup. It worked really
well with their overall feel and made it feel like a smaller show.

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/c6430d7504.jpg)

They had the largest disco ball I've ever seen. It was incredibly big.
At least six feet in diameter.

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/5b2f2af786.jpg)

After an initial set they brought on two more band members and played a
number of songs from Brothers.

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/3d3469c04c.jpg)

I thought these lights in three rows were pretty cool.

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/75121982fc.jpg)

This made me think we were on a porch on a summer evening. Strings of
bare lightbulbs.

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/b3ff21ecd3.jpg)

At the end a light board rose behind the stage with THE BLACK KEYS lit
up on it.

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/7cf793d3b6.jpg)

So I got a little excited with the lights. The music was really great
too! 😀
