On Sunday night Tammy and I went to see [The Black Keys](http://www.theblackkeys.com/) at [Roy Wilkins Auditorium](http://www.theroy.org/). I only [recently discovered the Black Keys](https://www.thingelstad.com/2011/02/22/the-black-keys.html). I heard they were coming to town and they seemed like a great band to see live so we made a point to go. The show was really good. The Black Keys play a really good live show. I had fun taking some photos with my iPhone while we were there.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/1147db3dee.jpg" alt="Crowd of concert-goers on the arena floor at an indoor venue with large speaker arrays and lighting rigs overhead">

Dan Auerbach and Patrick Carney came on the stage. It was a little odd just seeing two guys, but they made an amazing amount of sound for just two people.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/b886a0c136.jpg" alt="Dark concert hall with two performers lit by bright spotlights on a small stage, crowd silhouettes filling the foreground">

I really liked the simplicity of their stage setup. It worked really well with their overall feel and made it feel like a smaller show.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/c6430d7504.jpg" alt="Concert stage lit with red, blue, white and yellow spotlights beaming over silhouetted crowd and performers">

They had the largest disco ball I've ever seen. It was incredibly big. At least six feet in diameter.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/5b2f2af786.jpg" alt="Black and white concert photo showing crowd silhouettes raising hands toward an intensely bright starburst of stage lights on a darkened indoor stage">

After an initial set they brought on two more band members and played a number of songs from Brothers.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/3d3469c04c.jpg" alt="Concert stage bathed in red and blue spotlights with silhouetted crowd in foreground and bright LED screens behind performers">

I thought these lights in three rows were pretty cool.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/75121982fc.jpg" alt="Black and white concert scene with three rows of stage lights on trusses, bright spotlights cutting through dark air above a crowd silhouette">

This made me think we were on a porch on a summer evening. Strings of bare lightbulbs.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/b3ff21ecd3.jpg" alt="Blurry black and white concert photo showing rows of bright stage lights above a crowd at an indoor venue">

At the end a light board rose behind the stage with THE BLACK KEYS lit up on it.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/7cf793d3b6.jpg" alt="Black and white concert photo showing a crowded indoor venue with a brightly lit stage and rows of spotlights overhead">

So I got a little excited with the lights. The music was really great too! 😀
