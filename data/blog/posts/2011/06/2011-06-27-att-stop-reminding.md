Okay, these monthly text messages from AT&T; drive me crazy.

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/05149c6c49.gif)

Let me inventory all the ways these are annoying:

-   They seem to come at the worse times. I was having a busy day at
    work and got this interruption. Thought it was something important.
    It was a text message after all. But, no, it was a billing notice.
-   No human intelligence has been encoded into the scheduling of these.
    Can you believe I got one of these on Christmas Day? Yeah, Merry
    Christmas to you too!
-   The language is arcane. Get rid of the cruft and make the wording
    something that seems natural.

Okay, that's enough. I just needed to say that.
