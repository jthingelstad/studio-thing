This weekend I made [EGGfest
Chili](http://site.outdora.com/blog/eggfest-chili-compliments-big-green-egg-871.html)
for the second time. This recipe is from the new [Big Green Egg
Cookbook](http://www.amazon.com/dp/0740791451/). I'm a big fan!

What makes this chili unique is that you smoke it for a while on the Big
Green Egg. Here is the [Le
Creuset](http://www.amazon.com/dp/B000NNAVOO/) going on the grill.

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/d2f3d6623a.jpg)

A couple of hours later and you can see that the chili has darkened and
steeped itself to deliciousness.

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/1c51ab8ae6.jpg)

What was a blue pot, now looks completely black. That is the smoke
residue. Don't worry, an SOS pad gets it looking like new in no time.

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/e854bd59d1.jpg)

This is easily the best chili I've ever made.
