Tammy had noted that Mazie and Tyler really like to play in playhouses
when we visit people. Our yard is a standard city lot, so we don't have
much space but she figured out a good spot in the yard and a playhouse
that would work there.

The playhouse didn't have a floor so we made a trip to Home Depot and
got the stuff to build a simple deck that would sit on the ground with
cedar deck boards. Mazie helped me build the platform and Tammy prepared
the ground and got the playhouse ready to assemble. It was a fun
afternoon of building and assembling and the kids like the end result.
