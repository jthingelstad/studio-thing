It was a very cold morning but we made it out to see the [World Snow Celebration](https://www.worldsnowcelebration.com) and the snow sculptures. Teams were working towards the deadline of completing their sculptures. They were amazing. We also did the Peoples Choice votes. I voted for [Team Falcon of Mongolia](https://www.worldsnowcelebration.com/team-falcon-of-mongolia) and Tammy went for [Team Warm Your Cold Brits](https://www.worldsnowcelebration.com/team-great-britain).
