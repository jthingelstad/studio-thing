Peter Rukavina posted a [graph of his Amazon spending](https://ruk.ca/content/amazon-purchases-year) and I couldn't resist taking a look at my data. You can easily request this from Amazon in the "Privacy Center". A quick pivot table later and I had this graph. I'm omitting the actual scale on purpose — the shape does enough to tell the story.

I'm not that clear on what happened in 2015 that drove a big jump that stayed that way. My family comments that I purchase a lot from Amazon and I thought they were overstating it, but maybe not.

Honestly I was truly surprised at the frequency and amounts.
