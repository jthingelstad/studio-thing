In just over a week we head to Switzerland for our hiking trip. I wanted to get a longer and harder hike in before we left so this morning Tammy and I got up early, let the kids sleep in, and went to [Murphy-Hanrehan Park Reserve](https://www.threeriversparks.org/location/murphy-hanrehan-park-reserve) for a **5.3 mile hike** with about **900 ft of elevation gain**. It took us **2h 8m to finish**. My shirt was like I had wore it in a shower when we got done. I brought my poles and was initially feeling silly, but after a couple of miles the trail started going up and down more and I was happy to have them. 😅 [Also on Strava](https://www.strava.com/activities/9386988195).
