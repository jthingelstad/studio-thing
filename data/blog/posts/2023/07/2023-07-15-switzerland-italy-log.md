_Weather: Hot and Sunny._

- Breakfast at Motel One
- Took Tram with luggage to Zurich HB
- Stored luggage at station (23-06, 23-09)
- S24 train to Kilchberg
- [Visit Lindt Home of Chocolate](https://www.thingelstad.com/2023/07/15/we-had-a.html)
- S8 train back to Zurich HB
- Quickly retrieve luggage from storage, only 15 min stop
- Boarded IC909 to Landquart
- Transfer traina to Scuol
- Tammy published [her first blog post](https://tammy.thingelstad.com/2023/07/15/difference-in-air.html?ref=thingelstad.com) in over 11 years! 🤩
- Taxi to [Hotel Guardaval](https://www.belvedere-hotelfamilie.ch/en/our-hotels/guardaval/) in Scuol
- [Walk around Scuol a bit](https://www.thingelstad.com/2023/07/15/some-photos-walking.html)
- Meet fellow hikers and guides Dave and Erik for dinner
- Delicious four course dinner at hotel
