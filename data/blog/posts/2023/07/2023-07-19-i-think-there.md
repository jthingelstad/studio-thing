I think there is a huge potential for Generative AI in tools, but this is a heavy expense that is going to make it a tough decision.

> **Microsoft** announced that it would charge $30 a month for [AI features](https://www.economist.com/finance-and-economics/2023/07/16/your-employer-is-probably-unprepared-for-artificial-intelligence) in its productivity software. That could mean a cost increase of up to 83% for the millions of subscribers to products such as Word and Excel. Shares in the firm jumped to a record high at the prospect. — Economist Espresso, July 19, 2023
