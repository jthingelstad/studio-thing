_Weather: Hot and sunny in the morning. Day cooled off a bit and was nicer in the mountains. Evening cool._

- Jamie realizes that he has lost his wallet and that sort of sucks a bunch.
- Breakfast.
- Jamie asks front desk person to see if they can help with locating wallet. They have some ideas.
- Dave, one of our guides, attempts to find Jamie's lost wallet by going back to the train station at Ardez. Sadly no luck.
- We take off for [todays hike](https://www.thingelstad.com/2023/07/17/hiking-guarda-outnback.html) up to Piz Buin.
- Everyone else hikes down on the service road, Jamie takes a [mountain scooter](https://www.thingelstad.com/2023/07/17/after-getting-to.html) and has a blast.
- Jamie gets back early and decides wallet search is over. It is lost, time to move on.
- Tammy and Mazie explore the town and [Tammy has an amazing find](https://tammy.thingelstad.com/2023/07/17/so-here-we.html?ref=thingelstad.com)! 🤩
- [Cold therapy for our tired feet](https://www.thingelstad.com/2023/07/17/cold-therapy-for.html).
- Nice dinner at Meisser Hotel.
- Cool evening back in the room, getting ready for an earlier morning tomorrow.
