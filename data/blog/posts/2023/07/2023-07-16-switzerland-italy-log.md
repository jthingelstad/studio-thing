_Weather: Warm and sunny. Cooler in mountains but still hot and sunny at prime of day. Evening clouds and breeze with much cooler temps._

- Breakfast at [Hotel Guardaval](https://www.belvedere-hotelfamilie.ch/en/our-hotels/guardaval/)
- Depart at 9a for day of hiking
- Hike Scuol to Guarda. We ended hike at Ardez and took train and bus to [Hotel Messier](https://www.hotel-meisser.ch/en/) in Guarda 
- Relax with Coca Cola on terrace of Hotel Messier
- Delicious dinner at Hotel Messier
