> **Apple**, an American [tech giant](https://www.economist.com/leaders/2023/06/06/apples-vision-pro-is-an-incredible-machine-now-to-find-out-what-it-is-for), surpassed $3trn in market value for the first time since January 2022, when it briefly became the first publicly listed company to do so.
