Today's hike left right from the front steps of the Hotel Edelweiss and walked out of Sils Maria to a ascent above the tree line. The ascent was a bit more gradual which I was very appreciated of. We had a great time experiencing [Grevasalvas](https://www.thingelstad.com/2023/07/20/grevasalvas-switzerland.html) on the trail, a wonderful rest at the top, and a nice hike back along the lack. A bit over 9.5 miles today.
