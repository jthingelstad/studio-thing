_Weather: Warm morning in Florence. Airports fine._

- Pack up suitcases for journey home
- Breakfast at hotel
- Taxi to Florence Airport
- Tyler forgets Pokémon gifts in airport at another gate and runs back and is able to recover them right before we board. 😳
- KLM 1642 [City Hopper flight to Amsterdam](https://www.thingelstad.com/2023/07/30/boarding-the-klm.html)
- Flight delayed over 40 mins due to baggage loading issues. Anxious with an already short transfer to next flight. 
- Another Amazing Race experience as we dashed from the Cityhopper late arrival to gate D47 with only 25 minutes to departure. Cleared passport control on super-fast-cut-in-front-of-everyone and were the last four welcomed onto the plane by the KLM gate agent waving us along! 🏃🏼‍♀️🏃🏼‍♂️🏃🏼‍♀️🏃🏼‍♂️
- Delta 165 to Minneapolis landed at 5p CT
- Lyft ride home
- Home Sweet Home!
