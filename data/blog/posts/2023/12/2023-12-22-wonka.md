We saw [Wonka](https://www.wonkamovie.com) this evening and all thought the movie was great. Wonka tells the magical origin story of [Willy Wonka](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Willy_Wonka). [Timothée Chalamet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Timothée_Chalamet) plays the role of Wonka perfectly. I highly recommend going.

I wish we would have picked a different theater though. The seats were dirty, there was popcorn and debris all over, and the other people in the theater kept talking and making noise at various times. 

Thinking about the experience had me thinking of the ideal. It would be incredible to see Wonka and get a box of chocolates to have during the movie, that are timed with the scenes in the movie, and matched with the flavors and story. There are several scenes where chocolate is a key character and having a taste to go with it would be incredible. 🤩
