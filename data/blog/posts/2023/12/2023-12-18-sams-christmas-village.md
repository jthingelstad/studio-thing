We made our first trip to [Sam’s Christmas Village and Light Show](https://samschristmasvillage.com) to see the **12 million** lights that they have on display. It was our favorite holiday light display we have been to. They have music playing, concessions, hot cocoa, Santa visits, and holiday music playing while you walk around the area.
