[I won the auction](https://www.ebay.com/itm/225903780761) for the "10 of 10" museum quality brass plaque that [Jeff Atwood](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jeff_Atwood) auctioned in his [I Fight For The Users](https://blog.codinghorror.com/i-fight-for-the-users/) post. It arrived today and is even more awesome in person! 🤩

Description from the eBay listing:

> Let's celebrate the death of Twitter in style, with the best tweet of ALL TIME! This museum quality brass plaque features a famous tweet on June 28th, 2012 (which just so happens to be Elon Musk's birthday) by [@Horse_ebooks](https://twitter.com/horse_ebooks) that reads, "Everything happens so much." It's almost 8 pounds, 14" wide by 8" tall, and will outlive all of us, regrettably. Includes brass bolts for mounting, too (not pictured). All proceeds are donated directly to [The Trevor Project](https://www.thetrevorproject.org) charity, which provides 24/7 crisis support to LGBTQ young people.
> 
> This is a unique item, 1 of 10 in the world, and it's for a good cause, so bid appropriately :)
