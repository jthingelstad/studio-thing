Tammy and I had a good evening at the 10th Annual **[Building on Dreams](https://avenuesforyouth.org/building-on-dreams/)** fundraiser for **[Avenues for Youth](https://avenuesforyouth.org)**. [Shayla Reaves](https://www.linkedin.com/in/shaylareaves/) was emcee with [Katherine Meerse](https://www.linkedin.com/in/katherine-meerse-7108476/) of Avenues giving the main message of the evening, as well as [Princess Titus](https://www.linkedin.com/in/princess-titus/), the Co-Founder of [Appetite for Change](https://appetiteforchangemn.org) sharing the impact that Avenues has had. Amazing choral performances from [Known MPLS](https://www.knownmpls.com) bookended the evening. This organization was new to Tammy and I and we were very impressed with the good work and results they are having. **[Donate here](https://give.avenuesforyouth.org/give/247352/#!/donation/checkout)!**
