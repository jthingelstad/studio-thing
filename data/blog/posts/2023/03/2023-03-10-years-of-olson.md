This weekend we are celebrating 20 years of Olson Family Vacations. In 2004 Tammy's side of the family decided to have everyone get together for a long weekend, and that started the tradition that has been going on two decades now.

![Olson Family Vacation 20 Years](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/04d24d2e45.png)

Every year the family responsible for finding a location and planning the weekend rotates, with everyone having hosted four vacations at this point.

To celebrate two decades of Olson Family Vacations we decided to make a special shirt for everyone.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/e26e33e50a.png" alt="Blue t-shirt front and back showing a cartoon bee logo with orange circles and Olson Family Vacation text, with 20 vacation locations listed on the back.">

The back of the shirt records the location of every vacation.

![Grand Superior Lodge — October 2004, Wisconsin Dells — October 2005, Lanesboro — May 2006, Lundeen Cabin — June 2007, Alexandria — May 2008, Brainerd Lake House — August 2009, Chequamegon — September 2010, Barn on the Bluff — May 2011, Aitkin — September 2012, Lanesboro — May 2013, Grand View Lodge — October 2014, Hibbing — September 2015, Blue Heron Lodge — October 2016, Nicaboyne Lake — October 2017, Park Rapids — October 2018, Amery — August 2019, Madison Lake — October 2020, Birchwood Acres — September 2021, River Lodge — November 2022, Fergus Falls — March 2023](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/75943bedb7.png)

Of course we [celebrated with a POAP](https://poap.gallery/event/107694) as well!
