One of our must stop places for Tyler and I in New York was [Nintendo New York](https://www.nintendonyc.com)! We got here on Sunday right at 11a when they opened. The store is filled with cool Nintendo history, characters, games, and of course plenty of gear to buy. We shopped and roamed around for 90 minutes taking it all in, including selfies with Link and Bowser.
