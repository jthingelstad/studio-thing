**[Minnebar](https://minnestar.org/minnebar/) 17** is coming up on **April 22nd**! For the first time ever, there will be [Minnebar 17 POAPs](https://www.thingelstad.com/2023/03/04/minnebar-poaps.html) given to all attendees, presenters, and organizers. I am excited to share that there will also be a free [POAP Raffle](https://poap.fun) in partnership with **StayNftyMpls** for everyone that collects any of the event POAPs.

[StayNftyMpls](https://www.staynftympls.xyz/) is a supportive community that shares, celebrates, and encourages the creativity of our community by exploring and leveraging NFTs and blockchain technology. [Erik Halaas](https://twitter.com/ErikHalaas) started StayNftyMpls and launched the **[612 Series](https://www.staynftympls.xyz/612-series)** (also [on OpenSea](https://opensea.io/collection/612-series)) as its first collection.

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/c49ce55754.jpg)

The **612 Series** highlights landmarks around the Twin Cities area — places that many of us have memories and stories to share about. This is a local project, with local landmarks, from a local artist!

This is all completely free and for fun! And there will be **thirty-two winners**!
