The [Health & Longevity](https://samharris.org/episode/SE8D8076420) episode of Making Sense with Sam Harris is a great overview of Peter Attia's new book **Outlive**.

> Sam Harris speaks with [Peter Attia](https://peterattiamd.com/) about his book, [Outlive: The Science and Art of Longevity](https://amzn.to/479BIdN). They discuss "healthspan," centenarians, diet and nutrition, sugar, macronutrients, alcohol, fasting and time-restricted eating, exercise, Zone 2 training, heart disease, blood pressure, cholesterol, cancer, brain health, metabolic disorders, proactive medical testing, medication side effects, Rapamycin, emotional health, and other topics.

I actually listened to all **three hours** myself, and then listened **again** as a whole family. As a subscriber to Making Sense, I can now share the [full length episode](https://samharris.org/episode/SE8D8076420) for anyone to listen to.

It is worth a listen and am now reading the book.
