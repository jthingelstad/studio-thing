Today we held our **5th Annual TeamSPS Kubb Tournament**! This event has now become a real tradition and many of our team look forward to playing Kubb together in early fall every year.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/6334e1ac32.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="Group of coworkers playing Kubb on a sunny urban green space, one player mid-throw releasing a wooden baton toward wooden blocks on the grass">

We had 16 teams of 6 people, with a full registration of 96 players. Plus plenty of folks came down just to watch, hang out with team members, and cheer on the players.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/d4ab638b28.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="Man in jeans and gray t-shirt throws a wooden baton toward wooden kubbs standing upright on a green urban park lawn, with other players watching in the background.">

The great thing about Kubb for a team event is that anyone can play, the game is quick to pick up, and I guarantee you that you are going to meet and talk to the other members of your team. The game is casual, and can be enjoyed by players of any age.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/62509794c2.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="Four men relaxing in blue canvas deck chairs on a grassy park lawn, smiling and holding drinks on a sunny day">

Some of our team really get into it. As the Tournament Director I’m responsible for calling matches, making rulings on anything questionable on the field. It is awesome to see the folks that really git into the spirit of Kubb.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/15f6b4632b.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="Woman in striped shirt throws a wooden baton at wooden block targets on a grass field while others wait their turn holding batons">

This year we decided to up our game a bit and got 8 custom Kubb sets with SPS colors on the kings, and the logo etched into it. These amazing sets came from [JP's Backyard Games](https://www.jpsbackyardgames.com) and he threw in some shirts that I got to hand out to some players as well. 

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/7423cb4090.jpg" width="450" height="600" alt="Smiling man in a white Kubb tournament shirt and cap holds a wooden baton and a blue decorative Kubb king trophy on a grass field with office buildings behind him.">

**It was a great afternoon to Throw Some Wood!**

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/0306c921d3.jpg" width="600" height="600" alt="Group of people playing Kubb on a grass lawn outside a modern office building, with wooden blocks arranged on the field and folding chairs in the background.">

Just like previous years, we had a [POAP for the event](https://poap.gallery/event/150068)!

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/47f46799-186f-45e8-90bf-4bd3526d62ba.png" width="600" height="623" alt="Round logo for the 5th Annual SPS Kubb Tourney 2023 featuring illustrated wooden kubb pieces styled as Viking warriors with horned helmets, shields, axes, and spears">
