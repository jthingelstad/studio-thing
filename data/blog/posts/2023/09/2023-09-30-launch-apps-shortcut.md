I’m a big fan of Shortcuts and find it useful for many small automation tasks, creating one off scripts really fast, and even [building complex solutions](https://www.thingelstad.com/2023/09/24/how-i-build.html) like the [Weekly Thing](https://weekly.thingelstad.com). 

"Launch Apps" is a shortcut that I think every Mac user can benefit from. I have a set of applications that I always use. Mail, Calendar, Drafts, etc. Some apps I add contextually based on which computer I’m on, or what network. Launch Apps gives me one shortcut I can put in my Menubar and easily get everything that I want running quickly. 

You can grab my [Launch Apps Shortcut](https://files.thingelstad.com/shortcuts/Launch%20Apps.shortcut) as a starting point to make your own. It is a very simple shortcut, and a great way to get started with automation.
