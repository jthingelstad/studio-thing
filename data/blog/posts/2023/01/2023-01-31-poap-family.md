The new **POAP Family** feature is a cool way to see what POAPs people share between events. I looked at holders of my [50th Birthday POAP](https://poap.family/event/20896) and I was surprised to see that there were 7 [Cryptog's HODLER](https://poap.gallery/event/31630)'s who had my birthday POAP. The biggest were 13 of the 34 that also had my [51st Birthday POAP](https://poap.gallery/event/95194).

This kind of graph analysis of [POAP](https://poap.xyz)s is an interesting extension and a way to build connection.
