**tourist**

_noun_
1. One who travels for pleasure.

2. One who makes a tour; one who makes a journey for pleasure, stopping at a number of places for the purpose of seeing the sights, scenery, etc.

3. One who makes a tour, or performs a journey, especially for pleasure.
