This Bitcoin ATM is one of my favorite things I’ve seen at [Bitcoin 2023](https://b.tc/conference/). You put coins in the device, I put 3 pennies. You tap the screen and it gives you a Lightning code to scan and I got 99 sats in my wallet. 🤩

**Update (May 23):**

I did some digging and found that "Tortcher" is the one who made this awesome device. I found him online and was excited to see that he is selling the **[World's Smallest Bitcoin Hobby ATM](https://geyser.fund/project/bitcoinlightningatm)**. I've ordered the Multi-ATM and can't wait to show people how fun, easy, and fast Bitcoin and Lightning can be with it! 🤩⚡️ Oh yeah, and Tortcher lives in the Twin Cities area — small world! The [code is on GitHub](https://github.com/travishoglund/bitcoin-lightning-atm) as well.
