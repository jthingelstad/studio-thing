What do you do when you visit the Museum of Modern Art? Well for many it seems that you navigate your way to Van Gogh's [Starry Night](https://www.moma.org/learn/moma_learning/vincent-van-gogh-the-starry-night-1889/) and take a picture of it in a fairly unhelpful mass of people. 

I wonder, how many gigabytes of photos are taken of Starry Night each day?
