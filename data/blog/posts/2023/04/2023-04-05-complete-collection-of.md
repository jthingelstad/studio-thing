One of my favorite NFT projects is the [612 Series](https://www.staynftympls.xyz/612-series) by Erik Halaas of StayNftyMpls. I like that it is a local project, made by a local artist, and the landmarks are nearly all places that I have been to and have distinct memories of.

I decided that I wanted to get a [complete collection of all 32 landmarks](https://rainbow.me/thingelstad.eth?family=612-series).

The rarest one of these is Al's Breakfast, which there are only 2 of, so it is unlikely there will be any other complete collections of this series.
