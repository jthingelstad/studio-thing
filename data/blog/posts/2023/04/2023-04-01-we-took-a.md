We took a quick trip to Grand Rapids to see [Annie Jr.](https://www.mtishows.com/annie-jr) at the [Reif Center](https://www.reifcenter.org). Our nephew was playing one of the main roles. The performance was great. They had **70 kids** as orphans which made for awesome orphanage scenes. A fabulous time for all!
