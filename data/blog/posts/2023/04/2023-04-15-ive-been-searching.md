I've been searching for a super simple way to sign strings of text with my Ethereum wallet and share that proof with anyone online and generally the options have been terrible. I had resigned myself to having to use PGP signatures. And then I found [Signatorio](https://signator.io) which does **exactly** what I wanted! 👏
