It was cool to see the [Minnebar 17 POAPs](https://www.thingelstad.com/2023/03/04/minnebar-poaps.html) highlighted in the [April 25 issue of This Week in POAP](https://newsletter.poap.xyz/apr-25-2023/)!

> Minnebar, America's "largest and longest-running technology unconference" was held at Best Buy's corporate headquarters in Minneapolis and dropped POAPs for [attendees](https://poap.gallery/event/117328), [presenters](https://poap.gallery/event/117329), and [organizers](https://poap.gallery/event/117330), as well as a [POAP](https://poap.family/event/117279) during a session on creating digital community with POAP ([LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/minne-_minnebar-POAP-activity-7052686026112172032-uSCk) post highlighting the POAPs).
