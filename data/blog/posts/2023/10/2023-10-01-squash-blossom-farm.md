We had a fun evening driving out to [Squash Blossom Farm](https://www.squashblossomfarm.org/) just outside of Oronoco, Minnesota for a [Farm-to-Table dinner](https://www.squashblossomfarm.org/event-details/table-dinner-theater-with-sodhouse-theater-1) presented by the [Sod House Theater](https://www.sodhousetheater.org) group with funny skits and performances along with the meal. This was a truly unique experience!

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/94c3637892.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="">

The farm was pretty and very relaxed. The Sod House Theater had an accordian player to accompany the skits.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/dd846e872b.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="">

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/9153f7774b.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="">

We tried two different meads and had one other drink.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/202698d96e.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="">

The performances were fun and goofy and made us all chuckle. You were right in the action.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/aec7409394.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="">

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/67096368e9.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="">

The meals were good and not too foody. Simple dishes served very well.
