We had a fun evening driving out to [Squash Blossom Farm](https://www.squashblossomfarm.org/) just outside of Oronoco, Minnesota for a [Farm-to-Table dinner](https://www.squashblossomfarm.org/event-details/table-dinner-theater-with-sodhouse-theater-1) presented by the [Sod House Theater](https://www.sodhousetheater.org) group with funny skits and performances along with the meal. This was a truly unique experience!

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/94c3637892.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="Red barn with a Meadery sign and awning, decorated with potted flowers, mums, and pumpkins, with two people seated outside on a patio">

The farm was pretty and very relaxed. The Sod House Theater had an accordian player to accompany the skits.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/dd846e872b.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="Accordion and music stand set up on a brick patio beside a red barn surrounded by colorful fall flowers, with outdoor diners seated at tables in the background.">

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/9153f7774b.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="Two small tulip glasses of mead on a copper bar top, one golden and one deep red, with several labeled Squash Blossom Farm mead bottles behind them">

We tried two different meads and had one other drink.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/202698d96e.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="Six women in colorful aprons and skirts perform a skit outside a red barn for seated dinner guests">

The performances were fun and goofy and made us all chuckle. You were right in the action.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/aec7409394.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="Performers in colorful costumes dance and sing between two rows of seated dinner guests at an outdoor farm event at dusk">

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/67096368e9.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="Spinach salad with roasted squash, crumbled goat cheese, candied nuts, and a red edible flower on a white plate">

The meals were good and not too foody. Simple dishes served very well.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/18a0935b3b.jpg" width="600" height="450" alt="White plate with two crepes topped with cream sauce and herbs beside a stacked tomato and zucchini gratin, on a floral tablecloth with mead glasses nearby">
