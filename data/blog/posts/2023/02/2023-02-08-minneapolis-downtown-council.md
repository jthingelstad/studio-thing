I was able to attend the Minneapolis Downtown Council [Annual Meeting](https://www.mplsdowntown.com/annualmeeting/) today. I believe this was the first back in the [Armory](https://armorymn.com) since [the 2020 meeting](https://www.thingelstad.com/2020/02/12/impressive-progress-in.html). 

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/c89006824c.jpg)

The outgoing chair **[Mike Ryan](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mike-ryan-93b45429/)** gave a good overview of the improtance of downtown and the council, and also welcomed the new chair **[Karin Lucas](https://www.linkedin.com/in/karin-h-lucas/)** from SPS Commerce!

![](https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/6be3c1fd05.jpg)

SPS is the 10th largest employer downtown with 1,346 people! 

I enjoyed the session that **[Amelia Santaniello](https://www.linkedin.com/in/amelia-santaniello-6818766a/)** led with **[Steve Cramer](https://www.linkedin.com/in/steve-cramer-4368b387/)**, **[Greg Cunningham](https://www.linkedin.com/in/greggcunningham/)**, and **Mayor [Jacob Frey](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jacob-frey-049b4934/)**. Frey got up at one point and gave a stump speech for Minneapolis which was great.
