I ran across this [Adopt a Character](https://home.unicode.org/adopt-a-character/about-adopt-a-character/) campaign from the Unicode Consortium and felt like a donation made sense. But what emoji to pick? After thinking through a few I decided **Thinking Face**. 🤔


<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2023/7f8b8600c1.png" width="216" height="216" alt="Round bronze badge reading Official Bronze Sponsor, Unicode Consortium, Adopt a Character, with a thinking face emoji in the center." style=" width: 216px !important;" />

This is fun, but I think it would be so much cooler if this program worked by selling NFTs. It has all the right characteristics with Gold and Silver levels being limited, and Bronze being "open editions." It would be really fun to have this in my wallet as proof of participation.
