We went to the [Orpheum](https://hennepintheatretrust.org/theatres/orpheum-theatre/) to see [To Kill a Mockingbird](https://tokillamockingbirdbroadway.com) with new screenplay by [Aaron Sorkin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aaron_Sorkin) and directed by [Bartlett Sher](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bartlett_Sher). It was an incredible and powerful performance. [Richard Thomas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Richard_Thomas_(actor)) was so engaging as Atticus Finch. Some may remember him as ["John-Boy"](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_The_Waltons_characters#John_.22John-Boy.22_Walton_Jr.) from The Waltons.
