I want to take a moment to applaud [Flickr](http://www.flickr.com/). It
really frustrates me when websites do not give you full control of your
data. I decided that I wanted to start over on Flickr and delete every
picture I have. Some services make this so painful. In Flickr though, it
was just a couple of clearly labeled and easily accessible clicks away.
<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/3f3d66ed8b.png" alt="Progress dialog showing deletion of 1,800 of 2,228 Flickr items with a pink progress bar and a Cancel button.">

That took a while, but when it was done I had a blank slate to start with again.

<img src="https://www.thingelstad.com/uploads/2020/81689e8e9d.png" alt="Flickr photostream page showing an empty account with no uploaded photos and a prompt to get started or view friends uploads.">

Thank you Flickr for making it very clear that I am in charge of [my account](http://www.flickr.com/photos/jthingelstad/) and what is there.
