---

Last Saturday I announced that I was doing a first-ever fundraising campaign in **[Weekly Thing #140](https://buttondown.email/jthingelstad/archive/weekly-thing-140-conversations-leadership-remote/), matching up to $10,000 in contributions to Second Harvest!** As Covid-19 has done so much damage to our economy, it is very clear that there are going to be a lot of people that will need help. If you are in a position to offer that help, I want to match it with you!

**We have already had a big impact!** 🙌

So far **we've raised $2,905 for Second Harvest!** With the match, that is already **$5,810**! It gets better! Right now Blue Cross Blue Shield of Minnesota is also matching so we have **already brought in $11,620!**

That means we have already provided **34,860 meals for people in need!**

But, we can do much more. We can provide **another 85,140 meals for people in need if we hit the $10,000 goal** and maximize the match!

**[Make a donation to Second Harvest now!](https://www.2harvest.org)**

**Let's get another $7,095! Okay?** I know these are difficult times and there is a lot of uncertainty, but imagine how much more uncertainty there is if you aren't sure where your meals are coming from.

**[Make a donation now](https://www.2harvest.org)**, and then **forward your donation receipt to me via email at jamie@thingelstad.com**. I will add up all the donations and **match them up to $10,000**! Let's double, actually quadruple, the good we can all make together and help get food to people that need it!
